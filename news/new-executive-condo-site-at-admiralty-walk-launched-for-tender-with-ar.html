New executive condo site at Admiralty Walk launched for tender with around 450 homes政府招标Admiralty Walk EC地段，预计可建约450个单位

~450 ec homes at admiralty walkAdmiralty Walk EC单位数量

The government has put up a land parcel at Admiralty Walk in Sembawang for tender, inviting property developers to submit bids. The site is expected to yield around 450 executive condo units once built.

Executive condos occupy a middle ground in Singapore's housing ladder. They are built by private developers to a standard closer to private condos, but sold subject to HDB eligibility rules and income ceilings. That combination makes them a popular route for HDB upgraders who want a step up in quality without the full price tag of a private development.

A successful tender award would typically be followed by a launch in roughly one to two years. If you are an HDB upgrader keeping an eye on the north of Singapore, this is a project worth tracking as it progresses through the development pipeline.

政府已将Sembawang Admiralty Walk一块地段推出招标，邀请房地产发展商提交竞标。该地段预计落成后可提供约450个executive condo单位。

Executive condo在新加坡的置业阶梯中占据中间地位。它们由私人发展商建造，品质标准更接近私宅condo，但销售时须遵守HDB的资格规定和收入上限。这种结合，使其成为HDB升级者寻求品质提升、又不想付出全额私宅价格时的热门途径。

地段成功批出后，通常需要约一至两年才会正式发售。如果你是一位密切关注新加坡北部市场的HDB升级者，这个项目值得随着开发进程持续跟进。
