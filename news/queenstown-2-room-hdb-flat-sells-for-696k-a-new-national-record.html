Queenstown 2-room HDB flat sells for $696K, a new national record女皇镇2房式组屋以$696K成交，创全国最高纪录

$696K national 2-room HDB resale record全国2房式HDB转售纪录

A 2-room HDB flat in Queenstown has sold for $696,000, the highest price ever recorded for that flat type in Singapore. The transaction sets a new national benchmark and underscores how far prices have moved even at the smallest end of the public housing market.

Two-room flats are often thought of as entry-level or investment units, so a sale approaching $700,000 for one in Queenstown reflects how scarce well-located HDB stock has become. Queenstown's proximity to the city centre, its established amenities, and the limited availability of units in mature estates all push prices toward levels that would have seemed implausible not long ago.

For owners of 2-room flats in mature central estates, this sale shows the ceiling on your asset is higher than many assumed. For buyers, it drives home the premium attached to location in Singapore's HDB market regardless of flat size. If you want a central address and are working within a tight budget, the trade-off between size and where you live has never been more stark.
