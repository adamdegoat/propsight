VIP Condo Sales Under Scrutiny After Agents Get First Pick of UnitsCondo发展商让经纪人优先选房的做法遭到质疑

Regulators and buyers are starting to ask hard questions about how new condo developers handle launch day. The practice in focus is giving real estate agents access to unit bookings before the general public gets anywhere near the sales gallery.

On a typical new launch weekend, ordinary buyers queue up expecting a fair shot at the units they want. What many do not realise is that a portion of the better-facing or better-sized units may already have been informally set aside for agents and their clients. By the time you walk in, the real choice has already been narrowed.

If you are planning to buy at a new launch, do not assume the queue is first-come, first-served. Before launch day, ask the developer or project marketing team directly how units are allocated and whether agents have been given early priority. If you cannot get a straight answer, treat that silence as useful information in itself.

监管机构和买家开始就新condo发展商如何处理开盘当天的事宜提出尖锐问题。目前受到关注的做法是：在普通公众进入售楼处之前，让房产经纪人提前预订单位。

在典型的新盘开售周末，普通买家排队等候，以为自己有公平机会选到心仪单位。很多人不知道的是，朝向好或面积大的单位，可能早已非正式地预留给经纪人及其客户。等你走进去的时候，真正的选择空间已经大大缩小了。

如果你计划在新盘开售时出手，不要以为排队就是先到先得。在开盘前，直接向发展商或项目营销团队问清楚单位是如何分配的、经纪人是否享有优先权。如果你得不到正面回答，这种沉默本身就是有用的信息。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
