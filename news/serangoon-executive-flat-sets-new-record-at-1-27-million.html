Serangoon Executive Flat Sets New Record at $1.27 Million实龙岗执行式单位以$1.27 million售出，创历史新纪录

$1.27M serangoon exec flat record实龙岗执行式单位纪录

An executive flat in Serangoon has sold for $1.27 million, setting a new record for that flat type in the estate. Executive flats are the largest category in the HDB system, with more rooms and more floor area than a standard five-room unit, and buyers have been willing to pay up for the extra space.

The margin by which this transaction set the record is slim, but the direction is consistent. Serangoon is a well-established town with solid amenities and good transport links, and demand for larger flats there remains firm.

This fits a pattern playing out across Singapore where oversized HDB flats, regardless of location, are fetching prices that would have seemed out of reach not long ago. If you own an executive flat in a decent town, the market is telling you it is worth more than it has ever been.

实龙岗一个执行式单位以$1.27 million售出，为该区同类型单位创下新纪录。执行式单位是HDB体系中面积最大的类别，房间数和楼面面积都比标准五房式单位更多，而买家也愿意为额外的空间多付一些。

这次成交打破纪录的差距虽然微小，但走势是一致向上的。实龙岗是个发展成熟的市镇，配套齐全，交通方便，大型单位的需求依然稳健。

这与全岛的趋势一脉相承，不管在哪个地区，超大型HDB组屋都在以不久前看来难以想象的价格成交。如果你在条件不错的市镇拥有执行式单位，市场正在告诉你，它的价值已经是有史以来最高的了。
