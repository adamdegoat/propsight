Admiralty Walk executive condo site launched for tender with about 450 homes planned海军部道EC地段推出招标，预计提供约450个单位

~450 planned ec units at admiralty walk海军部道预计EC单位数量

A land parcel at Admiralty Walk in Sembawang has been put up for tender, meaning developers can now submit bids to win the right to build on it. The site is sized to yield around 450 executive condo units once developed.

A government tender launch is the earliest stage in the new launch pipeline. The winning developer still needs to design the project, secure approvals and construct the building, so any sale to buyers is at minimum a couple of years away. The tender result will also give a strong signal of what the eventual unit prices might look like, since developers factor their land cost into what they charge buyers.

If you are interested in a new EC in Sembawang, put this project on your radar but do not expect an imminent launch. Watch who wins the tender and what land price they commit to. That number will tell you a lot about where entry prices will land when the project eventually opens for sale.
