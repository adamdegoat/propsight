Over one million HDB households to receive utility and town council fee rebates in July逾百万HDB住户将于7月收到水电费及市镇会费回扣

1M+ hdb households getting rebates获得回扣的HDB住户数量

More than one million HDB households will receive two types of rebates this month. The first is the U-Save rebate, which offsets electricity and gas bills. The second covers Service and Conservancy Charges, the monthly fees paid to town councils for maintenance of common areas and estate facilities.

These rebates are part of the government's regular support package for HDB residents and are typically disbursed quarterly. Not every household qualifies for the same amount, as eligibility and quantum depend on factors such as flat type and household income level.

If your household qualifies, the savings will show up directly in your bills this month without any action needed on your part. It is still worth checking what you are entitled to through HDB's website or your town council, so you know what to expect and can flag it if the credit does not appear.

本月，逾百万HDB住户将收到两类回扣。第一类是U-Save回扣，用于抵消电费和煤气费。第二类则涵盖Service and Conservancy Charge，即住户每月缴交给市镇会、用于维护公共区域和组屋区设施的费用。

这些回扣是政府定期向HDB居民提供的支援配套的一部分，通常按季度发放。并非所有住户获得的金额都相同，资格及回扣数额视组屋类型和家庭收入水平等因素而定。

如果你的住户符合资格，节省的款项将在本月直接反映在账单上，无需你采取任何行动。不过，还是值得通过HDB官网或你的市镇会查清楚自己应得的金额，这样你就知道预期能收到多少，万一回扣没有出现，也能及时跟进。
