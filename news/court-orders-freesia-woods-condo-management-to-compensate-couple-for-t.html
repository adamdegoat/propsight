Court Orders Freesia Woods Condo Management to Compensate Couple for Termite Damage法院裁定Freesia Woods Condo管理机构须赔偿业主白蚁损失

A couple living in Freesia Woods condominium have won a court case against their management corporation after a termite infestation damaged their unit. The court found that management had failed to take adequate action after being informed of the problem, and ordered compensation to be paid to the affected owners.

Termite infestations in older condominiums are not uncommon, but disputes about who bears responsibility for fixing them often are. This case draws a clear line: if management is notified and does nothing, and damage results to an individual unit because of that inaction, they can be held legally liable.

The lesson for any condo owner dealing with a pest or maintenance problem is to document everything from the start. Report the issue in writing, follow up in writing, and keep records of every response or non-response from management. If they ignore you and you suffer damage as a result, you have legal recourse, and this case shows the courts will back you.

住在Freesia Woods condo的一对夫妻，在白蚁入侵损坏单位后，成功控告了他们的管理机构。法院认定管理层在接获投诉后没有采取足够的行动，并下令向受影响的业主支付赔偿。

年份较旧的condo出现白蚁问题其实并不少见，但关于谁该负责处理的纠纷却经常让人头疼。这个案例划出了一条清晰的界线：管理层若在接获通知后无所作为，导致个别单位因此受损，他们就要承担法律责任。

对于任何正在应对害虫或维修问题的condo业主来说，这个案例给出了一个重要提醒：从一开始就要把所有事情记录下来。以书面形式报告问题，以书面形式跟进，并保留管理层每一次回应或不回应的记录。如果他们置之不理，而你因此蒙受损失，你是有法律途径可以追究的，这个案例证明，法院会站在你这边。
