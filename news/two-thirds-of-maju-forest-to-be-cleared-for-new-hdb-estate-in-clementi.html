Two-Thirds of Maju Forest to Be Cleared for New HDB Estate in Clementi金文泰Maju森林三分之二将被清除，为新HDB组屋区让路

The government has confirmed that a new HDB estate is coming to Clementi, and most of Maju Forest will be cleared to make way for it. About two-thirds of the forested area will be developed, with the remaining third kept as green space.

Maju Forest sits in the western part of Singapore and has been a quiet patch of greenery in an already built-up neighbourhood. Clementi is well-served by the MRT and major expressways, so new flats here will likely attract strong demand from buyers who want a mature town with good connectivity.

For future buyers, this is another supply injection in a well-located part of Singapore, which should help moderate prices compared to what you would pay for resale in the same area today. For residents who live near the forest now, the trade-off is real: you lose most of the green buffer, and you gain a denser neighbourhood. The retained portion of forest is welcome, but it is a fraction of what is there now.

If you are watching the west for a BTO opportunity, keep Clementi on your radar when launches are announced. If you already own nearby, be realistic that more supply and less greenery can both weigh on premiums that mature-estate listings sometimes command.

政府已证实，金文泰将新建一个HDB组屋区，而Maju森林大部分将被清除以腾出用地。约三分之二的林地将被开发，其余三分之一则保留为绿地。

Maju森林位于新加坡西部，一直是这个已相当成熟的住宅区里一片难得的清静绿地。金文泰MRT和主要快速公路配套完善，因此这里的新组屋预计会吸引不少想在成熟市镇置业、又注重交通便利的买家踊跃申请。

对未来买家来说，这是新加坡一个地点不错的地区又一次新增供应，应该有助于压低价格，比起现在在同区买转售组屋划算不少。但对目前住在森林附近的居民来说，这个取舍是实实在在的：大部分绿化缓冲区将消失，换来的是更密集的居住环境。保留下来的那部分森林固然值得庆幸，但和现有面积相比，只是一小部分。

如果你正在留意西部的BTO机会，等推出消息出来后，记得把金文泰列入考虑范围。如果你已经在附近置业，则要务实看待：供应增加加上绿地减少，可能会对成熟市镇单位通常能享有的溢价造成一定压力。
