Singapore Lowers Entry Age for Senior-Friendly HDB Flats and Cuts Monthly Fees新加坡调低乐龄友善HDB组屋申请年龄并削减月费

75% fee cut for care apartments护理公寓费用削减幅度

The government has cut the minimum age to apply for Community Care Apartments from 65 to 55, and at the same time reduced the monthly fees for these units by up to 75%. Community Care Apartments are HDB flats with on-site care services built for seniors who want to age in a supported setting rather than a nursing home.

The fee reduction makes these units considerably more affordable, and pulling the entry age down to 55 means people can plan and apply earlier rather than scrambling for options as they approach retirement. That is a meaningful shift for anyone thinking about long-term housing well before the typical retirement age.

For the broader resale market, the knock-on effect is that seniors who move into these smaller care-enabled flats will be releasing their larger HDB units into the resale pool. More supply filtering through over time is generally good news for buyers, even if the impact builds gradually rather than overnight.

政府将申请Community Care Apartments的最低年龄从65岁降至55岁，同时将这类单位的月费削减最多75%。Community Care Apartments是附设驻场护理服务的HDB组屋，专为希望在有支援的环境中安享晚年、而非住进疗养院的乐龄人士而设。

费用削减让这类单位的经济负担大幅降低，而将申请年龄降至55岁，意味着大家可以更早开始规划和申请，不必等到快退休才手忙脚乱地找选项。对于提前考虑长远居住安排的人来说，这是一个颇具意义的改变。

对整体转售市场而言，连带效应是：搬入这些较小型护理组屋的乐龄人士，会将他们较大的HDB单位释放到转售市场。供应量随着时间慢慢增加，对买家来说通常是好消息，虽然影响不会在一夜之间显现，而是逐步积累的。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Older flat owners who move into these smaller care-enabled flats earlier can free up their larger HDB units for the resale market, which should gradually add more supply for buyers.较早搬入这些小型护理组屋的年长屋主，可以将较大的HDB单位释放到转售市场，有望随着时间推移逐步增加买家可选择的供应量。
