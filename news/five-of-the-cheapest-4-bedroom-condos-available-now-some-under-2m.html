Five of the Cheapest 4-Bedroom Condos Available Now, Some Under $2M目前最实惠的五个4房式condo，部分售价低于$2M

$2M entry price for 4-bedroom condos4房式condo的入手价

If you need four bedrooms in a condo and assumed you would have to spend $3 million or more, a new roundup of available listings may change your thinking. A survey of the current market has identified five 4-bedroom condo units for sale in Singapore right now, and some of them are priced below $2 million.

Four-bedroom condos at that price point do exist, but they tend to come with trade-offs: older developments, locations further from the MRT, or unit layouts that have been on the market longer than others. The $2 million mark is not typical for this size in the Core Central Region, so most of these options will be in the Outside Central Region or Rest of Central Region, where land costs are lower and demand is less concentrated.

For families who genuinely need the space and do not want to commit to a landed property, this is a useful reminder to search broadly and not anchor to the headline prices of newer launches. A slightly older resale condo with four proper bedrooms at under $2 million can offer far more liveable space per dollar than a new 3-bedroom at a similar price. The key is knowing which pockets of the market still offer this, and acting before those listings get absorbed.
