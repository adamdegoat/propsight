Smaller condos more likely to have construction defects, BCA finds小型condo楼盘更容易出现建筑缺陷，BCA调查发现

BCA looked at construction quality across condo developments and found a clear pattern: smaller, boutique projects consistently come out worse than larger ones.

The reason is mainly resources and leverage. A developer building 50 units has far less power to push back on a shoddy contractor than one building 500. Smaller sites also tend to run leaner site supervision, which means problems get caught later or not at all.

If you are buying a unit in a small condo, treat the defect inspection as non-negotiable. Hire an independent inspector before you accept the keys, and put every issue in writing so the developer is on the hook to fix it.

BCA对各condo楼盘的建筑质量进行了调查，发现了一个明显规律：规模较小的精品项目，表现一贯不如大型项目。

主要原因在于资源和谈判筹码。开发50个单位的发展商，在应对劣质承包商时，远不如开发500个单位的发展商有底气。小型工地的现场监管往往也相对精简，这意味着问题要么发现得太晚，要么根本就被忽略了。

如果你购买的是小型condo单位，缺陷检查绝对不能省略。收钥匙前请聘请独立验房师，并将每一项问题都以书面形式记录，这样发展商才有义务负责修缮。
