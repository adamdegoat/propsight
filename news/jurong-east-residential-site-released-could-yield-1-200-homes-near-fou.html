Jurong East residential site released, could yield 1,200 homes near four-line MRT hub裕廊东住宅地段推出，可建约1,200个单位，毗邻四线MRT转换站

1,200 new homes near mrt interchangeMRT转换站附近新单位

Singapore's land authority has released a residential site in Jurong East that planners say could yield around 1,200 homes. The site sits next to a future MRT interchange where four separate rail lines are eventually planned to converge, which makes it one of the more transit-connected residential locations coming to market in the west.

Four-line MRT access is genuinely rare. Only a small number of stations in Singapore sit where that many lines cross, and that kind of connectivity tends to show up in long-term demand and resale values. For future residents, it means direct access to a wide spread of the island without layering on multiple transfers for everyday journeys.

For buyers with an eye on the west, this site adds to the existing case for Jurong as a long-term residential bet. More supply coming to the area will create some short-term competition among developers, which is good for buyers at launch. The four-line interchange is the factor worth tracking over time. Watch what developers bid for the site and what price levels the eventual launch targets relative to other projects in the corridor.

新加坡土地局推出裕廊东一幅住宅地段，规划当局表示该地段可建约1,200个单位。地段紧邻未来一个MRT转换站，届时将有四条铁路线在此交汇，是西部即将推出市场的住宅地点中，交通连接性最强的之一。

四线MRT交汇实属难得。新加坡只有极少数车站拥有这样的多线交汇，这种交通便利性往往会反映在长期需求与转售价值上。对未来住户来说，日常出行可以直达岛内各处，不必来回转线。

对于看好西部的买家来说，这幅地段进一步巩固了裕廊作为长线置业之选的理由。更多供应进入市场，短期内会加剧发展商之间的竞争，这对新盘买家来说是好事。四线转换站才是值得长期关注的关键因素。不妨留意发展商的投标价，以及日后新盘的定价相比同一走廊其他项目处于什么水平。
