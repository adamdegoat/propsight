Lentor Gardens Residences preview pulls in 5,000 visitors over the weekendLentor Gardens Residences preview pulls in 5,000 visitors over the weekend

5,000 visitors at preview weekendvisitors at preview weekend

The Lentor Gardens Residences preview over the July 4 to 5 weekend drew roughly 5,000 visitors to the showflat, a turnout that signals very strong interest in this upcoming launch. Preview numbers are not a perfect predictor, but when thousands of people give up a weekend to look at a project, developers notice, and so should buyers.

The Lentor area has been building momentum with each successive launch as more amenities and transport options take shape. Clearly buyers are still willing to commit to the neighbourhood. That level of interest across one weekend points to a competitive ballot when the project officially opens for sale, with units likely moving quickly.

Anyone seriously considering a unit here needs to be prepared before launch day, not after. That means talking to a bank, getting your loan in principle approved, and deciding on your budget and preferred unit type in advance. When demand is running this hot, going in unprepared is how buyers end up either missing out or paying more than they intended under pressure.
