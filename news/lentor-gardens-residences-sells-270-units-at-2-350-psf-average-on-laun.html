Lentor Gardens Residences Sells 270 Units at $2,350 psf Average on Launch DayLentor Gardens Residences开盘当天售出270个单位，均价$2,350 psf

$2,350 psf average launch price开盘均价

Lentor Gardens Residences sold around 270 units on its opening weekend, hitting a take-up rate of roughly 54% at an average price of $2,350 per square foot. The smaller one-bedroom and mid-size two-bedroom layouts were the first to go, which reflects strong demand from both investors and owner-occupiers looking for a more affordable entry point into a new launch.

The $2,350 psf average sets a new benchmark for the Lentor area, which has only recently started attracting large-scale residential development. Earlier Lentor launches were already pushing past $2,000 psf, and this project has moved that bar up again.

If you are watching other upcoming projects in Lentor or the Ang Mo Kio fringe, expect developers to price at similar or higher levels given this result. Buyers holding out for value may find the secondary market more interesting once early buyers start selling, but that is realistically a few years away.

Lentor Gardens Residences在开盘周末卖出约270个单位，去化率约54%，均价$2,350 psf。小型一房和中等面积两房户型率先售罄，反映出投资者和自住买家都在争着以较亲民的价格踏入新盘市场。

$2,350 psf的均价为Lentor一带设立了新的价格基准。这片区域近年才开始吸引大规模住宅开发，早前的Lentor新盘已经突破$2,000 psf，而这个项目又将这条线再往上推了一步。
