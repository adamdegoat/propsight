Tampines Executive HDB Flat Sold for $1.24M, a New Town Record淡滨尼行政型HDB组屋以$1.24M成交，创该市镇转售最高纪录

$1.24M tampines hdb resale town record淡滨尼HDB转售市镇纪录

A 31-year-old executive HDB flat in Tampines has just sold for $1.24 million, setting a new record for the highest resale price ever paid for an HDB flat in that town. Executive flats are the largest HDB flat type, typically running above 140 square metres, and this unit has been around long enough to have a remaining lease well under 70 years.

The number is striking because older flats with shorter leases usually trade at a discount. Banks lend less on them, CPF usage is restricted, and buyers have to account for the lease running out within their lifetime. For a flat this age to still command seven figures tells you how much demand there is for large, well-located HDB units in a mature estate like Tampines.

If you own an executive flat in Tampines or a similar mature town, this sale is a useful reference point. Prices for big units in these areas have been climbing steadily, and your flat may be worth more than you last checked. On the other side, buyers should go in with clear eyes: at $1.24 million, you are paying a premium for size and location, and you need to run the lease decay numbers carefully before committing.

淡滨尼一间屋龄31年的行政型HDB组屋刚以$1.24 million成交，创下该市镇HDB组屋有史以来最高的转售价纪录。行政型组屋是HDB最大的户型，面积通常超过140平方公尺，而这个单位的剩余租约已远低于70年。

这个价格之所以令人瞩目，是因为屋龄较老、剩余租约较短的组屋通常会以折扣价成交。银行对这类单位的贷款额度较低，CPF的使用也受到限制，买家还得考虑租约可能在有生之年到期的风险。一个这么老的组屋还能卖到七位数，足以说明市场对淡滨尼这类成熟市镇中大型、地段好的HDB单位需求有多旺盛。

如果你在淡滨尼或类似的成熟市镇拥有行政型组屋，这宗交易是个很好的参考点。这类地区的大型单位价格一直在稳步攀升，你的组屋价值可能比你上次了解的还要高。另一方面，买家也需要保持清醒：以$1.24 million买入，你是在为面积和地段付出溢价，下决定之前一定要仔细算清楚租约递减对价值的实际影响。
