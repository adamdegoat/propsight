Dawson's First 5-Room Resale Flat Sells for $1.39M道逊首宗五房式二手组屋以$1.39M成交

$1.39M first 5-room resale at Dawson道逊首宗五房式二手成交

A five-room flat in Dawson, the public housing estate in Queenstown, has changed hands for $1.39 million. This is the first recorded resale of a five-room flat in this estate, making it a landmark transaction for the area.

Dawson sits in one of Singapore's most central mature estates, with Queenstown MRT nearby and the city well within reach. Buyers have long been willing to pay a premium for this location, but $1.39 million for an HDB flat confirms just how far prices have moved in recent years.

Anyone looking at Dawson needs to accept upfront that they are paying near-private-property money for a public housing unit. You get the HDB structure and its rules, but without the condo facilities or freehold tenure. If the location and flat size genuinely suit your needs and the numbers work for your household, Dawson remains a strong address. If they do not, the price tag here will be hard to justify.

女皇镇道逊住宅区一间五房式组屋以$1.39M易手。这是该区首宗有记录的五房式二手成交，是整个区域的一个标志性交易。

道逊坐落于新加坡最中心的成熟市镇之一，毗邻女皇镇MRT站，前往市区十分便捷。买家向来愿意为这个地段支付溢价，而$1.39M的HDB成交价，更清晰地说明了近年来楼价究竟走了多远。

任何有意考虑道逊的买家，都必须从一开始就接受这个现实：你花的是接近私人房产的钱，买到的却是公共住屋单位。你拥有的是HDB框架及其附带的种种规定，却没有condo的设施或永久地契。如果这个地段和单位面积真的符合你的需求，数字上对家庭也行得通，道逊依然是一个出色的地址。若两者都不符合，这里的价格就很难让自己说服自己了。
