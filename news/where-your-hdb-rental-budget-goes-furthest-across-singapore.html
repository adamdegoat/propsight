Where Your HDB Rental Budget Goes Furthest Across Singapore新加坡各区HDB租金性价比大比较，哪里最划算？

Renting an HDB flat in Singapore is not a single uniform market. How much space your budget gets you varies significantly depending on which part of the island you choose. Mature estates close to the city centre and well-served by MRT lines command noticeably higher rents than newer or more outlying towns.

The logic is straightforward. Established, convenient areas with dense amenities are in high demand, so landlords there can charge more. Towns further out offer more floor area for less money, but the trade-off is usually a longer commute or fewer options nearby.

If your job and daily routine do not require you to be near the centre, doing a proper neighbourhood comparison before signing a lease is worth the effort. The difference in what your budget gets you can be a whole extra bedroom or a noticeably larger living area. For renters managing a tight budget or families who need space, making that comparison carefully can meaningfully change what you decide to look for.

在新加坡租HDB组屋，并不是一个划一的市场。你的预算能租到多大的空间，很大程度上取决于你选择岛上哪个区域。靠近市区、有密集MRT线路服务的成熟市镇，租金明显高于较新或偏远的市镇。

道理很简单。配套完善、出行便利的成熟地区需求旺盛，房东因此可以开出更高的价格。距离较远的市镇能用更少的钱租到更大的面积，但代价通常是更长的通勤时间或周边选择更少。

如果你的工作和日常生活不一定需要靠近市中心，在签租约前认真做一个区域比较是值得的。同样的预算，在不同地区能租到的空间可能相差一整个房间，或者客厅面积大出不少。对于预算有限或需要更多空间的家庭来说，用心做这个比较，往往能让你的决定大不一样。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
