One of Only 93 Multi-Generation HDB Flats in Bishan Listed for $1.59M碧山全镇仅93个多代同堂HDB单位之一，以$1.59M挂牌求售

$1.59M multi-gen flat asking price多代同堂组屋叫价

A multi-generation HDB flat in Bishan has been listed for $1.59 million, and what makes this unit exceptional is just how rare the flat type is. Across the entire Bishan town, only 93 such units exist. These flats let two family households share one address while keeping separate living spaces and their own entrances.

They were built to solve a specific problem: ageing parents living close to their adult children without either party giving up privacy. Because so few exist and the design genuinely works for that purpose, owners rarely sell. When one does appear on the market, serious interest follows quickly.

For families considering multi-generation living under one HDB roof, the $1.59 million asking price reflects both the scarcity of the flat type and the strength of Bishan as a location. Do not expect much room to negotiate or time to deliberate. These listings move fast, and the next one in Bishan may not surface for years.

碧山一个多代同堂HDB组屋以$1.59 million挂牌，而这个单位真正特别之处，在于这种户型有多罕见。整个碧山镇，只有93个这样的单位。这类组屋让两个家庭共用同一个地址，同时各自保有独立的生活空间和出入口。

这类单位当年是为了解决一个具体问题而建造的：让年迈父母能够住在成年子女附近，双方又不必牺牲各自的私人空间。正因为数量极少，而且设计确实符合这个用途，业主几乎不会轻易出售。一旦有单位出现在市场上，有诚意的买家很快就会蜂拥而至。

对于有意多代同堂、共住在一个HDB屋顶下的家庭，$1.59 million的叫价既反映了这种户型的稀缺性，也体现了碧山作为地段的价值。不要期望有太多议价空间或慢慢考虑的时间。这类挂牌向来成交很快，碧山下一个同类单位可能要多年后才再次出现。
