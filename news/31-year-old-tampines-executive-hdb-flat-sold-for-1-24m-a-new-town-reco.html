31-year-old Tampines executive HDB flat sold for $1.24M, a new town record淡滨尼31年楼龄executive组屋以$1.24M成交，创该镇新纪录

$1.24M tampines executive flat record price淡滨尼executive组屋创纪录成交价

A 31-year-old executive flat in Tampines has changed hands for $1.24 million, the highest price ever recorded for an executive flat in that town.

At 31 years old, the flat has roughly 68 years of lease remaining. Banks will lend less on a flat with that kind of lease decay, and future buyers of the unit will face the same financing constraints. That is a real consideration that needs to be factored into what you pay today.

That said, sellers with executive or large flat types in Tampines are clearly in a strong position right now. Demand for big units in mature estates remains high even when the lease is not fresh. If you are buying, run the numbers on the remaining lease before you commit, not after.

话虽如此，在淡滨尼拥有executive或大型房型组屋的卖家，目前显然处于有利位置。成熟市镇对大型单位的需求依然旺盛，即使租约并不新鲜亦然。如果你是买家，请在决定出价前，而非之后，认真盘算一下剩余租约年限。
