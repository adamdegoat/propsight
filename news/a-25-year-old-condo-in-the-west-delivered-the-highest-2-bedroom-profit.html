A 25-Year-Old Condo in the West Delivered the Highest 2-Bedroom Profits, Averaging $529,000 a Unit西区一栋25年楼龄的condo创两房最高转售盈利，平均每单位赚$529,000

$529K average profit per 2-bedder sold每套两房单位平均盈利

A 25-year-old condominium in western Singapore has come out on top for 2-bedroom resale profits, with sellers averaging $529,000 in gains per unit. The development sits on a 999-year leasehold tenure, which for practical purposes is as close to freehold as most buyers will ever need.

That $529,000 average is not a one-off windfall. It reflects a pattern of strong exits from a mature, established project in a part of Singapore that has never carried the prestige of the prime districts. The numbers make a straightforward case: location matters, but so does what you actually hold, and for how long.

The lesson for buyers comparing freehold or near-freehold options against a standard 99-year lease is simple. A shorter lease is not always a dealbreaker, but as the clock ticks down, resale value tends to soften and your pool of willing buyers shrinks. A 999-year or freehold unit removes that headwind entirely.

If you are shopping for a 2-bedroom unit in the west right now, ask about the lease before anything else. The price difference between a near-freehold and a 99-year unit might feel modest at entry, but after two or three decades, the gap at the exit can be very wide indeed.

新加坡西区一栋25年楼龄的condo，在两房转售盈利榜上拔得头筹，卖家每单位平均入账$529,000。这个项目持有999年地契，实际上和永久地契没什么分别，对绝大多数买家来说已经足够了。

这$529,000的平均盈利并非偶然。它反映出，即使在并非黄金地段的区域，一个成熟的老牌项目依然能持续带来亮眼的退出成绩。数字说明了一个简单的道理：地段固然重要，但你手上持有什么、持有多久，同样关键。

对于正在比较永久地契或近永久地契与标准99年地契的买家来说，道理很简单。地契年限短并不代表一定不能买，但随着剩余年限减少，转售价值往往会下滑，愿意接手的买家也会越来越少。999年或永久地契单位则完全没有这个烦恼。

如果你现在正在西区物色两房单位，第一件事就是问清楚地契年限。近永久地契与99年地契之间的入场价差距，乍看或许不大，但等到二三十年后要卖出时，两者之间的差距可能相当悬殊。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Long-tenure properties in established areas can still deliver strong gains decades on, which is worth factoring in when you are weighing freehold or near-freehold against a shorter lease at the point of purchase.成熟地区的长契房产，几十年后依然能带来可观回报。在买房时权衡永久地契或近永久地契与较短地契之间的取舍，这一点值得好好考量。
