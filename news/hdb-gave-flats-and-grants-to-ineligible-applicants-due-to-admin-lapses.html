HDB gave flats and grants to ineligible applicants due to admin lapses, AGO finds审计长公署发现，HDB因行政疏失将组屋及津贴批给不符资格的申请者

The Auditor-General's Office has found that HDB made administrative errors that led to flats being allocated and housing grants being paid out to applicants who did not meet the eligibility requirements. HDB has acknowledged the lapses and indicated it will tighten its processes to prevent similar errors going forward.

Public housing allocations and grants are means-tested and tied to specific criteria for good reason. When the checks fail, resources meant for qualifying applicants end up elsewhere. While the AGO has not indicated these are widespread problems, even isolated errors in a system this important deserve scrutiny and correction.

For most flat buyers, this does not change anything about your own application. But if you are in the queue and have had dealings with HDB that felt inconsistent or hard to explain, it is worth knowing that official scrutiny is now squarely on the process. The system is expected to tighten, which should ultimately make it fairer for everyone who follows the rules.

审计长公署发现，HDB在行政上出现失误，导致组屋被分配给、以及住房津贴被发放给不符合资格的申请者。HDB已承认存在这些疏失，并表示将收紧相关程序，防止类似错误再次发生。

公共住屋的分配与津贴都有经济审查，也有明确的申请条件，这样做是有道理的。一旦审核出现漏洞，原本应该给符合资格申请者的资源就会流向不该去的地方。虽然审计长公署没有指出这是普遍性问题，但在这么重要的制度里，就算是个别失误，也值得认真审视和纠正。

对大多数买组屋的人来说，这件事不会影响你自己的申请。不过，如果你正在排队等候，曾经遇过与HDB打交道时感觉前后不一致或难以理解的情况，知道官方目前正在严查整个流程，也是好事。预计制度将会收紧，对所有按规则申请的人来说，最终应该会更公平。
