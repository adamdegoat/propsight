Private home prices up 0.5%, HDB resale prices down 0.3% in Q2 flash dataPrivate home prices up 0.5%, HDB resale prices down 0.3% in Q2 flash data

-0.3% HDB resale price change in Q2HDB resale price change in Q2

The government's Q2 2026 flash estimates show private home prices nudged up 0.5% for the quarter, while HDB resale flat prices fell 0.3%. That HDB dip is now two quarters in a row, which makes it a trend rather than a one-off.

The two figures are heading in opposite directions for a reason. Private home buyers and HDB resale buyers are different groups responding to different pressures, and right now those pressures are pulling the markets apart.

If you are buying or selling in the HDB resale market, do not take comfort from positive headlines about private home prices. They are measuring something different. And if you are in the private market, a softening HDB market is not automatically a warning sign for you either. Track the segment you are actually in.
