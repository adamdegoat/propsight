Job market worries drag HDB resale prices and transaction volumes lower in Q2就业市场忧虑拖累HDB转售价及成交量，第二季双双下滑

-0.3% hdb resale price fall q2 20262026年第二季HDB转售价格跌幅

HDB resale prices fell for the second quarter in a row in Q2 2026, dropping 0.3%. Transaction volumes also came down, meaning fewer deals were done. Analysts are pointing directly at job market anxiety as the main reason buyers are holding back, rather than any fundamental problem with the flats themselves.

When people are worried about their jobs, they delay big financial decisions. A resale flat purchase is one of the largest commitments a Singaporean household can make, so it makes sense that buyers are taking longer to commit or pulling back entirely.

If you are selling an HDB flat right now, expect buyers to negotiate harder and take more time before signing anything. Pricing your flat realistically from the start will matter more than it did a year ago. Waiting for the market to improve is a gamble when the employment picture remains unclear.

2026年第二季HDB转售价格连续第二季下跌，跌幅0.3%。成交量也同步减少，意味着市场上完成的交易变少了。分析师直接将主因指向就业市场的忧虑，而不是组屋本身出了什么根本性的问题。

当人们担心工作不稳，就会推迟重大的财务决定。购买转售组屋是新加坡家庭能做出的最大承诺之一，所以买家花更长时间考虑、甚至暂时按兵不动，是完全可以理解的。

如果你现在正在卖HDB，要做好心理准备，买家会压得更狠，也会花更长时间才愿意签名。从一开始就定一个实际的价格，比一年前重要得多。在就业前景还不明朗的情况下，守着等市场好转，是在赌博。
