Dunearn House Launches from $1.475M: First Condo in Bukit Timah Turf CityDunearn House开售，入门价$1.475M起：武吉知马Turf City首个condo

$2,799 psf average launch price per sq ft开售平均尺价

Frasers Property has launched Dunearn House, the first private condo to come out of the Bukit Timah Turf City transformation area. Entry prices start at $1.475 million and the project averages $2,799 per square foot across the development.

The Turf City precinct has been on many buyers' radar for years, but until now there was no real pricing data to anchor expectations. Dunearn House changes that. Whatever comes next in the precinct will almost certainly be benchmarked against what Frasers is charging here, so this launch effectively sets the floor for the whole area.

For buyers who have been waiting to see what Turf City homes would cost, you now have your answer. At $2,799 psf with entry around $1.475 million, this is priced at a level that reflects the location's appeal and the long-term redevelopment story. Whether that premium makes sense for you depends on how much confidence you have in how the precinct develops over the next decade.
