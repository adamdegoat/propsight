Five of the most affordable 4-bedroom condos in Singapore, from under $2M新加坡最实惠的五个4卧室condo单位，$2M以下即可入手

$2M entry price for 4-bed condo4卧室condo入门价

A recent survey of the Singapore resale condo market has identified four-bedroom units available for under $2 million, a price point that gives larger families a realistic path into private property without crossing into premium territory. Four-bedroom condos at this level do exist, though they require some compromise on location or building age.

The trade-off with most of these units is that they tend to sit in older developments, further from the central region, or in projects where lower per-square-foot prices reflect factors like lease decay or less convenient transport access. That is not necessarily a dealbreaker, but buyers should weigh those factors honestly rather than focusing only on the headline price.

Families who genuinely need the bedroom count and are flexible on location should treat these listings as seriously worth viewing. Compare the maintenance fees, the remaining lease and the commute before deciding. In many cases, a resale four-bedder at this price will deliver far more liveable space than a smaller new-launch unit at a much higher cost, and the comparison is worth making on paper before dismissing it.

这类单位的常见取舍在于，它们通常位于较旧的发展项目，距离中央地区较远，或者较低的psf价格反映了租约递减或交通不够便利等因素。这不一定是拒绝的理由，但买家应该诚实地权衡这些因素，而不是只盯着表面价格。

真的需要这么多房间、且地点选择灵活的家庭，应该认真对待这些房源，值得亲自安排参观。在做决定之前，比较管理费、剩余租约和上班的交通情况。很多时候，这个价位的转售4卧室单位所提供的居住空间，将远胜于价格高出许多的小型新盘单位，这个对比值得好好算一算，不要轻易否定。
