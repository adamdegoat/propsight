Younger HDB Buyers Are Choosing Build-To-Order Over Resale Flats年轻HDB买家越来越倾向于选择BTO，而非转售组屋

First-time buyers are increasingly skipping the resale market and going straight for new Build-To-Order flats from HDB. The share of resale transactions involving younger buyers has been falling, and the main reason is not hard to figure out: BTO supply has improved and waiting times have come down from the punishing levels of a few years ago.

For young buyers, a new BTO flat is often a better deal. You get a fresh unit, lower prices with grants, and no need to deal with cash-over-valuation or surprise renovation costs left by the previous owner. With waiting times no longer stretching to five or six years, the patience required is less of a deterrent than it once was.

If you are selling a typical two- or three-room starter flat, be aware that your natural buyer pool is shrinking. The young couples who used to be your most eager buyers now have an alternative they prefer. You may need to price more competitively or market your flat on specific advantages like location, lease remaining, or quality of renovation to draw them in.

首次购房的买家越来越多地跳过转售市场，直接申请HDB的新BTO组屋。年轻买家参与转售交易的比例持续下滑，背后的原因并不难理解：BTO供应增加了，等待时间也比几年前那段漫长的岁月缩短了不少。

对年轻买家来说，新BTO组屋往往是更合算的选择。你得到的是全新单位、加上补贴后价格更低，也不必应付超额现金付款或前屋主遗留下来的装修问题。如今等待时间不再动辄延伸至五六年，以前那种需要极大耐心的阻碍已大为减少。

如果你正在出售一套普通两房式或三房式入门级组屋，请注意你的潜在买家群正在缩小。那些曾经最热切的年轻夫妇，如今已有了他们更偏爱的选择。你可能需要把价格定得更有竞争力，或者从地点、剩余租约、装修质量等具体优势入手，来吸引他们的注意。
