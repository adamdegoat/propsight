Community Care Apartments Now Open from Age 55, Monthly Fees Cut by Up to 75%社区关怀公寓申请年龄降至55岁，每月费用最高减少75%

75% cut in monthly care fees每月护理费用削减幅度

The government has made two significant changes to Community Care Apartments, a type of HDB flat designed for seniors who want care services built into where they live. The minimum eligibility age has been lowered from 65 to 55, and monthly service fees have been cut by up to 75%.

Community Care Apartments are designed for people who want to age in place without moving into a nursing home, with features and on-site services built in from the start. Previously you could not even apply until you were 65. Now at 55, Singaporeans can start planning and locking in this option a full decade earlier.

The fee cut is just as significant as the age change. A reduction of up to 75% in monthly costs makes these flats far more viable as a long-term option for seniors on fixed incomes. Families thinking ahead about housing for ageing parents, or individuals planning their own retirement, now have a serious option available earlier and at a fraction of the previous cost.

政府对Community Care Apartments作出了两项重大调整。这是一种专为希望在居所内享有护理服务的乐龄人士设计的HDB组屋。最低申请年龄已从65岁降至55岁，每月服务费最高减少75%。

Community Care Apartments是为那些希望在熟悉的环境中安度晚年、不想搬进疗养院的人士而设计的，从一开始就内置了各种配套设施和驻场服务。以前，您要到65岁才能申请。现在，55岁的新加坡人就可以开始规划，提早整整十年锁定这个选项。

费用削减的意义，丝毫不亚于年龄门槛的降低。每月费用最高减少75%，让这些组屋对收入固定的乐龄人士来说，成为更加可行的长期居住选择。无论是为年迈父母提前筹谋住所的家庭，还是为自己规划退休生活的个人，现在都多了一个来得更早、费用更实惠的正经选项。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Seniors and families planning ahead can now access this option a full decade earlier and at far lower monthly cost than before.乐龄人士和提早规划的家庭，现在可以提早整整十年选择这个方案，而且每月费用比以前低得多。
