Community Care Apartments now open from age 55, monthly fees cut by up to 75%Community Care Apartments开放给55岁以上人士申请，月费最高削减75%

75% monthly fee reduction for seniors乐龄人士月费降幅

Community Care Apartments are HDB flats built for seniors who want to live independently but with care services available on-site. The government has now lowered the minimum entry age from 65 to 55, giving Singaporeans a full decade more lead time to plan this move. Monthly service fees have also been cut by as much as 75%, which changes the financial picture considerably.

The fee reduction is arguably the bigger practical win. Previously, the ongoing monthly charges on top of housing costs put many eligible seniors off. At 75% less, the total cost of living in one of these units becomes genuinely competitive against the alternative of staying in a standard flat and arranging care separately.

If you are in your mid-50s and starting to think about housing for your later years, this option is now worth putting on your radar early. Buying at 55 rather than 65 means CPF Ordinary Account balances are likely higher, your loan eligibility is stronger, and you have more time to make a considered choice rather than a rushed one.

Community Care Apartments是专为希望独立生活、同时又能在现场享有看护服务的乐龄人士而建的HDB单位。政府现已将最低入住年龄从65岁降至55岁，让新加坡人可以提前整整十年开始规划这一生活安排。每月服务费也最多削减75%，大幅改变了整体财务状况。

费用削减可以说是更实际的利好消息。过去，住房成本以外每月持续的服务收费，让许多符合资格的乐龄人士望而却步。减少75%之后，住在这类单位的整体生活成本，与留在普通组屋另行安排看护服务相比，真正变得具有竞争力。

如果你正值五十多岁、开始考虑晚年的住房安排，现在就值得将这个选项纳入视野。在55岁而非65岁购买，意味着CPF普通账户的结余通常更充裕，贷款资格也更好，同时有更多时间从容做出决定，而不是仓促行事。
