Government Audit Finds HDB Allocated Flats and Grants to Ineligible Applicants政府审计发现HDB将组屋和津贴发放给不合资格申请者

Singapore's Auditor-General's Office has found that HDB allocated public housing flats and financial grants to applicants who were not eligible to receive them, due to gaps in the agency's internal processes. HDB has acknowledged the findings publicly and says it is working to strengthen the systems involved.

The audit is not pointing to widespread fraud, but it does confirm that the system has had process weaknesses that allowed some ineligible applicants to benefit. The flip side of that is the possibility that some eligible applicants may have been disadvantaged without realising it.

If you have had an HDB application rejected and the decision did not feel right, this audit is a reminder that the system is not perfect. Review the eligibility criteria carefully, gather your documentation, and write to HDB with specific and detailed questions about your case. The agency is accountable to applicants, and a clear, persistent follow-up often produces a more thorough review of your situation.

新加坡审计长公署发现，HDB因内部流程存在漏洞，将公共组屋和财务津贴分配给了不具备资格的申请者。HDB已公开承认相关发现，并表示正在着手完善相关系统。

这次审计并非指向大规模的欺诈行为，但确实证实了系统存在流程漏洞，让部分不合资格的申请者从中受益。反过来说，也有可能某些符合资格的申请者在不知情的情况下受到了不公平对待。

如果你曾有HDB申请遭到拒绝，而感觉决定并不合理，这次审计提醒我们，系统本身并不完善。仔细重温申请条件，整理好你的文件，然后以具体而详细的问题书面向HDB查询。HDB对申请者负有责任，清晰而持续的跟进，往往能换来对你情况更为彻底的重新审查。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
