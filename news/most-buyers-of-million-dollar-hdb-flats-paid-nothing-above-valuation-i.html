Most Buyers of Million-Dollar HDB Flats Paid Nothing Above Valuation in 20252025年，大多数百万HDB转售买家无需额外支付超出估值的现金

Data from PropNex shows that the majority of buyers who paid $1 million or more for an HDB resale flat in 2025 did not have to put up any cash above the bank valuation. In plain terms, official valuations broadly kept pace with the high prices being agreed in the market, so buyers did not face a shortfall they needed to cover out of pocket.

This was not always the situation. In earlier years when HDB resale prices were climbing sharply, valuations often lagged behind transaction prices, leaving buyers to make up the difference in cash. That gap could easily reach tens of thousands of dollars. The fact that valuations have broadly caught up is a meaningful shift for anyone trying to budget for a purchase at the top end of the market.

If you are looking at a pricey HDB resale flat, this is relatively reassuring news. You are less likely to be caught needing a large sum of extra cash on top of your CPF and home loan. That said, every transaction is different. Get an early valuation before you lock in a price so you know exactly where you stand.

PropNex的数据显示，2025年以$1 million或以上购入HDB转售单位的买家，大多数都不需要额外支付超出银行估值的现金。简单来说，官方估值大致上跟上了市场成交价，买家不需要自掏腰包来填补差额。

以前可不是这样的。早几年HDB转售价急剧攀升时，估值往往追不上成交价，买家要自己掏现金补差额，这个差额动辄就是几万块。如今估值大致已追上市场价，对于想在高端市场置业的买家来说，这是一个很实在的好转变。

如果你正在考虑购买价格较高的HDB转售单位，这算是比较让人放心的消息。你不太可能需要在CPF和房贷之外再另外准备一大笔现金。话虽如此，每一笔交易情况都不同。建议你在谈定价格之前先做一个估值，这样心里才有底。
