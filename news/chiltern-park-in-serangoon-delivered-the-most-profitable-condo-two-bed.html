Chiltern Park in Serangoon delivered the most profitable condo two-bedders over the past 10 yearsChiltern Park in Serangoon delivered the most profitable condo two-bedders over the past 10 years

A ten-year look at resale condo data in the Serangoon area finds that Chiltern Park's two-bedroom units delivered the strongest profits of any comparable project over that period. Owners who bought and sold in that unit type consistently came out ahead of two-bedder owners at nearby developments.

Several factors tend to drive outcomes like this: proximity to transport and amenities, the original launch pricing, and whether the unit size stayed in demand as buyer preferences shifted over time. Chiltern Park's two-bedders appear to have hit a combination that the resale market kept rewarding.

For buyers looking at suburban condos, the lesson is to go beyond brochures and developer claims. Pull the actual resale data for the specific unit type you want at the projects you are considering. Past profits do not guarantee future ones, but they tell you something real about what buyers have historically been willing to pay.
