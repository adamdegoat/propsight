Frasers Pays $2.13 Billion for Bayshore Drive Site, Singapore's Largest Non-CBD Land DealFrasers以$2.13 billion投得Bayshore Drive地段，创非CBD最高地价纪录

$2.13B record non-CBD land bid非CBD最高地价纪录

A consortium led by Frasers Property has won the government land sale tender for a mixed-use site at Bayshore Drive, paying $2.13 billion. That is the highest price ever paid for a development site outside the central business district, a record that underscores just how competitive the land market here remains.

When a developer pays that much for the land alone, the eventual launch prices for the residential units have to reflect it. Buyers who go in on the Bayshore project at launch will be paying prices that justify a $2.13 billion land cost, before construction expenses and profit margins are even considered.

If you are eyeing the Bayshore area, budget accordingly for the new launch. For owners of existing condos nearby, a project launched at such elevated prices typically pulls up the benchmark for surrounding resale values too. It is good news for current owners but a higher bar for anyone looking to buy in the area.

由Frasers Property牵头的财团赢得了Bayshore Drive综合用途地段的政府售地招标，出价$2.13 billion。这是有史以来在中央商业区以外，为一块发展地段所付出的最高价格，充分说明本地地价市场的竞争依然激烈。

当发展商单单在地价上就花了这么多，最终住宅单位的推出价格自然得反映这一成本。有意在Bayshore项目开盘时入手的买家，所付的价格必须能够撑起$2.13 billion的地价，这还没算上建筑费用和利润空间。

如果你看中的是Bayshore一带，购买新盘时请做好相应的预算。对于附近现有condo的屋主来说，一个以这么高价格推出的项目，通常也会带动周边二手condo的参考价格上涨。对现有屋主来说是好消息，但对有意在该区置业的买家而言，门槛就更高了。
