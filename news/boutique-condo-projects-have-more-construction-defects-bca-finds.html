Boutique condo projects have more construction defects, BCA finds小型精品condo瑕疵率更高，BCA调查发现

The Building and Construction Authority has flagged a pattern worth knowing: smaller boutique condo developments tend to produce more construction defects than larger projects. This is not a minor footnote. It is a direct challenge to one of the main selling points of boutique condos, which is the idea that fewer units means more care and attention to quality.

The likely reason is resources and oversight. Large developments have site supervisors, quantity surveyors, and contractors who know their reputation is on the line across hundreds of units. A small boutique project does not always get the same depth of monitoring, and if workmanship slips, there are fewer people catching it before handover.

If you are buying into a boutique condo, do not rely on a casual walk-around when you collect your keys. Book a proper defect inspection with an independent building inspector before you sign the handover documents. Any defects you accept at that point become your responsibility to chase, and that process can drag on for months.

建筑局（BCA）发现了一个值得关注的规律：规模较小的精品condo发展项目，往往比大型项目出现更多建筑瑕疵。这不是可以轻描淡写的小事，而是直接挑战了精品condo的一大卖点，即单位越少、品质越有保障的说法。

最可能的原因在于资源与监管力度。大型发展项目配有工地主管、工程量测量师和承包商，他们清楚自己的声誉与数百个单位息息相关。小型精品项目却未必有同等深度的监管，一旦施工质量出现问题，在交屋前能发现问题的人也相对较少。

如果你购入的是精品condo，收钥匙时切勿只是随便走一圈看看。在签署交屋文件之前，务必聘请独立建筑检验师进行正式的瑕疵检查。任何在交屋时接受的瑕疵，日后都得靠自己去追讨，整个过程可能拖上数月。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are buying a boutique condo, budget extra time for a thorough defect check and consider appointing an independent inspector before accepting your keys.如果你正在购买精品condo，要预留充足时间做好全面的瑕疵检查，并考虑在收钥匙前聘请独立验屋师。
