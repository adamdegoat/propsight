Million-dollar HDB flat sales hit new record in June as overall prices hold steady六月份百万HDB组屋成交创单月新高，整体房价基本持平

HDB resale flat prices in June 2026 held broadly steady compared to May, with no meaningful move up or down on a monthly basis. Within that flat headline, however, the number of flats sold at $1 million or above hit a fresh record for a single month, showing that the top end of the HDB resale market is running on a different track from the average.

The two facts together tell a more nuanced story than either number alone would suggest. The average resale price is not rising, which points to softness across much of the market. But buyers prepared to pay a million dollars or more for a public flat are clearly still active and competitive enough to keep pushing the ceiling to new highs.

For buyers, this means location and flat type within the HDB market are doing an enormous amount of the work. A well-positioned flat in a mature or sought-after estate can still command a record price even while the broader market drifts lower. If you are selling, be honest about whether your unit genuinely belongs in the premium tier or whether you are pricing it as if it does. If you are buying, be clear about what specific attributes you are paying a premium for and whether they justify the price relative to what else is available.

2026年6月，HDB转售组屋价格与5月相比基本持平，按月计算没有明显涨跌。但就在这个平稳的大背景下，成交价达$1百万或以上的组屋数量却在单月内创下新高，说明HDB转售市场的高端部分，走的是和整体市场截然不同的一条路。

这两组数字放在一起，讲的是一个比单看任何一个都更立体的故事。平均转售价格没有上涨，说明大部分市场偏软。但愿意为一套组屋付出$1百万甚至更多的买家，显然依然活跃、竞争激烈，足以不断把天花板推向新高。

对买家来说，这意味着在HDB市场里，地段和房型发挥着举足轻重的作用。一套位置优越、坐落在成熟或热门市镇的组屋，即便在整体市场走低的情况下，仍能卖出创纪录的价格。如果你在卖房，要诚实地问问自己，你的单位是否真的属于高端那一档，还是只是按那个价位来定价。如果你在买房，则要想清楚你究竟在为哪些具体条件付出溢价，以及相较于市场上的其他选择，这个价格是否真的值得。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Premium HDB flats in good locations are still fetching record prices even as average resale values drift lower, so where and what you buy within the HDB market matters more than ever.即使平均转售价格走低，地段好的优质HDB组屋仍能以创纪录的价格成交，所以在HDB市场里，买哪里、买什么，比以往任何时候都更加关键。
