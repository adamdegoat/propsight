Town Hall Link white site in Jurong Lake District goes to tender with up to 1,200 homes planned裕廊湖区Town Hall Link白色地段招标，计划兴建多达1,200个住宅单位

1,200 homes planned at JLD site裕廊湖区地段计划住宅数量

The government has put the Town Hall Link white site in the Jurong Lake District up for public tender. The developer who wins the bid is expected to build up to 1,200 homes as part of a mixed-use development that will also include commercial space. Jurong Lake District has been a long-running government priority as a second major business hub outside the city centre.

The site's position is significant. It sits next to a future MRT interchange that will connect four rail lines, making it one of the better-connected addresses in the western part of Singapore. Good transport access is one of the most durable drivers of property value, and a four-line interchange would put this development in rare company.

For buyers already in the Jurong area or considering it, more supply coming online over the next few years means more competition for your unit when you eventually want to sell or rent. It is not necessarily bad news, as the area's overall profile continues to rise, but it is a factor worth accounting for in your long-term planning. How much the site attracts at tender will also be a useful read on how confident developers are about Jurong's prospects.
