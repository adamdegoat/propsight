More than 2,500 EC income ceiling appeals filed in five years; 845 approved五年内逾2,500份EC收入上限申诉，845份获批

845 ec ceiling appeals approved in 5 years五年内获批的EC上限申诉宗数

Over the past five years, more than 2,500 buyers applied to HDB for a waiver on the income ceiling that applies to Executive Condominiums. Of those, 845 were approved, which works out to roughly one in three applicants getting through.

The EC income ceiling currently sits at $16,000 a month for most applicants. Households that earn just above that threshold can still apply for a waiver and, based on these numbers, they have a real shot at getting one.

If your income is nudging past the cap and an EC fits your budget, it is worth the time to file an appeal. The odds are not guaranteed, but one in three is better than most people assume.

过去五年，超过2,500名买家向HDB申请豁免Executive Condominium的收入上限。其中845份获批，大约每三位申请者中就有一位成功。

EC的收入上限目前对大多数申请者设定为每月$16,000。家庭收入略高于这一门槛的买家，仍可申请豁免，从这些数据来看，确实有一定的成功机会。

如果你的收入刚好超过上限，而EC又符合你的预算，花时间提交申诉是值得的。成功率虽然没有保证，但三分之一的机率比大多数人预想的要高。
