First Recorded 5-Room Resale at Dawson HDB Transacts at $1.39MDawson HDB 首个五房式转售单位以 $1.39M 成交

$1.39M first dawson 5-room resale priceDawson 首个五房式转售价

A 5-room flat at Dawson estate in Queenstown has changed hands for $1.39 million, marking the first recorded transaction at that price level for a 5-room unit in the development. Dawson sits in one of Singapore's most central mature towns, and it has long drawn buyers who want something that looks and feels a cut above the typical HDB block.

The $1.39 million figure is not just a headline number. It signals that buyers are willing to pay close to the price of a small private apartment for a flat in a well-designed, well-located public housing estate. Queenstown gives residents fast access to the city core, good schools, and established amenities, and Dawson in particular benefits from architecture and layouts that are more thoughtful than older HDB stock.

For owners in Dawson, this transaction sets a new reference point and confirms that the estate holds its value strongly. For buyers eyeing a unit there, the message is that waiting for prices to come down is unlikely to pay off. If you are comparing Dawson against private options at a similar quantum, factor in the HDB resale restrictions on ownership and future sale eligibility before committing.

$1.39M 这个数字不只是个吸睛的标题。它说明买家愿意为一套位置好、设计好的公共组屋，付出接近小型私人公寓的价钱。女皇镇交通便捷，靠近市区核心，学校和配套设施成熟；Dawson 更因为建筑设计和户型布局比老一代 HDB 组屋更用心，格外受到青睐。

对于 Dawson 现有业主来说，这次成交设立了新的参考价位，也再次证明该区的保值能力相当强。对于有意在那里置业的买家，信号已经很清楚：指望价格回落恐怕难以如愿。如果你在对比价位相近的 Dawson 和私人住宅，在拍板之前，记得将 HDB 转售在持有资格和未来出售条件方面的限制一并纳入考量。
