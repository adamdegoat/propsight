Condo and HDB rentals pick up in June after a quiet MayCondo和HDB租赁市场6月回暖，从五月淡静中复苏

Leasing activity in both the private condo and HDB rental markets bounced back in June after a noticeably slow May. More tenants were signing leases and more landlords were finding takers, suggesting the market had simply paused rather than stalled.

A quieter May is fairly typical as the market catches its breath after busier earlier months. The June recovery across both the private and public rental segments points to seasonal timing rather than any deeper shift in demand. Tenants still need homes and landlords still need income.

For landlords, the pickup is a good signal that your unit will not sit empty for long if it is priced sensibly. For renters, a more active market means more choices are available, but also more competition for the well-priced units. If you find something that fits your budget and requirements, do not assume it will wait.

私人condo和HDB租赁市场在6月双双回弹，结束了五月明显偏淡的局面。签约的租客增多，房东也更容易找到租户，这说明市场只是暂时喘口气，并非真的陷入停滞。

五月相对淡静，在这个市场里其实很正常，经过前几个月的忙碌，市场需要缓一缓。6月私人和公共租赁市场的同步回暖，更多反映的是季节性规律，而非需求上的根本性转变。租客还是需要找地方住，房东也还是需要租金收入。

对房东来说，这是个好信号，只要开价合理，单位不会空置太久。对租客来说，市场活跃意味着选择多了，但价格实惠的好单位竞争也更激烈。如果找到了符合预算和需求的单位，别以为它会等你，要果断出手。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are a landlord, the market is moving again; if you are hunting for a rental, expect more competition for well-priced units.如果你是房东，市场又活络起来了；如果你在找租房，要有心理准备，价格合理的单位竞争会更激烈。
