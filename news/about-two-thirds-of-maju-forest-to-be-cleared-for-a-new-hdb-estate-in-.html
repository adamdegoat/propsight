HDB10 Jul

About two-thirds of Maju Forest to be cleared for a new HDB estate in Clementi约三分之二的Maju Forest将被清除，为金文泰新HDB组屋区让路

~67% of Maju Forest to be clearedMaju Forest将被清除的比例

The government has announced plans to build a new public housing estate near Clementi that will require clearing roughly two-thirds of Maju Forest. Some sections of the forest and surrounding nature areas will be preserved, but the majority of the woodland will be converted to make way for HDB flats.

Maju Forest is a secondary forest that has attracted attention from conservation groups over the years. The scale of the clearing is substantial, and the decision to retain only a portion of it reflects the government's ongoing balancing act between housing demand and nature preservation. The preserved sections will matter to future residents, but the net loss of greenery is significant.

For people living near Clementi or considering buying there, the key practical points are these: more housing supply is coming to the area, which will weigh on price appreciation for existing properties in the short to medium term, and construction activity will be a constant backdrop for the neighbourhood over the years ahead. If the natural surroundings are part of why you chose Clementi, the area will look noticeably different once the estate is complete.

政府宣布计划在金文泰附近兴建新的公共住宅区，需要清除约三分之二的Maju Forest。部分森林和周边自然地带将予以保留，但大多数林地将被改建为HDB组屋。

Maju Forest是一片次生林，多年来一直受到自然保育团体的关注。这次清除的规模相当可观，只保留一部分林地的决定，折射出政府在住房需求与自然保育之间持续进行的权衡取舍。保留的部分对未来居民仍有意义，但绿地的净减少是显著的。

对于目前居住在金文泰附近或考虑在该区购房的人来说，以下几点实际考量值得关注：更多住房供应即将进入该区，这将在短中期内压制现有房产的价格升幅；而且未来多年，建筑工程将成为这一带持续不断的背景。如果当初选择金文泰正是因为喜欢这里的自然环境，待组屋区落成后，这一带的面貌将会有明显的改变。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Residents and buyers near Clementi should expect a new HDB precinct and years of construction activity ahead, which will add supply and change the character of the area.金文泰附近的居民和买家应预期未来将有一个新HDB组屋区，以及多年的建筑工程，这将增加供应并改变该区的整体面貌。
