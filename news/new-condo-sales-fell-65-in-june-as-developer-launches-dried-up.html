New Condo Sales Fell 65% in June as Developer Launches Dried Up六月新condo销售量骤降65%，发展商推盘明显减少

65% drop in new condo sales june六月新condo销售量跌幅

New private home sales in Singapore fell 65% from May to June, a sharp month-on-month drop that looks alarming on the surface. The real reason is straightforward: developers deliberately held back project launches during the school holiday period, leaving very little new stock for buyers to transact on.

When there is nothing to buy, nobody buys. This is a supply problem, not a demand problem. Analysts are already expecting the numbers to bounce back in July as developers bring more projects to market and buyers return.

If you are watching the market and worried this signals a collapse in buyer appetite, set that concern aside. One month of thin sales caused by a lack of launches tells you nothing useful about where prices are heading. Wait for the second half of the year's launch pipeline before drawing any conclusions about the broader market.

新加坡私宅新盘销售量从五月到六月下跌了65%，环比跌幅看起来相当惊人。但背后的原因其实很简单：发展商在学校假期期间刻意减少推出新项目，导致市面上可供买家选购的新盘寥寥无几。

没有新盘可买，自然就没有成交。这是供应问题，不是需求问题。分析师预计七月份数据将会回弹，因为届时发展商将会推出更多项目，买家也会陆续回归市场。

如果你一直在观望市场，担心这预示着买家需求大幅萎缩，大可放下这个顾虑。一个月因为新推盘减少而导致的低成交量，并不能说明房价的走向。不妨等到下半年的推盘计划逐渐明朗后，再对整体市场做出判断。
