More Two-Room Flexi BTO Flats Built, But Demand From Singles Stays HighHDB增加BTO两房式灵活单位，但单身人士需求依然旺盛

HDB has been increasing the number of two-room flexi flats in recent BTO launches, recognising that this is the only flat type single Singaporeans aged 35 and above can buy directly from HDB. The intention is to give singles more chances to secure a home through the public housing system.

The problem is that demand from singles is still running far ahead of supply. Application rates remain high, which means that even with more units on offer, your odds of winning a ballot on the first or second try are not good. More flats helps at the margins, but it has not closed the gap.

If you are a single planning to buy a BTO flat, go in with realistic expectations. Budget for two or three rounds of balloting rather than counting on a quick win. Some singles also weigh buying a resale flat instead, which costs more upfront but gives certainty that a ballot cannot.

HDB在最近几轮BTO推介中持续增加两房式灵活单位的数量，因为这是35岁及以上单身新加坡人唯一可以直接向HDB购买的组屋类型。这样做的目的，是让单身人士有更多机会通过公共住房系统置业。

问题在于，单身人士的需求依然远超供应。申请率持续偏高，这意味着就算单位多了，第一次或第二次抽签就中的机率也不高。多出来的单位虽然有帮助，但还远没有填平供需之间的差距。

如果你是单身，正在计划买BTO，心态要实际一些。做好抽两三次签的准备，不要指望一击即中。也有一些单身人士会考虑买转售组屋，虽然前期费用更高，但好处是不用靠运气，买了就是买了。
