Gillman Barracks confirmed as a new housing estate, 25 heritage buildings retained政府确认Gillman Barracks将发展为新住宅区，保留25栋历史建筑

25 heritage buildings to be retained将予以保留的历史建筑数量

The government has confirmed that Gillman Barracks, the arts enclave off Alexandra Road, will be redeveloped into a new residential estate. Twenty-five of the site's heritage buildings will be retained, and the existing greenery will be woven into the future development.

Gillman Barracks sits in a part of Singapore that has had very little new housing for decades. It is close to the MRT, well connected by road, and flanked by good amenities, which makes it genuinely sought-after land.

For buyers, this means future supply in a tightly held, central location. It will take years to materialise, but the announcement signals that even areas close to the city core are not off limits for new housing.

政府已确认，位于亚历山大路旁的艺术聚落Gillman Barracks将重新发展为新住宅区。场地内25栋历史建筑将获保留，现有绿化也将融入未来的发展设计之中。

Gillman Barracks所在地区数十年来几乎没有新房供应。这里靠近MRT、道路连接便捷，周边配套设施完善，是真正炙手可热的地段。

对买家来说，这意味着未来将在一个供应稀缺的中心地带出现新房源。虽然还需要数年时间才能落成，但这项公告传递出一个信号：即使是靠近市区核心的地带，也不是新房供应的禁区。
