HDB Resale Prices Hold Steady in June, Million-Dollar Flat Sales Hit a New Monthly Record6月HDB转售价格基本持稳，百万元成交量创单月新高

HDB resale prices were broadly unchanged in June 2026, which on the surface sounds like a quiet month. But underneath that stability, the top end of the market was anything but calm. The number of HDB flats sold at $1 million or more reached a new monthly record, meaning more million-dollar transactions happened in June than in any previous month.

The two trends are not contradictory. Overall prices can hold steady while the upper segment pushes higher. Larger flats in mature, well-served towns are commanding premiums that buyers are clearly willing to pay, even as the broader market stays flat.

For buyers, the June data is a reminder that HDB is not one single market. A three-room flat in a less central town sits in a very different environment from a five-room in Bishan or Queenstown. Know which segment you are actually competing in, because what is happening at the million-dollar end tells you very little about pricing in the segment where you are actually shopping.

2026年6月HDB转售价格整体变化不大，表面上看似平静的一个月。但平静的表象之下，市场高端的交易却一点都不平淡。当月成交价达$1 million或以上的HDB组屋数量创下单月历史新高，即6月的百万元成交宗数超过了此前任何一个月。

这两个趋势并不矛盾。整体价格可以保持稳定，同时高端市场继续走高。成熟市镇、配套完善地区的大型单位正获得买家愿意支付的溢价，即便整体市场维持平稳。

对买家而言，6月的数据提醒我们：HDB并不是一个单一市场。较偏远市镇的三房式单位，和碧山或女皇镇的五房式单位，所处的市场环境截然不同。清楚自己真正在哪个细分市场竞争，因为百万元高端市场的动向，对你实际选购的价格区间来说，参考意义其实相当有限。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Overall HDB prices are not falling sharply month to month, but if you want a large flat in a sought-after area, crossing the million-dollar mark is increasingly the norm rather than the exception.整体HDB价格并没有逐月大幅下跌，但如果你想买一个地段好、面积大的单位，破百万元已经越来越成为常态，而不是例外。
