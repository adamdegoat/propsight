Community Care Apartments open to seniors from age 55, monthly fees cut by up to 75%Community Care Apartments 向55岁乐龄人士开放，每月费用最高削减75%

75% monthly fee cut for care apts护理公寓每月费用削减幅度

The government has made two significant changes to the Community Care Apartments scheme. The minimum entry age has been brought down from 65 to 55, and monthly service fees have been cut by as much as 75%. Together these changes make assisted-living public housing a realistic option for a much wider group of seniors and their families.

Community Care Apartments are purpose-built flats that combine independent living with on-site care support, designed for seniors who want to manage their own lives but have help nearby when needed. Previously, the entry age of 65 and higher fees limited who could access the scheme. The new rules mean someone in their mid-fifties can start planning and securing a place a full decade earlier than before.

If you or a family member are approaching 55 and thinking about retirement housing, this scheme is now worth looking into seriously. Getting in early while you have options and time to plan is far better than waiting until care needs become more pressing. With fees now substantially lower, the financial case is much stronger than it was even a year ago.

政府对Community Care Apartments计划做出了两项重大调整。最低入住年龄从65岁降至55岁，每月服务费最高削减75%。两项改变加在一起，让这个提供生活协助的公共住房方案，成为更多乐龄人士和家庭都能考虑的实际选择。

Community Care Apartments是专门建造的组屋单位，结合了独立生活与现场护理支持，专为那些希望自主生活、但需要时能获得协助的乐龄人士而设计。以前，65岁的入住年龄门槛加上较高的费用，限制了能够申请该计划的人群。新规定意味着，五十出头的人可以比以前整整早十年开始规划和锁定住所。

如果你或家人快到55岁，正在考虑退休住房的问题，这个计划现在很值得认真了解。趁还有选择余地、有时间规划时提早行动，远比等到护理需求变得更迫切才处理要好得多。如今费用大幅降低，从经济角度来看，这个方案比一年前更加吸引人了。
