Lentor Gardens Residences preview draws 5,000 visitors over two daysLentor Gardens Residences 预览活动两天吸引5,000名访客

5,000 visitors at preview weekend预览周末访客人数

The preview of Lentor Gardens Residences held on 4 and 5 July drew 5,000 visitors across two days. That is a substantial crowd for a single project and signals that buyer appetite in the Lentor corridor remains well above average even as sentiment has cooled in other parts of the market.

Lentor has become one of the more consistently active residential areas in the north over the past couple of years, with several new launches drawing strong early interest. Whether 5,000 preview visitors converts into equally strong sales figures will be the real measure, but a turnout of that size is not something developers take lightly or attribute to casual curiosity.

For buyers interested in this project or others in the Lentor area, high preview attendance means genuine competition for units is likely. Going in with your finances already in order, your preferred unit types ranked by priority, and a clear sense of your firm maximum price will put you in a much stronger position than arriving on launch day without a plan. Popular launches move quickly and the best units go first.
