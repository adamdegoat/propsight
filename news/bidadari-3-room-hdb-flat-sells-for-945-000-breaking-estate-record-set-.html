Bidadari 3-room HDB flat sells for $945,000, breaking estate record set just 8 months ago碧达发3房式HDB单位以$945,000成交，打破8个月前刚创下的区内纪录

$945K bidadari 3-room record price碧达发3房式纪录成交价

A 3-room HDB flat in Bidadari has sold for $945,000, setting a new record for the estate. The previous record was broken less than eight months ago, which tells you how quickly prices there are moving.

To put that number in perspective, $945,000 for a 3-room flat ranks among the highest prices ever paid for that flat type anywhere in Singapore. Bidadari draws buyers with its relatively central location, good connectivity and newer stock, but those fundamentals have been known for a while. What is new is how aggressively buyers are willing to pay.

If you own a flat in Bidadari, this is good news for your paper value. If you are buying there, understand that you are paying a significant premium over most of Singapore for a flat that is still, on paper, public housing. The record will likely be broken again.

把这个数字放进去看：$945,000买一个3房式单位，跻身新加坡有史以来该房型最高成交价之列。碧达发能吸引买家，靠的是相对中心的地理位置、良好的交通连接以及较新的楼龄，这些优势大家都已经知道了。新的变化是，买家愿意付出的价格变得越来越进取。

如果你在碧达发有一个单位，这对你的账面价值是好消息。如果你正在那里购房，要明白你付的价格比新加坡大多数地方高出一大截，而那终究还是一个公共组屋单位。纪录很可能还会继续被打破。
