Condo Owner Sues MCST Over Termite Damage. Here Is Who WonCondo业主因白蚁损害起诉MCST，最终谁赢了？

A condo owner took their management corporation to court after termites spread from the building's common areas into their unit and caused damage. The owner argued the MCST had failed its maintenance duties and should bear the cost. The court's ruling helps draw a clearer line between what the MCST is responsible for and what sits with the individual owner.

MCSTs are legally required to maintain common property. If pests or structural problems originate there and cause damage inside a private unit, owners do have legal options. But the case also shows that evidence is everything. You need to demonstrate clearly that the source of the problem was common property and that the MCST knew about it or should have.

Before spending money on lawyers, get your facts in order. Document the damage with photos, report the problem in writing to your MCST, and keep a paper trail of their response. If the MCST ignores a known pest issue in a common area and your unit suffers as a result, this ruling gives you a firmer basis to pursue a claim.

一名condo业主因白蚁从大楼公共区域蔓延至其单位并造成损坏，将管理公司告上了法庭。该业主认为MCST没有尽到维护职责，应该承担相关费用。法院的判决有助于厘清MCST的责任范围，以及哪些部分该由个别业主自己负责。

MCST在法律上有责任维护公共财产。如果害虫或结构问题源自公共区域，并对私人单位造成损坏，业主是有法律途径可以追究的。不过，这个案例也告诉我们，证据才是关键。你必须清楚地证明问题的根源在公共区域，而且MCST知道或理应知道这个情况。

在花钱请律师之前，先把证据整理好。用照片记录损坏情况，以书面形式向MCST报告问题，并保存好对方的回应记录。如果MCST对公共区域已知的害虫问题置之不理，导致你的单位受损，这项判决就为你追讨赔偿提供了更有力的依据。
