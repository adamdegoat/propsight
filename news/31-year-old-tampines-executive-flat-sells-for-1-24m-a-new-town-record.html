31-year-old Tampines executive flat sells for $1.24M, a new town record淡滨尼一间屋龄31年的Executive组屋以$1.24M成交，创该镇转售新高

$1.24M Tampines resale town record淡滨尼转售镇最高价

A 31-year-old executive flat in Tampines has changed hands for $1.24 million, the highest price ever recorded in the town. Executive flats are among the largest HDB unit types available, but the age of this flat makes the price particularly striking. At 31 years old, the unit has already burned through roughly a third of its 99-year lease.

That lease age matters in practical terms. Banks typically reduce loan amounts for older HDB flats, and CPF usage rules tighten as the remaining lease shortens. A buyer committing $1.24 million to this flat is doing so with those constraints already in play, which says a lot about how much they valued the size, condition and location of the unit.

For sellers of large, older HDB flats in Tampines, this is an encouraging data point. The market is clearly willing to pay record prices even for flats that are not young. If your unit is well-maintained and in a good spot within the town, do not assume age alone caps what you can achieve.

淡滨尼一个屋龄31年的Executive组屋单位以$1.24 million成交，创下该市镇有史以来的最高转售价。Executive组屋是面积最大的HDB户型之一，但这个单位的屋龄让这个价格格外引人瞩目。屋龄31年，意味着该单位已消耗了99年租约中大约三分之一的年限。

租约年限在实际层面至关重要。银行通常会对较旧的HDB单位减少贷款额，而随着剩余租约缩短，CPF使用规则也会趋严。一个愿意为这个单位支付$1.24 million的买家，是在这些限制条件已然存在的情况下做出决定的，足以说明他们有多看重这个单位的面积、状况和地点。

对于在淡滨尼拥有大型旧组屋单位的卖家来说，这是一个令人振奋的数据。市场显然愿意为并不年轻的组屋支付创纪录的价格。如果你的单位保养良好、在市镇内地理位置不错，别以为屋龄本身就限制了你能卖到的价格上限。
