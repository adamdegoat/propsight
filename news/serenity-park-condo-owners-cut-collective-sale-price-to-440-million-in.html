Serenity Park Condo Owners Cut Collective Sale Price to $440 Million in Second AttemptSerenity Park condo业主第二次尝试集体出售，叫价降至$440 million

$440M serenity park collective sale priceSerenity Park集体出售价格

Owners at Serenity Park have returned to the collective sale market with a reduced asking price of $440 million. This is their second attempt after the first round failed to attract a developer willing to meet the original figure.

Developer appetite for large land sites has been cautious for good reason. The combined cost of buying the site, paying stamp duties, financing construction and then selling into an uncertain market is substantial. Sellers who set their price during the en bloc enthusiasm of a few years ago found that the numbers stopped working for developers as conditions shifted.

If you own a condo currently discussing a collective sale, manage your expectations carefully. The Serenity Park experience is a clear reminder that even a motivated and united group of sellers may need to trim their price and sit through multiple tender rounds before any developer commits. Do not plan your personal finances around a windfall that may arrive much later than you expect, or at a lower number.

Serenity Park的业主以$440 million的降低要价重返集体出售市场。这是他们第二次尝试，第一轮招标未能吸引到愿意接受原价的发展商。

发展商对大型地段持谨慎态度是有原因的。购地成本、印花税、建筑融资，加上在不确定市场中销售的压力，是一笔相当可观的综合开销。几年前en bloc热潮期间按当时价格定价的卖家发现，随着市场环境变化，这些数字对发展商而言已经不再划算。

如果你拥有一套condo，目前正在讨论集体出售事宜，请务必理性管理自己的预期。Serenity Park的经历清楚说明，即便是一个积极性高、意见统一的卖家群体，也可能需要下调价格、经历多轮招标，才能等到发展商点头。不要把可能姗姗来迟、甚至低于预期的一笔横财，提前计入自己的个人财务规划。
