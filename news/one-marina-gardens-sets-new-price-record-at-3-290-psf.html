One Marina Gardens sets new price record at $3,290 psfOne Marina Gardens sets new price record at $3,290 psf

$3,290 psf new price record per sq ftnew price record per sq ft

A unit at One Marina Gardens has just changed hands at $3,290 per square foot, the highest price ever recorded in the development and the new high-water mark among Marina Bay condos.

Marina Bay has always carried a premium, but this transaction pushes it to a new level. A 1,000 sq ft unit at that rate costs $3.29 million. Buyers in this precinct are paying for views, address, and prestige as much as for the actual space inside the unit.

If you are shopping in this area, be clear-eyed about what you are buying. A significant portion of that price tag is for the postcode. Ask yourself honestly whether that premium holds its value over your intended holding period, or whether you are paying top dollar at a market peak.
