HDB Resale Prices Hold Steady in June as Million-Dollar Flat Sales Hit New HighHDB转售价格6月保持平稳，百万元组屋成交量创历史新高

HDB resale prices barely moved in June 2026, but underneath that calm surface, the number of flats sold at $1 million or more hit the highest monthly count on record. The overall market is holding steady while demand at the top end keeps setting new benchmarks.

This split matters because it tells you the market is not uniformly hot. Million-dollar transactions are concentrated in specific flat types, specific floors, and specific locations. They are not happening across the board, and they should not set your expectations if your unit does not share those characteristics.

If you are selling an HDB flat, price it for what it actually is. A large flat in a mature, well-connected estate is competing in a different league from an average unit in a less popular town. The record headlines belong to a narrow slice of the market, and your flat may or may not be part of it.

For buyers, the overall stability means you are not being pushed into panic purchases across the board. Do your homework on comparable transactions for the specific area and flat type you want, and negotiate from real data rather than from what you read in the headlines.

HDB转售价格在2026年6月几乎没有变动，但在这平静的表面之下，成交价达到$1 million或以上的组屋数量创下了有史以来单月最高纪录。整体市场保持稳定，而高端需求则持续刷新新标杆。

这种分化很重要，因为它告诉你市场并非全面火热。百万元成交主要集中在特定房型、特定楼层和特定地段，并非遍地开花。如果你的单位不具备这些特质，就不应该以此来设定你的预期。

如果你正在出售HDB组屋，定价时要根据单位的实际情况来。一套位于成熟、交通便利市镇的大型组屋，和一般市镇里普通单位的竞争层次完全不同。那些破纪录的新闻只属于市场里极少数的组屋，你的单位可能属于这一类，也可能不属于。

对于买家来说，市场整体稳定意味着你不需要因为普遍的恐慌而仓促出手。在你想要的特定区域和房型上，认真研究可比较的成交记录，用真实数据来谈判，而不是被新闻标题牵着鼻子走。
