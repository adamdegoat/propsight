HDB Resale Prices Hold Steady In June As Million-Dollar Flat Sales Hit A New High6月HDB转售价格基本平稳，百万元组屋成交量再创新高

HDB resale flat prices held broadly steady in June 2026, a slight pause from the two-quarter slide that preceded it. But the more striking story is what happened at the top end at the same time: the number of flats sold for $1 million or more hit a fresh record in the same month.

These two things happening together tell you something important about where the HDB resale market sits right now. The middle and lower end is soft, with fewer young buyers competing and more BTO options pulling them away. But large, well-located flats in mature estates are playing in a different market, and demand there remains strong and willing to pay.

If you are selling a large flat or one in a prime HDB location, do not let the broader headline about softening resale prices talk you into underpricing. The million-dollar trend is real and still running. If you are buying a smaller or more standard resale flat, the softer overall market gives you more leverage. Know which part of the market your transaction sits in before you set or accept a number.

2026年6月，HDB转售组屋价格整体保持稳定，与此前两个季度的下滑趋势相比略有停顿。但同期更引人注目的，是顶端市场的动态：同一个月内，成交价达到$1 million或以上的组屋数量创下新纪录。

这两件事同时发生，透露出HDB转售市场目前所处位置的重要信息。中低端市场较为疲软，年轻买家竞争减少，更多BTO选项将他们引向别处。但成熟市镇中面积大、地段优越的组屋，正在另一个市场里角逐，那里的需求依然强劲，买家愿意付出代价。

如果你正在出售一套大型组屋，或位于优越HDB地段的单位，不要因为整体转售价格走软的大标题而低估自己的房产。百万元趋势是真实的，而且仍在延续。如果你正在购买一套规模较小或较为普通的转售组屋，整体市场的疲软给了你更多议价筹码。在开价或接受报价之前，先搞清楚你的交易属于市场的哪个部分。
