Bidadari 3-Room HDB Flat Sells for Record $945,000, Just Eight Months After the Last RecordBidadari三房式HDB组屋以$945,000创纪录成交，距上次破纪录仅八个月

$945k record 3-room resale price三房式转售价格纪录

A 3-room flat in Bidadari recently sold for $945,000, setting a new record for that flat type. What makes this particularly notable is that the previous record was set only eight months ago, meaning buyers have pushed the ceiling up twice in under a year.

Bidadari has earned its premium. It is a newer estate with thoughtful design, green communal spaces, and a direct MRT connection that puts residents within easy reach of the city. Buyers clearly see it as a cut above a typical HDB estate, and the transaction prices reflect how much they are willing to pay for that combination of quality and convenience.

If you own a flat in Bidadari or a comparable newer well-connected estate, the market may be valuing your home at more than you think. But if you are a buyer paying close to $1 million for a 3-room flat, the numbers need careful scrutiny. A future buyer will need to justify paying even more, and as prices rise and the remaining lease shortens, that becomes progressively harder. Buy because you genuinely want to live there, not primarily because you expect someone else to pay even more later.
