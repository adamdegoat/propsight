Future Sembawang Executive Condo Could Still Be Competitive Despite New 10-Year Lock-In即便有新的10年限制，实里达未来的EC或仍具竞争力

10 years new EC minimum occupancy period新EC最低自住年限

The government has extended the minimum occupation period for executive condominiums to 10 years before owners can sell on the open market to Singapore permanent residents or foreigners. A planned EC site in Sembawang will be among the first projects to come under this new rule, and the question for prospective buyers is whether the longer lock-in changes whether an EC still makes financial sense.

Sembawang land values are lower than more central locations, which gives developers some room to keep launch prices reasonable even under the tighter policy. An EC in the north could still come in meaningfully cheaper than a fully private condo with comparable finishes, which remains the core appeal of the EC scheme for buyers who qualify.

The practical shift is one of mindset. A 10-year occupation requirement means an EC is a genuine long-term commitment, not a medium-term investment with a clear exit at the five-year mark. If you are buying it as a home you expect to live in for a decade, the value proposition can still be strong. If you were counting on being able to sell or monetise the unit earlier, that plan no longer works under the new rules, and you should factor that in before signing.

政府已将executive condominium的最低自住年限延长至10年，届时业主才能在公开市场向新加坡永久居民或外国人出售单位。实里达一个计划中的EC地段将是首批适用新规的项目之一，准买家需要考虑的问题是：更长的锁定期，是否改变了EC在财务上的可行性。

实里达的地价低于更靠近市区的地段，这给了发展商一定的空间，在更严格的政策下仍能将推出价格维持在合理水平。北部一个EC的价格，仍可能比同等装修水平的完全私宅便宜不少，这对符合资格的买家来说，依然是EC计划的核心吸引力所在。

实质上的变化是心态上的转变。10年自住要求意味着EC是一个真正的长期承诺，而不是一个有明确五年出场点的中期投资。如果你是打算在那里住上十年的自住买家，性价比依然可以很高。但如果你原本打算早些出售或变现，新规则下这条路已经行不通了，签约前必须将这一点纳入考量。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are considering an executive condo, be comfortable committing to it as a genuine long-term home for at least 10 years rather than treating it as a shorter-term investment with a quick exit.如果你正在考虑购买EC，要做好以它作为长期自住房屋至少10年的心理准备，而不是把它当成一项可以尽早套现的短期投资。
