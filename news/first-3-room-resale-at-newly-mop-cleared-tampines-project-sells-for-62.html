First 3-room resale at newly MOP-cleared Tampines project sells for $628,000刚过MOP的淡滨尼HDB项目，首个3房式转售单位以$628,000成交

$628,000 first 3-room resale price首个3房式转售价

A 3-room flat at a Tampines HDB project that recently cleared its Minimum Occupation Period has sold for $628,000, the first resale recorded there. The MOP is the five-year window during which flat buyers cannot sell on the open market, so this transaction represents the earliest moment a seller could have cashed out.

For a 3-room flat in a non-central town, $628,000 is a meaningful number. These are typically the smaller units in any development, and this price signals that buyers are willing to pay a clear premium for newer stock with modern fittings and a longer remaining lease, even outside the central region.

For anyone buying or selling in Tampines right now, this figure gives you a fresh anchor. Sellers in the same project will watch the next few transactions carefully to see whether this holds, while buyers can use it as a starting point for evaluating whether similar units in older nearby blocks represent better or worse value.

淡滨尼一个HDB项目刚过Minimum Occupation Period，首宗转售记录显示一个3房式单位以$628,000成交。MOP是指组屋买家在五年内不能在公开市场出售单位，因此这笔交易正是卖家能够套现的最早时机。

对于非中央地区市镇的3房式单位来说，$628,000是一个很有分量的数字。这类单位通常是一个发展项目中面积较小的户型，而这个价格说明买家愿意为拥有现代装修、剩余租约较长的新组屋支付可观溢价，即便地点不在中央地区。

对于正在淡滨尼买卖的人来说，这个数字为你提供了一个全新的参考点。同一项目的卖家会密切关注接下来几宗交易，看价格能否维持；买家则可以用它作为起点，评估附近旧组屋区同类单位的性价比孰优孰劣。
