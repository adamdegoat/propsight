VIP Preview Sales At Condo Launches Are Under Regulatory ScrutinyCondo新盘VIP预览销售受到监管机构关注

Regulators are now looking at the practice of VIP previews at new condo launches, where property agents and their preferred clients get first pick of units before the general public can even register interest. The practice has been around for years but has drawn growing criticism for locking ordinary buyers out of the best opportunities.

At a VIP preview, the best-facing, highest-floor, and best-priced units often sell before most buyers are even aware the project is open. By the time a regular buyer shows up on launch day, they are choosing from what is left. If you do not have an agent with the right connections to the developer, you start at a disadvantage from the very beginning.

Authorities paying attention to this is a good sign for buyers who have felt shut out. If tighter rules follow, it could mean a more level playing field where desirable units go to whoever gets there first, not whoever has the most connected agent. Nothing has changed yet, but if you are planning to buy a new launch, it is worth watching how this review plays out.

在VIP预览中，朝向最好、楼层最高、价格最有吸引力的单位，往往在大多数买家意识到项目已开售之前就已售出。等到普通买家在发售日当天现身，能选择的已是剩余货色。如果你的经纪与发展商之间没有合适的关系网络，你从一开始就处于劣势。

监管机构对此表示关注，对于感到被拒之门外的买家来说是个好消息。如果更严格的规定随之出台，可能意味着一个更公平的竞争环境，受欢迎的单位归最先到达的人，而非拥有最多人脉的经纪的客户。目前尚无任何改变，但如果你有意购买新楼盘，这项审查的进展值得密切关注。
