Singapore Lowers Age for Senior-Friendly HDB Flats to 55 and Cuts Monthly Fees by Up to 75%新加坡将HDB乐龄关怀组屋申请年龄降至55岁，月费最高减75%

75% cut in monthly cca feesCCA月费削减幅度

The government has lowered the minimum age to apply for a Community Care Apartment from 65 to 55, and cut the monthly service fees by as much as 75%. These flats are purpose-built for seniors, with care services included, but until now you had to wait until you were 65 to get one.

The fee cut is substantial. If the ongoing monthly cost was putting you off before, that barrier is now much smaller. And pulling the entry age down by a full decade means seniors can plan a move sooner, rather than waiting until they are older and potentially less mobile or less able to manage the transition.

For the broader HDB resale market, this matters too. More people downsizing at 55 rather than 65 means larger flats will start freeing up sooner. If you own a 4-room or 5-room flat and are thinking of selling in the next few years, a steadier stream of downsizers entering the market could help support demand for your unit.

政府已将申请Community Care Apartment的最低年龄从65岁降至55岁，每月服务费最高可减75%。这类组屋专为乐龄人士而建，附带护理服务，但以往必须等到65岁才能申请。

费用削减幅度相当可观。如果你之前因为每月持续费用太高而打退堂鼓，现在这道门槛已经低了许多。申请年龄一口气提早了整整十年，意味着乐龄人士可以更早做搬迁打算，不必等到年纪更大、行动可能更不方便、或更难适应转变的时候才动身。

这对整体HDB转售市场同样有影响。更多人在55岁而非65岁时缩小住房，意味着较大的单位将更早流入市场。如果你拥有4房式或5房式组屋，并打算在未来几年出售，持续有新一批缩小住房的买家进入市场，有助于支撑你单位的需求。
