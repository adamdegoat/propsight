Queenstown 2-Room HDB Flat Sells for $696,000, a New National Record女皇镇两房式HDB组屋以$696,000成交，创全国最高价纪录

$696,000 national record 2-room flat price两房式组屋全国最高价纪录

A two-room HDB flat in Queenstown just sold for $696,000, setting a new national record for Singapore's smallest standard flat type. Two-room flats are compact homes typically suited to singles or couples, and this price would have seemed almost unthinkable for such a small unit not many years ago.

What is driving this is simple: Queenstown is a mature estate close to the city, jobs and amenities, and demand for that combination is fierce. Small flats in good locations attract buyers who want central living without stretching to a private property. Competition among those buyers has pushed prices into territory that would have raised eyebrows even five years ago.

For owners of two-room or other small flats in mature estates, this transaction is a clear reminder not to anchor your expectations to what similar flats sold for in the past. The market has moved significantly, and pricing your flat too conservatively just to close quickly could mean leaving real money on the table.
