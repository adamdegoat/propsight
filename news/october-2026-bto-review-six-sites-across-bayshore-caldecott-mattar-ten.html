6 bto launch sites in october 20262026年10月BTO推介地点数量

The October 2026 BTO launch covers six sites spread across very different parts of Singapore. The locations are Bayshore, Caldecott, Mattar, Tengah, Chencharu and Sembawang North, and each one comes with its own mix of transport access, nearby amenities and price positioning.

At one end you have Bayshore, which is shaping up as a new waterfront neighbourhood near East Coast Park. At the other end are greener, newer towns like Tengah and the still-developing Chencharu. Caldecott and Mattar sit closer to the city fringe, while Sembawang North is further out but tends to be more affordable for that reason.

The gap in lifestyle trade-offs between these six sites is real, so it pays to do a proper comparison before you decide where to apply. Transport links, waiting times and resale potential all vary significantly across the list. If you are planning to ballot in October, working through the sites now rather than rushing a choice closer to the date will save you from committing to somewhere that does not fit your actual needs.
