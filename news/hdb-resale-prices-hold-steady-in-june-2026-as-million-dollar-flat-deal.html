HDB resale prices hold steady in June 2026 as million-dollar flat deals hit a new record2026年6月HDB转售价格持稳，百万元组屋成交数量创历史新高

HDB resale prices held roughly flat in June 2026, with no significant movement up or down across most of the market. But buried in those numbers is a striking figure at the top end: the number of flats that changed hands at $1 million or more hit a new all-time monthly record, meaning more million-dollar HDB deals closed in June than in any previous month on record.

This split tells a nuanced story. The broad resale market has stopped rising, which means sellers across most flat types cannot count on general price momentum to carry their asking prices higher. But for large, well-located flats in mature estates with strong schools and MRT access, deep-pocketed buyers are still actively competing.

If you are selling a premium flat, the buyers are still out there. But pricing it right matters more than it did two years ago, because you are no longer being carried by a rising tide. If your flat is more ordinary in size or location, be realistic about what the market will actually bear right now rather than anchoring to the million-dollar headlines.

2026年6月HDB转售价格整体持平，市场大部分范围没有明显的升跌。但在这些数字里，顶端藏着一个引人注目的数据：成交价达$1 million或以上的组屋数量创下单月历史新高，意味着6月份完成的百万元HDB交易比历史上任何一个月都要多。

这种分化反映了一个较为复杂的面貌。广泛的转售市场已停止上涨，这意味着大部分单位类型的卖家，不能再依靠整体价格上涨来推高要价。但对于成熟组屋区内配套名校和MRT的大型优质组屋，资金充裕的买家仍在积极竞争。

如果你正在出售优质组屋，买家依然存在。但定价是否到位比两年前更为关键，因为你已不再被上涨的潮水所托举。如果你的组屋在面积或地段上较为普通，对于当前市场实际能承受的价格，要保持务实的预期，而非锚定在百万元的头条新闻上。
