Community Care Apartments Now Open to Singaporeans from Age 55, Monthly Fees Cut by Up to 75%乐龄关爱组屋申请年龄降至55岁，月费最多减少75%

75% reduction in monthly care fees每月护理费削减幅度

HDB is making two significant changes to its Community Care Apartments scheme. The minimum age to apply is being lowered from 65 to 55, and monthly service fees are being cut by as much as 75%. Community Care Apartments are purpose-built HDB flats designed for seniors who want to live independently but with optional care services available as their needs change over time.

The fee cut is the more immediately impactful change. A 75% reduction means what was once a significant ongoing cost becomes far more manageable month to month. The age change means people in their mid-50s can now plan this housing transition while they still have ample time to make calm, considered decisions rather than being forced into a choice later under pressure.

If you have a parent in their mid-50s who is thinking ahead about future housing, or if you are in that age range yourself, this scheme is now worth looking at seriously. It offers the security of a purpose-built, care-friendly environment at a cost that is considerably more accessible than before, without needing to move into a nursing facility or rely on family to retrofit a standard flat.

HDB正对乐龄关爱组屋计划作出两项重大调整：申请的最低年龄从65岁降至55岁，每月服务费最多削减75%。乐龄关爱组屋是专为乐龄人士建造的HDB单位，供希望独立生活、同时又能随着需求变化选用护理服务的乐龄人士入住。

月费削减是这次即时影响最大的调整。削减75%意味着原本较为可观的持续月费，将变得更加负担得起。年龄调整则意味着五十多岁的人士现在就可以开始规划这一居住转变，还有充裕的时间从容地做出经过深思熟虑的决定，而不必等到日后被迫在压力下仓促选择。

如果你有父母正值五十多岁、在考虑未来的住房安排，或者你自己也处于这个年龄段，这个计划现在很值得认真考虑。它提供了专门为护理需求而设计的居住环境，费用比以前亲民得多，而且无需搬入疗养院或依赖家人对普通单位进行改装。
