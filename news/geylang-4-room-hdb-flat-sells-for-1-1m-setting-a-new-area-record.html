Geylang 4-room HDB flat sells for $1.1M, setting a new area record芽笼4房式组屋以$1.1M成交，刷新该区转售记录

$1.1M geylang 4-room resale record芽笼4房式转售记录

A 4-room HDB flat in Geylang has sold for $1.1 million, making it the most expensive resale deal ever recorded in that estate. What makes this stand out is that the unit is on a lower floor, where prices usually sit softer than mid or high floors.

Geylang has historically been one of Singapore's more affordable HDB towns. It is not Bishan or Queenstown. The fact that even a lower-floor flat there has crossed the million-dollar mark shows how broadly the resale price rally has spread, well beyond the estates buyers traditionally think of as premium.

For sellers around Singapore, this is a signal that buyer demand is strong enough to push prices into record territory in areas that were cheap just a few years ago. For buyers, the message is straightforward: do not assume a less fashionable address means an affordable price. Budget carefully and check recent transaction data before assuming what you can afford.

芽笼一个4房式组屋以$1.1 million成交，成为该区有史以来最贵的转售交易。特别之处在于，这个单位位于较低楼层，低楼层单位的价格通常比中层或高层来得软。

芽笼历来是新加坡较实惠的HDB市镇之一，比不上碧山或女皇镇。但就连这里的低楼层单位也突破百万元，说明转售价格的涨势已扩散得多广，远不只是买家传统上认为的优质市镇。

对全岛各地的卖家来说，这是一个信号：买家的需求足够强劲，就连几年前还很便宜的地区，也能把价格推至破纪录的水平。对买家而言，信息很直接：别以为地段不那么抢手，价格就一定实惠。做好预算规划，出手前先查近期成交数据，弄清楚自己真正负担得起的范围。
