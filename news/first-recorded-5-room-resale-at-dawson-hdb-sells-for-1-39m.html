First recorded 5-room resale at Dawson HDB sells for $1.39MDawson HDB首个5房式转售单位以$1.39M成交

$1.39M first Dawson 5-room resaleDawson首个5房式转售价

A 5-room flat at Dawson estate in Queenstown has sold for $1.39 million, the first recorded 5-room resale in the development. Dawson is widely considered one of the best-designed public housing projects in Singapore, with sky gardens, quality communal spaces and layouts that feel closer to private housing than typical HDB fare.

The price reflects both the location and what the project offers. Queenstown is a mature, centrally located town with strong MRT access and a full range of amenities nearby. Dawson itself has won architectural recognition and built a loyal following among buyers who prioritise how a building is designed and feels to live in, not just where it sits on a map.

This first 5-room transaction at $1.39 million sets a reference point the market will pay close attention to. If subsequent resales in Dawson hold near this level, it confirms that premium HDB developments in central locations command a tier of their own, one that sits well above the broader resale market and may continue to attract buyers willing to pay for quality over sheer square footage.

女皇镇Dawson组屋区一个5房式单位以$1.39 million成交，这是该发展项目首宗有记录的5房式转售交易。Dawson被公认为新加坡设计最出色的公共住宅项目之一，拥有空中花园、高品质的公共空间，以及比一般HDB组屋更接近私宅感觉的户型布局。

这个价格反映了地理位置与项目本身的双重价值。女皇镇是一个成熟的中央地区市镇，MRT交通便利，周边设施一应俱全。Dawson本身荣获建筑奖项肯定，在那些不只看地图位置、更重视楼宇设计和居住感受的买家中，积累了一批忠实拥趸。

这宗以$1.39 million成交的首个5房式单位，为市场设下了一个将被密切关注的参考点。如果Dawson接下来的转售价格维持在这个水平附近，就进一步印证优质中央地区HDB项目自成一个档次，远高于整体转售市场，并将持续吸引愿意为品质而非单纯面积出高价的买家。
