Over 1 million HDB households to receive utility and town council rebates in July逾百万HDB住户将于7月收到水电及市镇理事会回扣

1 million+ hdb households receiving rebates获得回扣的HDB住户数量

More than a million HDB households will receive two types of rebates in July: U-Save credits that go toward utility bills, and service-and-conservancy charge rebates that offset what you pay to your town council. Both arrive automatically with no application needed from residents.

The U-Save rebate is credited directly to your SP Services account and draws down against your electricity and gas bills over time. The S&CC rebate covers part of the monthly fee you pay for estate maintenance. Together they provide real relief on two of the regular costs of living in an HDB flat.

If you live in an HDB flat, just check your utility statement and town council billing in July to see the credits applied. There is nothing to do on your end.

超过百万个HDB住户将在7月收到两类回扣：U-Save回扣用来抵扣水电费，服务与杂费回扣则抵扣每月交给市镇理事会的费用。两项回扣均自动发放，居民无需申请。

U-Save回扣会直接存入你的SP Services账户，日后在扣除电费和煤气费时自动抵用。S&CC回扣则覆盖部分每月支付的房屋维护费。两项合计，能切实减轻住在HDB的日常开销。

如果你住在HDB，7月份只需查看水电账单和市镇理事会的账单，就能看到回扣已经抵扣的记录。不需要你做任何事情。
