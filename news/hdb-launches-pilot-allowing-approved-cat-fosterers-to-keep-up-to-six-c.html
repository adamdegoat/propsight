HDB Launches Pilot Allowing Approved Cat Fosterers to Keep Up to Six CatsHDB Launches Pilot Allowing Approved Cat Fosterers to Keep Up to Six Cats

6 cats allowed per hdb fosterercats allowed per hdb fosterer

HDB has launched a pilot scheme that allows approved cat fosterers to keep up to six cats in their flat, which is above the limit that ordinary households are allowed. The scheme is specifically for people who are actively working to rehome cats through official channels, not for regular pet owners who simply want more animals.

The word to focus on is approved. You will need to be verified as part of a recognised fostering programme to qualify. This is not a general relaxation of cat ownership rules in HDB flats, so do not assume you can keep six cats just by calling yourself a fosterer.

If you are already doing cat fostering work and have been navigating the rules carefully, this pilot gives you a proper, above-board path to house more animals while you find them permanent homes. Get yourself into the approved scheme and you remove the risk of a complaint or enforcement action against you.
