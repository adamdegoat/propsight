Why Renting Near the New Circle Line Stations Makes Sense in 20262026年，为什么在环线新站附近租房是个好主意？

The Circle Line extension is bringing new MRT stations to parts of Singapore that have been less connected until now. Better accessibility tends to attract more renters to an area, and the pockets around the newest stops are starting to see that kind of interest build.

The rental market in these areas has not fully repriced yet, because the stations are still fresh. That creates a short window where you might find a flat or condo near a new stop at a price that has not yet caught up with the improved connectivity.

If you are renting and can be flexible on location, it is worth checking what is available near the newer Circle Line stops before landlords adjust their asking rents upward. Once the convenience becomes common knowledge in the market, deals in those pockets tend to dry up fast.

环线延伸工程为新加坡一些过去交通不太方便的地区带来了新MRT站。交通改善了，自然会吸引更多人到附近租房，最新开通的几个站点周边，已经开始感受到这股需求升温。

目前这些地段的租金还没有完全涨上去，因为新站才刚开通不久。这就给租客留了一段短暂的窗口期，说不定还能在新站附近找到价格还没跟上交通改善步伐的HDB单位或condo。

如果你正在找租房，而且对地点比较弹性，建议趁房东还没调高要价，先去看看环线新站附近有什么选择。一旦这些地方的便利性在市场上广为人知，好价钱的盘往往很快就没了。
