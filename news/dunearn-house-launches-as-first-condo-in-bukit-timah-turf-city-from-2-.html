Dunearn House launches as first condo in Bukit Timah Turf City from $2,799 per square footDunearn House以每平方英尺$2,799推出，成为武吉知马Turf City首个condo新盘

$2,799 psf dunearn house launch price per sqftDunearn House每尺推出价

Frasers Property has launched Dunearn House, the first private condo released in the long-awaited Bukit Timah Turf City redevelopment. Prices start at $2,799 per square foot, with entry units from $1.475 million. Stepping up to a three-bedroom adds roughly $1.1 million on top of that.

Turf City has been earmarked for homes for a long time and this is the first concrete pricing the market has seen from it. The per-square-foot rate puts it squarely in premium territory, consistent with what buyers in the Bukit Timah corridor have come to expect.

For anyone watching Turf City hoping for value, this launch confirms it is not that kind of market. Buyers here are paying for the address, the greenery and the proximity to well-regarded schools, not for affordability. If that fits your brief, you now have a real number to plan around.

富誉集团（Frasers Property）推出Dunearn House，这是众所期待的武吉知马Turf City重建项目首个私宅condo。价格由每平方英尺$2,799起，入门单位$1.475 million起，升级至三房式单位则需在此基础上再加约$1.1 million。

Turf City早已被规划用于住宅发展，而这是市场首次看到来自该地区的具体定价。每平方英尺的价位明显属于高端范畴，与买家对武吉知马一带的预期相符。

若你一直关注Turf City，期望能从中找到具有性价比的选择，这次推出已经给出了答案：这里并非那种市场。在这里置业，你所支付的是地段、绿意，以及靠近名校的便利，而非实惠的价格。如果这符合你的需求，你现在终于有了可以据此规划的实际数字。
