Private home prices edge up 0.5% in Q2 while HDB resale falls for second straight quarterQ2私宅价格微涨0.5%，HDB转售价连续第二季下跌

+0.5% private home price rise q2 20262026年第二季私宅价格涨幅

Q2 2026 flash estimates show private home prices nudged up 0.5% for the quarter. The gain is real but modest, and it marks a clear slowdown from the pace seen in recent years.

The HDB resale market is telling a different story. Prices there dipped 0.3%, the second quarter in a row they have fallen. That puts the two markets on opposite tracks heading into the second half of the year.

For private home owners, the numbers are reassuring but not cause for celebration. Values are stable, not surging. For HDB sellers, two consecutive dips are a signal that buyers have the upper hand for now, and pricing expectations need to be realistic.

2026年第二季闪估数据显示，私宅价格季度涨幅为0.5%。涨幅虽然是实实在在的，但相当温和，与近年来的步伐相比，明显是慢下来了。

HDB转售市场则呈现出截然不同的面貌。转售价格下跌了0.3%，这是连续第二季录得下跌。两个市场走向相反，就带着这样的格局迈入下半年。

对私宅业主来说，这个数字让人安心，但也不必太过兴奋，价格是稳住了，不是在猛涨。对想出售HDB的屋主来说，连续两季下跌是个信号：现在是买家占上风，卖家对价格的期望必须实际一点。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
