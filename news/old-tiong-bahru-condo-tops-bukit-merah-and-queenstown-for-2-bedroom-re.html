Old Tiong Bahru Condo Tops Bukit Merah and Queenstown for 2-Bedroom Resale Profits中峇鲁旧condo两房转售利润领跑武吉美拉和女皇镇

$306,000 average 2-bedroom resale profit两房转售平均利润

An analysis of resale transactions across Bukit Merah and Queenstown found that one older condominium in Tiong Bahru delivered average profits of $306,000 per 2-bedroom unit resold, outperforming newer projects in the same district. That is a strong result, especially given that older developments typically carry a discount in buyers' minds due to shorter remaining lease or dated fittings.

The outperformance came down to two things: the Tiong Bahru location has sustained genuine demand thanks to its charm, food culture, and accessibility, and original buyers entered at prices low enough that even modest appreciation translated into solid dollar returns.

For buyers, the lesson is not to rule out older developments on age alone. What matters more is what you pay to get in, whether the location has durable appeal, and whether the building has been reasonably well maintained. A well-located older condo bought at the right price can easily beat a newer one bought at a steep premium.

针对武吉美拉和女皇镇一带转售交易的分析发现，中峇鲁一栋较旧的condo每个两房转售单位的平均利润达$306,000，跑赢同区较新的项目。这个成绩相当亮眼，尤其考虑到旧楼盘在买家心目中通常会因为剩余租约较短或装修过时而打折扣。

这番出色表现归结于两点：中峇鲁的地段凭借其独特魅力、饮食文化和交通便利，维持了持续的真实需求；加上早期买家的入手价足够低，即便楼价只是温和升值，换算成实际金额也是相当可观的回报。

对买家而言，这个案例告诉我们，不要单凭楼龄就把旧楼盘排除在外。更重要的是你以什么价格入手、地段是否具有持久吸引力，以及大厦是否维护得当。以合理价格买入一个地段优越的旧condo，回报可以轻松超越那些以高溢价购入的新项目。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Age alone does not decide a condo's resale return; location and the price you paid to enter matter more, so do not dismiss older developments when looking for good value.楼龄本身不决定condo的转售回报；入手价格和地段更为关键，所以不应轻易否定旧楼盘的投资价值。
