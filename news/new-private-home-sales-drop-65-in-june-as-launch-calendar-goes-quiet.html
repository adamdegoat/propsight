New Private Home Sales Drop 65% in June as Launch Calendar Goes Quiet6月新私宅销量骤降65%，推盘淡季压低成交

65% fall in new condo sales june6月新condo销量跌幅

June was an unusually slow month for new private home sales. Developers moved just a fraction of what they sold in May, with volumes dropping 65% and landing at a two-year low. The main reason is straightforward: the school holiday period meant almost no new projects were launched, leaving buyers with very little fresh stock to look at.

This does not signal a market in trouble. When there is nothing to buy, no one buys, and that is largely what happened here. Developers are expected to release more projects in July, which should bring sales numbers back to a more normal pace.

For buyers, a quiet month like this can be a useful window. Fewer competitors at showflats means more time to compare options, and developers sitting on unsold units may be slightly more open to sweeteners or flexible payment terms. But do not walk in expecting big discounts. Underlying supply remains tight and prices have not softened in any meaningful way.

6月是新私宅市场异常淡静的一个月。开发商的成交量仅为5月的一小部分，整体跌幅达65%，跌至两年最低点。主要原因很直接：学校假期期间几乎没有新项目推出，买家能看到的新房源寥寥无几。

这并不意味着市场出了什么问题。没有东西可买，自然就没有人买，这次大体上就是这种情况。开发商预计将在7月陆续推出更多项目，届时销量应该会恢复到较为正常的水平。

对买家而言，这样的淡静月份其实是个好时机。看楼的竞争者少了，比较各个选项的时间更充裕；手上还有未售单位的开发商，也可能稍微愿意提供一些额外优惠或更灵活的付款安排。不过，不要抱着期待大幅折扣的心态入场。整体供应依然偏紧，楼价也没有出现任何明显的松动。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
The market slowdown gives buyers more breathing room, but do not mistake a quiet month for falling prices as overall supply remains tight.市场放缓给买家多了一些喘息空间，但不要把淡静的月份误读为楼价下跌，整体供应依然偏紧。
