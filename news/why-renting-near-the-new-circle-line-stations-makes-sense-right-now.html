Why Renting Near the New Circle Line Stations Makes Sense Right Now为何现在就在新环线站附近租房是明智之举

The Circle Line extension is set to open new stations in 2026, and analysts are noting that rents near these stations have not yet fully priced in the coming MRT connectivity. That creates a short window where tenants can lock in leases at comparatively lower rates before the trains start running.

Historically in Singapore, rents around new MRT stations rise noticeably once the lines open and landlords understand the premium they can charge. The same pattern has played out around past MRT openings along the Downtown Line and the Thomson-East Coast Line.

If you are currently house-hunting as a tenant and one of the new stations is near where you want to live, it is worth moving quickly rather than waiting. Signing a lease now, or locking in a longer tenancy at current rates, could mean you pay today's prices for another year or two rather than the higher rents that are likely to follow once the stations open.

环线延伸段预计将于2026年开通多个新站，分析师指出，这些新站附近的租金尚未完全反映即将到来的MRT连通优势。这为租客创造了一个短暂的窗口期，可在列车正式通车前，以相对较低的租金锁定租约。

从新加坡的历史来看，每当新MRT站开通，周边租金都会在通车后明显上涨，因为房东意识到自己可以收取更高的溢价。同样的规律，在过去滨海市区线和汤申-东海岸线开通时也一再上演。

如果你目前正以租客身份物色住所，而其中一个新站恰好在你心仪的居住范围内，建议尽快行动，不要观望。现在签下租约，或以当前租金锁定较长的租期，未来一两年内就能继续以今天的价格租房，而不是等到通车后再以更高的租金入市。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are renting, locking in a lease near one of the new stations before they open could save you money versus waiting until landlords price in the MRT premium.如果你正在租房，在新站开通前锁定附近的租约，可能比等到房东将MRT溢价纳入租金后才租，更能省下一笔钱。
