Private Home Prices Up 0.5% in Q2 While HDB Resale Prices Slip 0.3%: Flash Estimates闪估数据：Q2私宅价格涨0.5%，HDB转售价格跌0.3%

+0.5% private home price growth Q2私宅价格Q2涨幅

Flash estimates from the government show private home prices rose 0.5% in Q2 2026, while HDB resale prices fell 0.3% over the same period. These are preliminary figures that will be revised when the full data is released, so they point to a direction rather than a precise final reading.

The two markets moving in different directions is a pattern that has been building for some time. Private condos are holding firm or edging up, while the HDB resale market continues to soften gradually. The gap between the two affects the upgrade path for buyers trying to move from public to private housing, making that step a little harder each quarter.

For private condo buyers, the slight price increase means you have marginally less bargaining room than you did six months ago, though the market is not hot enough to eliminate negotiation entirely. For HDB resale sellers, a softening market means buyers have options and are not in a hurry. Overpricing a flat in this environment means it will sit, and a listing that sits too long usually ends up selling for less than a well-priced one would have from the start.

政府闪估数据显示，2026年第二季度私宅价格上涨0.5%，同期HDB转售价格则下跌0.3%。这些是初步数字，待完整数据发布后会有所修订，因此目前只能反映趋势方向，而非精确的最终读数。

两个市场朝相反方向发展，这一格局已持续了一段时间。私宅价格企稳或小幅上扬，而HDB转售市场则持续温和走软。两者之间的差距，影响着那些希望从公共住房升级到私宅的买家，令这一步每一季度都变得更加难以迈出。

对于私宅买家来说，价格小幅上涨意味着你的议价空间比六个月前稍微收窄了一点，不过市场还没热到完全没有谈判余地的程度。对于HDB转售卖家来说，市场走软意味着买家有足够的选择，并不急于出手。在这种环境下定价过高，组屋就会挂着卖不动，而一个挂牌太久的单位，最终往往比一开始就定价合理的单位卖得更便宜。
