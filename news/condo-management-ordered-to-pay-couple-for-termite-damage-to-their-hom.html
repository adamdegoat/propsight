Condo Management Ordered to Pay Couple for Termite Damage to Their Home法庭裁定condo管理机构须赔偿白蚁损毁住户单位

A couple living at Freesia Woods condominium took their management corporation to the Strata Titles Boards after termites spreading from the building's common areas damaged their unit. The tribunal ruled in their favour and ordered the management corporation to pay compensation. The central finding was that keeping common areas free of pests is the management body's responsibility, not something individual owners need to absorb on their own.

This kind of dispute is more common than many condo owners realise. Termites and other pests often nest in landscaping, mature trees or structural elements in common areas before spreading into individual units. Owners who suffer damage are sometimes told by management that it is their own problem to resolve, but this ruling directly contradicts that position.

If your unit has been damaged by pests that originated in a common area, you now have a clearer legal basis to claim compensation from your management corporation. Document the damage thoroughly with photos, get a pest control professional to confirm the source in writing, and put your complaint formally to the management council before escalating to the tribunal if needed.

居住在Freesia Woods condo的一对夫妇，因白蚁从大厦公共区域扩散至其单位并造成损毁，遂将管理机构告上地契分层委员会。委员会裁定夫妇二人胜诉，并命令管理机构支付赔偿金。裁决的核心认定是：保持公共区域免受害虫侵扰是管理机构的责任，而非个别业主需要自行承担的事宜。

这类纠纷比许多condo业主所意识到的更为普遍。白蚁和其他害虫往往先在公共区域的绿化植栽、成熟树木或结构构件中筑巢，然后才扩散到个别单位。遭受损失的业主有时会被管理机构告知这是他们自己的问题，但这项裁决直接推翻了这种说法。

如果你的单位因源自公共区域的害虫而遭受损毁，你现在有了更清晰的法律依据，可以向管理机构索偿。务必拍照详细记录损毁情况，请专业的害虫防治公司以书面形式确认害虫来源，并正式向管理机构提出书面投诉，如仍未获解决，再提报地契分层委员会处理。
