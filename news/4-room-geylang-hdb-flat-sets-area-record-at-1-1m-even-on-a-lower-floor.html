4-Room Geylang HDB Flat Sets Area Record at $1.1M, Even on a Lower Floor芽笼4房式HDB组屋低楼层也能卖$1.1M，创区域最高纪录

$1.1M Geylang 4-room resale record芽笼4房式转售纪录

A 4-room HDB flat in Geylang has changed hands for $1.1 million, setting a new price record for the area. What makes this deal notable is that the flat is on a lower floor, not a high-level unit with panoramic views that might justify a premium.

Geylang is not an estate that traditionally commands top-tier prices. It is a well-connected, lived-in neighbourhood, but it does not carry the same prestige as Bishan, Queenstown or Toa Payoh. A lower-floor flat there crossing the million-dollar mark tells you how widely the surge in HDB resale prices has spread, well beyond the usual headline estates.

For buyers in Geylang and similar areas, this is a reality check on budgeting. Relying on what flats sold for two or three years ago will leave you underprepared. For sellers, it is worth finding out what your flat is actually worth right now. You may be sitting on considerably more value than you realise.

芽笼不是传统上以高价著称的组屋区。这里交通方便，生活气息浓厚，但名气和地位远不及碧山、女皇镇或大巴窑。然而，一个低楼层单位在这里也能突破百万元，足以说明HDB转售价格的涨势已经蔓延得多广，早已超出那几个惯常上头条的热门地区。

对于有意在芽笼或类似地区置业的买家来说，这是一个实实在在的预算警醒。若还在参考两三年前的成交价，只会让自己措手不及。对于卖家而言，现在不妨认真了解一下自己的组屋目前究竟值多少钱，你手中的房产，价值可能比你想象的高出不少。
