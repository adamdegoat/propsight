Turf City's First Condo Starts at $1.475M, But 3-Bedders Cost $1.1M More马场城首个Condo起价$1.475M，但三房式单位贵了约$1.1M

$1.475M starting price at Dunearn HouseDunearn House起价

Dunearn House, the first private condominium to launch from the long-awaited Bukit Timah Turf City redevelopment, has started previewing. Entry prices begin at $1.475 million, but that only gets you into the smaller units.

A three-bedroom unit will cost roughly $1.1 million more on top of that entry price, putting family-sized homes at well above $2.5 million. That is a steep ask for a brand-new address that has not yet been built out or proven as a functioning neighbourhood.

If you are a single buyer or a couple comfortable with a one or two-bedroom unit, the entry price is not unreasonable for the Bukit Timah corridor. But families counting on a proper three-bedroom home here need to go in with a clear-eyed view of the total budget required. If $2.5 million-plus is a stretch, this project is probably not for you.

Dunearn House是备受期待的武吉知马马场城重建计划中首个推出的私人condo，目前已开始预览。入门价从$1.475M起，但这只能让你买到较小的单位。

三房式单位的价格在入门价基础上再高出约$1.1M，也就是说，适合家庭居住的单位售价远超$2.5M。对于一个尚未建好、周边配套也还未成熟的新地址来说，这个价格着实不低。

如果你是单身买家或夫妻二人，可以接受一房或二房式单位，以武吉知马这一带的地段来说，入门价尚在情理之中。但若一家人需要一个真正的三房式单位，就必须对所需的总预算有清醒的认识。如果$2.5M以上对你来说有压力，这个项目大概不适合你。
