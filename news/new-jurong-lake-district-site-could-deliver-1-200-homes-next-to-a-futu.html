New Jurong Lake District Site Could Deliver 1,200 Homes Next to a Future 4-Line MRT Interchange裕廊湖区新地段将建1,200套住宅，毗邻未来四线MRT interchange

1,200 homes planned at JLD site裕廊湖区地段计划住宅数量

URA has launched a mixed-use land parcel at Town Hall Link in Jurong Lake District for public tender. The site is planned for up to 1,200 homes and sits next to a future MRT interchange connecting four train lines, which would make it one of the most accessible new addresses in the west of Singapore.

Jurong Lake District has been in the government's development plans for many years, intended to become Singapore's second major business and lifestyle hub outside the city centre. The area is still being built out, and any homes on this new site will take years to complete. Buyers should calibrate their expectations around a long time horizon.

For anyone interested in the Jurong area, more supply is coming. That is a positive for long-term liveability and connectivity as amenities build up around the hub. But it also means more competing units when you eventually want to sell or rent out, so the investment case is not automatic. Buying early in a developing precinct carries real upside but also real uncertainty about how quickly the surroundings actually deliver.

URA已将裕廊湖区Town Hall Link的一块混合用途地段推出公开招标。该地段计划建设多达1,200套住宅，紧邻一个未来将连接四条地铁线的MRT interchange，届时将成为新加坡西部地区交通最便利的新住址之一。

裕廊湖区多年来一直在政府的发展规划之中，旨在发展成为新加坡市区以外的第二个主要商业与生活中心。整个区域仍在持续建设中，这个新地段上的住宅也需要多年才能竣工。买家应该将预期建立在较长的时间跨度上。

对于任何对裕廊地区感兴趣的人来说，未来供应量会继续增加。随着中心区周边配套逐步完善，这对长期宜居性和交通便利性来说是好消息。但这也意味着，当你日后想要出售或出租时，会面临更多竞争单位，投资回报并非理所当然。在一个正在发展的新区域早期入市，有实实在在的上升空间，但周边配套能否如期兑现，同样存在真实的不确定性。
