About Two-Thirds of Maju Forest Will Be Cleared for New HDB Estate in Clementi克里门蒂Maju森林约三分之二将被清除以兴建新HDB住宅区

2/3 of Maju Forest to be cleared将被清除的Maju森林比例

About two-thirds of Maju Forest in Clementi will be cleared to make way for a new public housing estate. The remaining section, including some trees and nature corridors, will be preserved as part of the development.

Maju Forest is a patch of secondary forest that has grown up over the years between existing residential areas in the west. Clearing the bulk of it for housing is the kind of trade-off Singapore routinely makes given its land constraints, though it will draw pushback from residents and nature groups who have come to see the area as a meaningful urban green space.

For buyers interested in the west, a new HDB estate in Clementi means fresh supply coming to a popular and well-connected location near the National University of Singapore and one-north. It is worth tracking BTO launch announcements for this site if you want to get in at balloted prices rather than paying resale premiums once the estate is built and demand builds up around it.

Maju森林是一片次生林，多年来在西部现有住宅区之间自然生长起来。鉴于新加坡的土地限制，将大部分森林用于建房是这座城市惯常的取舍，但这也会引来居民和自然团体的反对声音，他们早已将这里视为珍贵的城市绿色空间。
