Private home prices edge up 0.5% in Q2 2026 while HDB resale prices dip 0.3%2026年第二季度私宅价格微涨0.5%，HDB转售价格小跌0.3%

+0.5% private home price rise Q2 20262026年第二季度私宅价格涨幅

The latest official flash estimates show private residential prices in Singapore edged up 0.5% in the second quarter of 2026. At the same time, HDB resale prices dipped 0.3%, extending a softening trend that has been building for several quarters. The two markets are now clearly tracking in opposite directions.

The private market's small gain shows that demand for condos and landed homes is holding up, even if growth is modest and nowhere near the pace seen in 2021 and 2022. Buyers are still willing to pay a little more, but they are not in a rush and they have more options. The HDB dip reflects a market where the frenzy has cooled and sellers no longer have the upper hand they once did.

In practical terms: if you are selling an HDB flat, you need to price carefully based on what comparable units have actually transacted at recently, not what you hoped for six months ago. If you are buying a private condo, the market is still slowly moving against you, so indefinite waiting is not a risk-free strategy, but there is also no reason to panic-buy. In both segments, the buyers who do their homework on recent transaction data are going to make better decisions than those who rely on gut feel or headline numbers.

最新官方预估数据显示，新加坡私人住宅价格在2026年第二季度微涨0.5%。与此同时，HDB转售价格下跌0.3%，延续了连续数季以来持续积累的下行趋势。两个市场目前明显朝相反方向运行。

私人市场的小幅上涨显示，condo和有地住宅的需求依然支撑，尽管增长幅度温和，远不及2021年和2022年的势头。买家仍愿意多付一点，但并不急于出手，选择也更多了。HDB的下跌则反映出一个热潮已退的市场，卖家不再像以前那样占据主动。

从实际层面而言：如果你正在出售HDB组屋，需要根据近期可比单位的实际成交价来仔细定价，而非六个月前的预期。如果你正在考虑购买私人condo，市场仍在缓慢地对你不利，无限期等待并非毫无风险的策略，但也没有理由恐慌性买入。在两个市场中，做足近期成交数据功课的买家，都将比那些依靠直觉或头条数字做决定的人作出更明智的选择。
