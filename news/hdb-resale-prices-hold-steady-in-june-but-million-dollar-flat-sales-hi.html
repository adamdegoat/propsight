HDB Resale Prices Hold Steady in June but Million-Dollar Flat Sales Hit New HighHDB二手房6月价格基本持平，百万元成交组屋数量创新高

June 2026 HDB resale data shows the overall market holding roughly flat, with prices across most flat types not moving significantly. But buried in that stable picture is a striking outlier: the number of resale flats selling at one million dollars or more has hit a fresh all-time high in a single month.

The million-dollar threshold used to feel exceptional. It is now a recurring headline. The record count of such deals points to buyers in the premium segment, typically chasing large or well-located flats in popular mature estates, who are still competing hard and willing to pay whatever it takes.

For most HDB sellers and buyers, the market is calm right now and there is no great urgency on either side. But if your target is a big flat in a sought-after estate, you are effectively shopping in a different market. Expect aggressive competition and asking prices that have little to do with what the broader HDB market is doing.

2026年6月HDB二手房数据显示，整体市场基本持平，大多数房型的价格没有明显变动。但在这一平稳格局背后，有一个引人注目的例外：单月成交价达一百万元或以上的二手组屋数量，刷新了历史最高纪录。

百万元这个门槛曾经让人觉得遥不可及，如今已成为常见新闻。创纪录的成交数量说明，高端市场的买家，通常是在热门成熟市镇追寻大型或地段优越组屋的人，依然竞争激烈，愿意出任何价钱。

对大多数HDB买卖双方而言，现在市场平静，双方都不急着出手。但如果你的目标是热门市镇的大型组屋，你实际上是在一个不同的市场里购物。要做好面对激烈竞争的准备，那里的开价与整体HDB市场的走势几乎没什么关系。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Most HDB resale prices are not moving much right now, but if you are eyeing a large or well-located flat, expect stiff competition and record-level asking prices at the top end.目前大多数HDB二手房价格没什么动静，但如果你看中的是面积大或地段好的组屋，就要做好面对激烈竞争和创纪录高价的心理准备。
