Are Progressive Payment Schemes Pushing New Condo Prices Higher?分期付款机制，是否推高了新condo的价格？

Business Times has examined whether the progressive payment scheme for new condo launches is one structural reason why new launch prices stay elevated. Under this scheme, buyers pay in stages as construction advances rather than settling the full purchase price upfront, which lowers the short-term financial pressure on buyers.

The argument is that staggered payments make it easier for buyers to absorb high headline prices because the pain is spread out over years. That in turn reduces the pressure on developers to price competitively at launch, since buyers are not feeling the full weight of the price at one moment. The structure may quietly allow prices to stay higher than they otherwise would.

For anyone weighing a new launch against a completed resale unit in the same area, this context is worth sitting with. New condos carry a premium, and part of that premium may be structural rather than reflecting something genuinely better about the unit. Before signing, compare a resale option nearby on a like-for-like basis. You may find the gap is harder to justify than it first appears.

《商业时报》近日探讨了新condo的分期付款计划是否是新盘价格居高不下的结构性原因之一。在这一安排下，买家按工程进度分阶段付款，无需一口气付清全额，短期资金压力因此大为减轻。

有一种看法认为：分期付款让买家更容易接受高价，因为负担被摊分到好几年。这样一来，发展商在新盘定价时少了竞价压力，买家在任何一个时间点感受到的，只是部分款项，而不是全额的重量。这种机制，或许就在不知不觉间，让价格得以维持在比正常情况更高的水平。

如果你正在考虑是买新盘，还是同区域的现成转售单位，这一点很值得细想。新condo本来就有溢价，而其中有一部分可能只是结构性的，并非真的因为单位本身有什么过人之处。签约之前，不妨按同等条件认真比较一下附近的转售选项，你可能会发现，那个差价其实没那么好解释。
