BCA Finds Boutique Condos More Prone to Construction Quality DefectsBCA发现小型精品condo更容易出现建筑质量缺陷

The Building and Construction Authority has found that smaller boutique condo developments are more likely to have construction defects than larger projects, based on inspections of completed buildings. The finding suggests that build quality is not uniform across the new condo market.

Smaller projects may have less structured quality control, fewer repeat specialist contractors, or tighter development budgets, any of which can affect what you end up with when you collect your keys. Larger projects tend to have more oversight baked into the process simply because of the scale of the work involved.

If you have bought or are buying a boutique condo, do not treat the defect inspection as a formality. Go through the unit carefully, and consider hiring a professional inspector if you are not confident doing it yourself. Getting defects documented before handover is the cleanest way to make sure the developer fixes them at their cost, not yours.

建筑与建设局（BCA）检查已竣工建筑后发现，规模较小的精品condo项目比大型项目更容易出现建筑质量缺陷。这说明，新condo市场的建筑质量并不整齐划一。

小型项目可能质量管控不够规范，惯用的专业分包商也较少，或者开发预算比较紧，任何一个因素都可能影响你最终收到手的单位质量。大型项目因为规模大，整个施工过程自然会有更多监督到位。

如果你已经买了或正在考虑买精品condo，千万别把缺陷检查当走过场。要认认真真地检查单位，如果自己没有把握，可以考虑请专业验屋师来帮忙。在交屋前把所有缺陷都记录在案，是确保由发展商负责修缮、而不是让你自掏腰包的最妥当做法。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are buying a new or recently completed boutique condo, set aside time for a thorough defect check before you accept your keys.如果你买的是新竣工或刚完工的精品condo，收钥匙之前记得预留时间做彻底的缺陷检查。
