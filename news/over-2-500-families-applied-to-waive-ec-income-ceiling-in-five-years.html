Over 2,500 Families Applied to Waive EC Income Ceiling in Five Years五年内超过2,500个家庭申请豁免EC收入顶限

845 EC income ceiling appeals approvedEC收入顶限豁免申请获批数

Between 2020 and 2025, more than 2,500 households wrote to HDB asking for permission to buy an Executive Condominium despite earning above the income ceiling. HDB approved 845 of those appeals, which works out to roughly one in three applicants getting the green light.

The EC income ceiling currently sits at $16,000 a month for most buyers. It exists to keep ECs within reach of middle-income families, but household incomes shift due to bonuses, job changes or a spouse returning to work. The appeal process gives households a formal channel to put their case directly to HDB.

If your combined income has pushed you just over the ceiling, do not assume you are automatically locked out. A one-in-three approval rate is meaningful enough to make the effort worthwhile. The key is having a genuine reason behind the application, such as a temporary income spike that does not reflect your usual earning level, and making sure your paperwork makes that case clearly.

2020年至2025年间，超过2,500个家庭致函HDB，申请在收入超过顶限的情况下购买Executive Condominium。HDB共批准了845份申请，换算下来大约每三个申请者就有一个获得批准。

EC的收入顶限目前为每月$16,000，适用于大多数买家。这个限制是为了让EC维持在中等收入家庭的可负担范围内，但家庭收入往往会因为花红、换工作或配偶重返职场而有所变动。申请程序让家庭有一个正式渠道，直接向HDB陈述情况。

如果你的家庭合并收入刚刚超过顶限，不要以为自己就被拒于门外了。三分之一的批准率已经相当值得一试。关键是要有真实的理由支撑申请，例如收入一时偏高、并不反映平常的收入水平，同时确保你的文件能清楚说明这一点。
