Old Tiong Bahru Condo Averaged $306K Profit Per 2-Bedroom Resale, Beating Nearby Areas中峇鲁旧condo两房转售平均获利$306K，跑赢周边区域

$306K average profit per 2-bedder resale两房转售平均获利

Resale data shows that an older condo in Tiong Bahru outperformed developments in nearby Bukit Merah and Queenstown for 2-bedroom returns, averaging $306,000 profit per transaction. Consistent buyer demand and the estate's enduring appeal drove the strong numbers.

Tiong Bahru has built a loyal following over the years thanks to its distinct character, its food scene, and its easy reach of the city. Buyers who want to live there tend to be committed to the estate specifically, and that kind of targeted demand supports prices even when the building itself is no longer new. That staying power does not always show up when you look at an estate's headline figures.

The lesson for buyers and investors is that age alone is a poor guide to value. An older condo in a neighbourhood with genuine lifestyle appeal and limited new supply can quietly beat newer developments elsewhere. Before writing off a project because of its age, look at what units there have actually sold for and what owners walked away with.

转售数据显示，中峇鲁一个较旧的condo在两房回报方面，超越了附近红山和女皇镇的楼盘，每宗交易平均获利$306,000。稳定的买家需求加上这片社区经久不衰的吸引力，共同成就了这份亮眼的成绩。

中峇鲁多年来积累了一批忠实拥趸，靠的是它独特的风貌、丰富的美食文化，以及方便进城的交通。想住在这里的买家，往往是冲着这片社区本身而来，这种精准的买家需求，即便楼盘不再新了，也依然能撑住房价。这种韧性，光看一个社区的整体数据，往往是看不出来的。

对买家和投资者来说，这里有个值得记住的道理：楼龄本身不是判断价值的好指标。在生活配套扎实、新供应有限的社区，旧condo完全可以悄悄跑赢其他地方的新楼盘。在因为楼龄而否定一个项目之前，不妨先看看那里的单位实际卖了多少，以及业主离场时到底落袋了多少。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
An older condo in a well-loved neighbourhood can still deliver strong resale profits, so do not write off ageing developments purely on age when weighing investment potential.在深受喜爱的社区，旧condo照样能带来可观的转售利润，评估投资潜力时，不要单凭楼龄就否定一个项目。
