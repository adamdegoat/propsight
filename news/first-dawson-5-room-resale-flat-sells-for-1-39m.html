First Dawson 5-Room Resale Flat Sells for $1.39MDawson首个五房式二手组屋以$1.39M易手

$1.39M first Dawson 5-room resale price首个Dawson五房式二手成交价

The first recorded resale transaction for a five-room flat in the Dawson estate in Queenstown has come in at $1.39 million. It is a landmark number for a development that has consistently been held up as one of the better-planned HDB projects in the country.

Dawson flats were built to a higher design standard and sit inside Queenstown, one of Singapore's most centrally located and convenient towns. Good connectivity, established amenities and proximity to schools and private housing have always pointed toward strong prices once these flats cleared their minimum occupation period. That prediction has now shown up in the transaction data.

For existing Dawson owners with five-room flats, this deal effectively sets the floor for what future buyers can expect to pay and it is a solid floor. For buyers eyeing the Queenstown resale market, $1.39 million is now the reference point for this flat type in Dawson. Factor that into your planning if the estate is on your shortlist.

女皇镇Dawson住宅区首宗五房式组屋二手交易以$1.39 million成交，为这个长期被视为全国规划最出色HDB项目之一的住宅区立下一个里程碑式的价格。

Dawson组屋以更高的设计标准建造，坐落在新加坡交通最便利、生活最方便的市镇之一，女皇镇。便捷的交通、完善的配套设施，加上靠近学校和私人住宅区，早已预示着这批组屋在完成最低居住期后价格会相当强劲。如今这个预测已经在交易数据中得到印证。

对于现有Dawson五房式组屋的业主而言，这笔交易实际上为未来买家设定了底价，而且是一个相当扎实的底价。对于有意在女皇镇二手市场置业的买家，$1.39 million现在是Dawson此类户型的参考定价。如果这个住宅区在你的考虑名单上，记得将这个数字纳入规划。
