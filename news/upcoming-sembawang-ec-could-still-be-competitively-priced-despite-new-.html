Upcoming Sembawang EC could still be competitively priced despite new 10-year ownership rule三巴旺即将推出的EC项目，定价或仍具竞争力——尽管持有年限延长至10年

10 years new ec minimum occupation periodEC最低自住年限（新规）

Executive condos in Singapore now come with a longer lock-in period. Buyers must hold for 10 years before they can sell on the open market, up from the previous 5. That is a significant change, and it has understandably made some potential buyers hesitate.

The upcoming EC in Sembawang may soften that blow through pricing. Because the longer wait makes the product less attractive to short-term investors, developers are expected to launch at a more modest price point to move units. The less you pay at entry, the less the extended restriction actually costs you in practice.

For genuine owner-occupiers who plan to stay for a decade anyway, the rule change is largely academic. The real risk is for buyers who were counting on a quick exit after five years. If that was part of your plan, you need to rethink it and evaluate Sembawang on its own merits rather than as a flip.

新加坡的EC现在附带更长的禁售期。买家须持有满10年才能在公开市场出售，此前的规定是5年。这是一个很大的改变，自然让一些潜在买家打了退堂鼓。

三巴旺即将推出的这个EC，可能会在定价上弥补这一点。由于更长的等待期降低了短线投资者的兴趣，发展商预计会以比较适中的价格推盘来促进销售。你买入时付的价格越低，这个延长限制对你实际造成的影响就越小。

对于本来就打算住满十年的自住买家来说，这项规定的改变几乎没有实质影响。真正需要重新盘算的，是那些原本指望五年后快速脱手的买家。如果这曾经是你计划的一部分，现在要重新思考，根据三巴旺这个项目本身的价值来判断，而不是把它当成一个短期套利的工具。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are looking at ECs as a more affordable step between HDB and private property, the Sembawang project may still make sense even though you will be locked in longer.如果你在考虑以EC作为介于HDB与私宅之间的置业选择，即便持有年限更长，三巴旺这个项目或许还是值得看的。
