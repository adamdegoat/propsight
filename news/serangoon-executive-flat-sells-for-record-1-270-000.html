Serangoon Executive Flat Sells For Record $1,270,000实龙岗执行型组屋以$1,270,000创历史新高

$1.27M serangoon exec flat record实龙岗执行型组屋纪录

An executive flat in Serangoon has just changed hands for $1,270,000, nudging past the previous record for the town by the slimmest of margins. Executive flats are the largest type of flat HDB builds, typically running between 140 and 160 square metres, so they attract buyers who want space and are prepared to pay for it.

This sale stands out because it comes at a time when the broader HDB resale market has been cooling. Average prices have eased over the past couple of quarters, yet the biggest and best-located flats keep finding buyers willing to go to record-breaking levels.

If you own a large HDB flat in a mature estate like Serangoon, this is a reminder that demand at the top end of the market is still very real. Buyers are out there and they are prepared to pay. Price it right and you may find more competition than the broader market mood suggests.

实龙岗一套执行型组屋刚刚以$1,270,000易手，以极微弱的差距超越该市镇此前的成交最高纪录。执行型组屋是HDB兴建的最大型组屋，面积通常在140至160平方米之间，因此吸引的是那些需要宽裕空间、愿意为此付出代价的买家。

这笔交易之所以引人注目，正是因为它发生在整体HDB转售市场趋于冷静的时期。过去几个季度，平均价格有所回落，然而那些面积最大、地段最优的组屋，依然能找到愿意以破纪录价格接盘的买家。

如果你在实龙岗这类成熟市镇拥有一套大型HDB组屋，这个消息提醒你：市场顶端的需求依然十分真实。买家就在那里，而且他们愿意付出代价。只要定价合理，你会发现竞争比整体市场气氛所暗示的还要激烈。
