Boutique Condos More Prone to Construction Defects, BCA FindsBCA发现：精品小型condo更容易出现建筑质量问题

The Building and Construction Authority has found that smaller boutique condo developments have a higher rate of construction defects compared to larger projects. The finding comes from BCA quality audits and identifies a pattern that buyers in smaller projects should take seriously when handover day comes around.

Larger developments benefit from scale in quality control. A big project justifies dedicated site supervisors, more rigorous internal inspections, and a developer whose reputation depends on thousands of buyers walking away satisfied. Boutique projects with smaller teams and fewer units do not always have the same depth of oversight baked in at every stage of construction.

The types of defects typically flagged in these audits include cracks, water seepage, finishing problems, and alignment issues. Most are fixable, and under the Defects Liability Period the developer is legally required to rectify faults at their own cost for a set period after handover. But getting things fixed can drag on if the developer is slow to respond.

If you are buying a boutique condo, treat the handover inspection seriously. Bring a checklist and, if possible, a professional inspector. Document every defect you find in writing before you sign anything. Your legal right to have things fixed is only as useful as the paper trail you create at the start, so do not leave it to memory or a verbal agreement.

建设局（BCA）发现，规模较小的精品condo项目与大型项目相比，建筑缺陷的发生率更高。这一发现来自BCA的质量审计，揭示了一个在小型项目购房的买家在收楼当天应认真对待的规律。

大型项目在质量管控上受益于规模效应。一个大型项目有理由配备专职工地监督员、更严格的内部检验，以及一个声誉依赖于数千名买家满意而来的发展商。规模较小、单位较少的精品项目，未必在建设的每个阶段都具备同等深度的监督机制。

这类审计中通常标记出的缺陷包括裂缝、渗水、装修瑕疵和对准问题。大多数是可以修复的，而根据缺陷责任期（Defects Liability Period）的规定，发展商在交楼后的指定期限内须自费修缮缺陷。但如果发展商迟迟不响应，修缮工作可能会拖延许久。

如果你正在购买精品condo，请认真对待收楼检验。带着清单去，如果可能，请专业验房师同行。在签署任何文件之前，以书面形式记录所有发现的缺陷。你依法享有的修缮权利，只有在你从一开始就建立起完整书面记录的前提下才真正有用，不要仅凭记忆或口头协议来处理。
