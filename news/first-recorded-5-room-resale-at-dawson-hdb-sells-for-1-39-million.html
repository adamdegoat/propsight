First Recorded 5-Room Resale at Dawson HDB Sells for $1.39 MillionDawson HDB首个五房式转售单位易手，售价$1.39 million

$1.39M dawson 5-room resale priceDawson五房式转售价

A five-room flat at Dawson in Queenstown has sold for $1.39 million, the first time a resale unit in that estate has changed hands on the open market. Dawson is a relatively young estate, built as replacement housing for residents who were displaced when older blocks in the area were torn down for redevelopment.

Because the flats are newer and sit in a mature, well-connected town, they have always attracted interest. But $1.39 million for a five-room HDB flat is still a significant milestone for Dawson, putting it firmly in the same bracket as some of the pricier HDB addresses in Singapore.

For the broader market, this is one more sign that million-dollar resale flats are no longer confined to a handful of city-fringe locations. Strong prices are spreading into estates that were not traditionally associated with top-of-market HDB transactions, and Dawson is now on that list.

女皇镇Dawson的一个五房式单位以$1.39 million成交，这是该屋苑首次有转售单位在公开市场易手。Dawson是一个建成时间不长的组屋区，当年是为安置旧楼被拆除重建时须迁出的居民而兴建的。

由于这批单位较新，又位于交通便利的成熟市镇，一直颇受买家关注。不过，一个五房式HDB单位卖到$1.39 million，对Dawson而言仍是一个重要里程碑，令它正式跻身新加坡价格较高的HDB地址之列。

对整体市场来说，这又是一个信号，百万元转售组屋已不再局限于少数靠近市区边缘的地点。高价正在向那些传统上与顶价HDB交易无缘的屋苑蔓延，Dawson如今也跻身其中。
