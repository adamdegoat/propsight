Serangoon Executive Flat Sets New Area Record at $1.27M实龙岗执行式单位以$1.27M刷新该区成交纪录

$1.27M record serangoon exec flat实龙岗执行式单位纪录

A Serangoon executive HDB flat has just sold for $1,270,000, clearing the previous area record by the narrowest of margins. Executive flats are among the largest flat types in the HDB system, and in mature towns like Serangoon they have been quietly reaching new highs over the past couple of years.

The appeal is not hard to understand. Executive flats offer living space that is closer to a smaller private condo than a typical HDB unit. Pair that with a mature estate that has established amenities, transport links, and schools, and you have a combination that a specific type of buyer will pay top dollar for.

For sellers of similar flats in Serangoon or nearby estates in the same band of maturity, this transaction is a useful reference point. The market for large, well-located HDB flats has not stalled out by this measure. If your flat is in good condition and has the right characteristics, you can reasonably test the upper end of the range.

Buyers should factor this into their planning. The $1 million mark for HDB flats has become routine in many mature estates, and executive units are increasingly pushing well past that. Before committing, it is worth comparing against older resale condos in the same area to make sure the value equation holds for your specific situation.

实龙岗一个执行式HDB单位刚以$1,270,000成交，以极微小的差距超越该区前一纪录。执行式单位是HDB系统中面积最大的单位类型之一，在实龙岗这类成熟市镇，过去两年来一直悄悄屡创新高。

这种吸引力不难理解。执行式单位提供的居住空间，比一般HDB单位更接近较小型的私人condo。再加上成熟市镇完善的配套设施、交通连接和学校，造就了一种特定类型买家愿意出高价的组合。

对于实龙岗或附近同等成熟市镇拥有类似单位的卖家，这宗交易是一个有用的参考点。以这个成交价来看，大面积、地段优越的HDB单位市场还没有降温。如果你的单位状况良好且具备合适条件，合理地探一探市场高位，是说得过去的。

买家也应将这一点纳入预算规划。在许多成熟市镇，HDB单位突破$1 million已成常态，而执行式单位越来越多地远超这个价位。在下决定之前，值得与同一地区较旧的二手condo作比较，确认价值方程式对你的具体情况确实成立。
