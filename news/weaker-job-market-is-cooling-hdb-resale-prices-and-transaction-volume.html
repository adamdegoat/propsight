Weaker Job Market Is Cooling HDB Resale Prices and Transaction Volume就业市场走弱令HDB二手房价格和成交量双双降温

Analysts tracking Q2 2026 HDB resale figures are pointing to a softening job market as a central reason behind the continued decline in both prices and transaction volumes. When people feel less certain about their employment and income, they pull back from large financial commitments, and buying a home is about as large as it gets.

HDB resale has now posted two consecutive quarters of price decline. This is not a sharp fall, but the direction is consistent. Hiring sentiment in Singapore is currently not providing the confidence that typically pushes buyers to commit quickly and pay over the odds for a flat they want.

If you are selling your HDB flat in this environment, do not price it as though it is still a sellers' market. Buyers have more time to look around and less urgency to close. A realistic asking price and genuine willingness to negotiate will serve you better than holding out for a number the market is not currently prepared to meet. Build a longer selling timeline into your plans from the start.

追踪2026年第二季度HDB二手数据的分析师，将就业市场走软列为价格和成交量持续下滑的核心原因。当人们对自己的工作和收入感到不那么确定时，他们会在重大财务承诺上退缩，而买房正是其中最大的一项。

HDB二手市场已连续两个季度录得价格下跌。跌幅并不剧烈，但方向一致。目前新加坡的招聘市场情绪，没有为买家提供那种通常会让人迅速出手、不惜高价争抢心仪组屋的信心。

如果你在这种市场环境下出售HDB组屋，不要按卖方市场的标准来定价。买家有更多时间货比三家，也没有急着成交的迫切感。实事求是地定价、真诚地愿意议价，会比死守市场目前无法接受的价格更有效。从一开始就把更长的销售周期纳入你的计划。
