First Resale 3-Room Flat at Newly Eligible Tampines Project Sells for $628,000淡滨尼新晋合格项目首个三房式转售组屋以$628,000成交

$628,000 first 3-room resale tampines淡滨尼首个三房式转售价

The first resale transaction at a Tampines HDB project that has just completed its five-year minimum occupation period came in at $628,000 for a three-room flat. Flats that have just become eligible for resale are watched closely because they represent the first real signal of what the open market will pay in that specific development.

Newly eligible flats tend to attract buyers who want a unit they can move into right away, without waiting years for a new BTO build to complete. That ready-to-move-in premium, combined with the fact that these are relatively newer flats compared to older resale stock in the neighbourhood, often pushes prices slightly above the surrounding area average.

If you own a three-room flat in this Tampines block and are weighing whether to sell now, the $628,000 transaction is your opening reference point. Your floor level, flat orientation and the condition of your renovation will determine whether you can realistically price above or below that figure.
