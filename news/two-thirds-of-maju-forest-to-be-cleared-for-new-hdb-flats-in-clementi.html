Two-thirds of Maju Forest to be cleared for new HDB flats in Clementi约三分之二的马朱森林将被清除，为金文泰新组屋让地

2/3 of maju forest to be cleared马朱森林将被清除的比例

About two-thirds of Maju Forest near Clementi will be cleared to make way for new HDB flats. A portion of the forest and some green corridors will be preserved within what becomes the new estate.

Clementi is an established, in-demand town and HDB land there is scarce. The trade-off for losing most of a forest is a meaningful injection of new supply in an area where competition for flats has been stiff for years.

For buyers interested in Clementi, this is good news over the medium term. More flats will mean more options and less upward pressure on prices, though the timeline from land clearing to BTO ballot to actual keys will run several years.

金文泰附近约三分之二的马朱森林将被清除，为新组屋建设腾出空间。部分森林及绿色走廊将保留，纳入未来的新住宅区规划。

金文泰是一个成熟且备受追捧的市镇，HDB用地向来稀缺。失去大部分森林所换来的，是在这个多年来组屋竞争激烈的地区注入可观的新供应量。

对有意在金文泰置业的买家来说，这是中期内的好消息。更多的组屋意味着更多选择，也将减轻价格的上行压力，不过从清地到BTO抽签再到实际收钥匙，还需要数年时间。
