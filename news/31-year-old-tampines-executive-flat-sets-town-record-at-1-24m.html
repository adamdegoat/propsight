31-Year-Old Tampines Executive Flat Sets Town Record at $1.24M31年楼龄淡滨尼Executive组屋以$1.24M创区内成交价新纪录

$1.24M Tampines exec flat town record淡滨尼Executive组屋区内成交价新纪录

A 31-year-old Executive flat in Tampines has sold for $1.24 million, setting a new record for the town. Executive flats are the largest standard flat type in the HDB system, already a step above regular five-roomers, but seeing a three-decade-old unit cross this price point is still a significant marker.

Tampines is a well-established mature town with solid amenities, transport links and schools, which keeps demand firm even for older stock. The flat in question is about 31 years old, meaning the lease has already run down to roughly 68 years remaining. Buyers were still willing to pay record money for it, which tells you how competitive the market for larger flats in mature towns has become.

For sellers in Tampines with Executive flats, this is encouraging news and sets a new benchmark to reference. For buyers, the lesson is to think carefully about lease decay on older flats before committing at a record price. A strong transaction today can lead to a harder resale or a lower bank valuation further down the road as the remaining lease shortens.

淡滨尼一个31年楼龄的Executive组屋单位以$1.24 million成交，创下该区新纪录。Executive组屋是HDB系统内最大的标准户型，本已比普通五房式高出一个档次，但一个有三十年历史的单位能突破这个价位，依然是一个重要的市场信号。

淡滨尼是一个设施完善、交通便利、学校资源丰富的成熟市镇，这让它对旧房的需求也保持稳定。这个单位楼龄约31年，意味着剩余租约只剩大约68年。买家仍然愿意以创纪录的价格购入，充分说明成熟市镇大户型组屋的市场竞争已经到了何等激烈的程度。

对于淡滨尼拥有Executive组屋的卖家来说，这是个好消息，也树立了新的参考基准。对买家而言，这个案例提醒你在以创纪录价格入手前，要仔细考量旧房的剩余租约问题。今天一笔亮眼的成交，随着租约年限缩短，日后转售或向银行申请估价时都可能遇到更大的难度。
