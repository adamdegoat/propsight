Bayshore Drive Mixed-Use Government Land Site Goes to Frasers-Led Group at $1,323 per Square FootBayshore Drive综合用途政府地段以每平方英尺$1,323元，由Frasers领衔财团夺得

$1,323 psf bayshore gls land bid priceBayshore GLS地段出价

The government's Bayshore Drive mixed-use land parcel has been awarded to a Frasers Property-led consortium, which came in with a winning bid of $1,323 per square foot of buildable area. The site is designated for a development that combines homes with other uses, likely including retail and community facilities.

That land price is on the higher side for the Bayshore corridor, which is still being built out as a new waterfront neighbourhood east of the city. When a developer pays more for land, the gap has to be recovered through unit prices. A future condo on this site will almost certainly carry a premium to reflect that cost, and buyers should factor that in when thinking about the area.

If you are keeping an eye on Bayshore as a place to buy, this land deal gives you a concrete signal for where new launch prices are likely to land when this project eventually comes to market. It will not be cheap. That makes current resale options in the area worth comparing carefully, since they may represent better value once the new supply starts pricing in the higher land cost.
