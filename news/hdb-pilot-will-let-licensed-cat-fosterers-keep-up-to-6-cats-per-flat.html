HDB pilot will let licensed cat fosterers keep up to 6 cats per flatHDB试行计划：持牌猫咪寄养者每户最多可养6只猫

6 cats allowed per flat in pilot试行计划下每户可饲养猫咪数量

HDB has started a pilot scheme that allows residents who are licensed cat fosterers to keep up to six cats in their flat. That is a significant step up from the current rule, which limits all HDB households to one cat.

The key detail is the word licensed. This is not a change for the average cat owner who just has a couple of cats at home. To qualify, you need to be an active fosterer registered with a recognised welfare organisation, and HDB will set conditions on how the arrangement works. The scheme exists to make life easier for people who temporarily house rescued cats or cats waiting for adoption.

If you are already doing cat fostering work from an HDB flat, this is worth looking into because it removes a practical constraint that has likely created friction for you. For everyone else, the one-cat rule stands. This pilot does not change anything for the general HDB population.

HDB推出了一项试行计划，允许持牌猫咪寄养者在组屋单位内饲养多达6只猫。这比目前每户只能养1只猫的规定，迈进了一大步。

关键词是「持牌」。这不是针对在家随便养了几只猫的普通猫主人的改变。要符合资格，你必须是在认可动物福利机构注册的活跃寄养者，HDB也会对具体安排设定条件。这个计划的出发点，是为那些临时照顾获救猫咪或等待领养的猫咪的人减少不便。

如果你本来就在HDB组屋里做猫咪寄养工作，这个计划值得关注，因为它解决了一个在实际操作上给你带来不少麻烦的限制。对其他人来说，每户1只猫的规定依然不变。这个试行计划不影响一般HDB居民。
