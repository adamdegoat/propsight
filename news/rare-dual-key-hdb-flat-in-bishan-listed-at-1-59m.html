Rare Dual-Key HDB Flat in Bishan Listed at $1.59M碧山罕见双钥匙HDB组屋叫价$1.59M

$1.59M asking price for dual-key HDB flat双钥匙HDB单位叫价

A Multi-Generation flat in Bishan has been listed for sale at $1.59 million. These dual-key units come with two separate living spaces under one roof, designed so parents and adult children can share an address while still maintaining real day-to-day privacy from each other.

There are only 93 of these Multi-Generation flats in Bishan, and because HDB has not built them in significant numbers for some time, the supply is essentially fixed. That scarcity alone pushes up asking prices whenever one comes to market, on top of whatever Bishan location value already commands.

For families genuinely considering this living arrangement, the $1.59 million ask reflects the rarity of the product as much as the address. Be prepared for competition if this suits your needs. If the price feels too steep, the honest alternative is to look at buying two separate units nearby, which may or may not work out cheaper depending on what is available at the time.
