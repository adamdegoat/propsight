Rare Bishan Dual-Key HDB Flat Listed for $1.59M罕见碧山Multi-Generation HDB组屋以$1.59M挂牌求售

A Multi-Generation flat in Bishan has been listed for sale at $1.59 million. These flats are built with a separate entrance that allows a second household to live independently under the same roof, a practical arrangement for families who want to stay close to elderly parents without fully sharing a living space day to day.

There are only 93 of these units in Bishan, and they almost never appear on the open market. This is a distinct HDB flat type, not the same as the dual-key units found in private condominiums. The separate door is the defining feature, giving each generation genuine privacy within the same property.

If your family needs exactly this kind of arrangement, you are dealing with an extremely thin pool of supply. Ninety-three units in one town, with most owners holding on to them for the very reason they bought, means that when one does appear, it tends to go quickly and at a clear premium. At $1.59 million this listing reflects that scarcity. Waiting for the next available unit could mean waiting a long time.
