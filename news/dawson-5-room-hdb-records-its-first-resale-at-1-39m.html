Dawson 5-room HDB records its first resale at $1.39MDawson五房式组屋首次转售，成交价$1.39M

$1.39M first dawson 5-room resale priceDawson五房式组屋首个转售价

A five-room flat at Dawson estate in Queenstown has sold for $1.39 million, the first five-room resale recorded at that project.

Dawson units are only now starting to appear on the resale market as early buyers clear the minimum occupation period. Given the estate's location in Queenstown, its relatively modern design, and the premium reputation it has built, the pricing was always expected to land at the top end.

For sellers who bought in at BTO prices years ago, this is a healthy return. For buyers, Dawson is now firmly in the same conversation as other premium HDB estates, and the million-dollar-plus price tag is not a one-off.

女皇镇Dawson住宅区一套五房式组屋以$1.39 million成交，创下该项目五房式组屋的首个转售纪录。

Dawson的单位现在才开始陆续在转售市场上出现，因为早期买家正逐步满足最低居住年限（MOP）。鉴于该住宅区位于女皇镇的优越地段、相对现代的设计，加上其所建立的高端口碑，这样的定价水平早在预料之中。

对于当年以BTO价格购入的卖家来说，这是一个丰厚的回报。对买家而言，Dawson如今已稳稳跻身其他高端组屋住宅区的行列，百万元以上的价位绝非个案。
