Bidadari 3-room flat sells for $945,000, breaking its own record set just 8 months agoBidadari 3-room flat sells for $945,000, breaking its own record set just 8 months ago

$945,000 record 3-room flat pricerecord 3-room flat price

A 3-room HDB flat in Bidadari has sold for $945,000, breaking the previous record for that flat type in the estate. The previous record had only been set eight months ago, which means prices in Bidadari are still climbing even while the broader HDB resale market is softening.

Bidadari is a relatively new and carefully developed estate with strong connectivity and decent amenities, and buyers have been consistently willing to pay a premium for it. A 3-room flat at $945,000 is a number that would have seemed extreme for any 3-room anywhere in Singapore not long ago.

If Bidadari is on your shortlist, budget for what it actually costs now, not what you think it should cost based on the flat size or comparisons to other estates. The premium here is well established and it is not showing signs of fading. Waiting for a correction in this estate while the record keeps climbing is a risky strategy.
