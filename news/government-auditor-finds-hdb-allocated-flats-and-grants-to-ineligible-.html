Government Auditor Finds HDB Allocated Flats and Grants to Ineligible Applicants审计长办公室发现HDB曾将组屋和津贴批给不符合资格的申请者

Singapore's Auditor-General's Office reviewed HDB's processes and found that flats and housing grants had been given to applicants who did not actually meet the eligibility criteria. The findings point to gaps in how HDB was verifying whether people qualified before approving their applications.

HDB has since said it is tightening its verification procedures in response. That means the agency will be checking application details more carefully going forward, and applicants should expect greater scrutiny on the information they submit. The days of inconsistencies slipping through unchecked are likely over.

The practical takeaway for anyone applying for an HDB flat or a housing grant is straightforward: make sure every detail in your application is accurate and complete. With HDB now under pressure to get eligibility right, errors and omissions are more likely to be caught, which could mean delays, rejection, or being asked to return money that was paid out incorrectly.

新加坡审计长办公室对HDB的审核程序进行了检查，发现有组屋和购屋津贴被批给了实际上不符合资格的申请者。这些发现揭示了HDB在批准申请之前，核查申请人资格方面存在的漏洞。

HDB事后表示将加强核查程序。这意味着今后审批时会更仔细地核对申请资料，申请者应该预期自己提交的信息会受到更严格的审查。以往那种疏漏被忽视、蒙混过关的情况，很可能已经成为过去式了。

对于正在申请HDB组屋或购屋津贴的人来说，实际上要做的其实很简单：确保申请表上的每一个细节都填写准确、完整。HDB现在必须把好资格审核这一关，填错或漏填更容易被发现，轻则拖慢审批进度、申请被拒，重则被要求退还已错误发放的款项。
