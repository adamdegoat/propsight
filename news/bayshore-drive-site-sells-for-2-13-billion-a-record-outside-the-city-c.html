Bayshore Drive site sells for $2.13 billion, a record outside the city centreBayshore Drive地段以$2.13 billion成交，创市区外历史最高纪录

$2.13B bayshore gls winning bidBayshore GLS 中标价

A consortium led by Frasers Property has secured the Bayshore Drive government land sale site for $2.13 billion, the highest price ever paid for a mixed-use site outside Singapore's central business district. The scale of the winning bid signals that major developers see strong future demand for both residential and commercial space in that part of the East Coast.

To make that land cost work financially, the eventual condos and commercial units developed on the site will need to sell and lease at prices well above the current Bayshore neighbourhood average. Developers do not bid at record levels unless they are confident buyers will follow at the prices needed to generate a return.

For anyone looking at Bayshore as a place to buy, the practical takeaway is that new launches from this site will carry a significant premium to today's resale prices nearby. If you want to enter the area before that premium is baked in, the window at current resale prices may be narrower than it looks. Keep an eye on how the neighbourhood reprices as this development moves forward.

由Frasers Property牵头的财团以$2.13 billion投得Bayshore Drive政府售地，创下新加坡中央商业区以外综合用途地段的历史最高成交价。这个破纪录的出价，显示大型发展商对东海岸这一带的住宅和商业需求都抱有强烈信心。

要让这笔地价在财务上站得住脚，未来在这块地上开发的condo和商业单位，售价和租金都必须远高于目前Bayshore一带的市场水平。发展商不会轻易出创纪录的高价，除非他们有把握买家愿意跟上，让他们得以回本并获利。

对于有意在Bayshore置业的朋友来说，有一点要留意：这块地日后推出的新项目，价格预计会比目前附近的转售价高出一截。如果你想趁溢价还没完全反映进去之前入市，目前转售市场留给你的时间窗口可能比你想象的要窄。不妨密切留意这一带随着项目推进而出现的价格变化。
