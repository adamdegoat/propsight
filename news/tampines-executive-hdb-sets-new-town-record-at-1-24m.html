Tampines Executive HDB Sets New Town Record at $1.24M淡滨尼Executive HDB创全镇最高成交价，达$1.24M

$1.24M tampines town HDB record price淡滨尼镇HDB成交价记录

A 31-year-old executive flat in Tampines has sold for $1.24 million, setting a new price record for any HDB flat in the town. Executive flats are the largest units HDB has ever built, typically around 150 square metres, so they do command a premium over standard flat types.

Tampines has always been a popular mature estate, but crossing $1.24 million for a resale HDB flat is a significant milestone. To put it in perspective, that price is more than some older condominiums in less central locations are currently fetching.

For owners in Tampines, this is straightforwardly good news as it shows the resale market is at a record high. For buyers, it is a reality check: if you want a large flat in this town, you need to be prepared to spend condo-level money without getting a condo. Go in with your eyes open on what that means for your mortgage and future resale flexibility.

淡滨尼一间已有31年楼龄的executive flat以$1.24M成交，创下该镇所有HDB单位的最高成交价记录。Executive flat是HDB有史以来建造的最大单位类型，面积通常约150平方米，售价向来比其他单位类型更高。

淡滨尼一直是热门的成熟市镇，但二手HDB突破$1.24M，确实是一个重要里程碑。打个比方，这个价格已经超过不少位于非中心地段的旧condo目前的成交价。

对淡滨尼的屋主而言，这无疑是个好消息，说明二手市场正处于历史高位。对买家而言，这是一记当头棒喝：如果你想在这个市镇买一个大单位，就要做好花condo价钱买HDB的心理准备。在贷款能力和日后转售灵活度这两点上，务必想清楚再行动。
