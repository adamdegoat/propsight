VIP Condo Previews Under Scrutiny After Agents Get First Pick Before Public Buyers经纪抢先选购单位，condo VIP预览惯例遭监管机构审查

Regulators are examining how new condo launches handle VIP previews, a long-standing industry practice where property agents get access to units before the general public does. At many launches, agents not only bring clients during the preview period but also buy units for themselves or reserve the most desirable options well before ordinary buyers get a look in.

This has frustrated public buyers for years. You arrive at a launch genuinely interested in a specific stack or floor level, only to be told those units are already gone. The VIP preview system has effectively created a two-tier market where being connected to an agent, or being an agent yourself, gives you a structural head start over the average buyer.

Nothing has changed in the rules yet, but the regulatory scrutiny is a sign that it might. If you are planning to buy at an upcoming new launch, go in with eyes open. Ask your agent directly how the preview process works and whether any units are being held back. If the rules do tighten, the experience for public buyers at new launches should eventually become considerably fairer.

监管机构正在审查新condo开售时处理VIP预览的方式。这是业内由来已久的做法，房产经纪在公众买家进场之前，就能优先接触到单位。在许多开售活动中，经纪不仅会在预览期间带客户来看房，自己也会趁机购入单位，或早早预留最抢手的楼层和方位，让普通买家根本没有机会。

这种情况令公众买家不满已久。你兴冲冲地到新楼盘开售现场，对某个楼栋方向或楼层真的很感兴趣，却被告知那些单位早已售出。VIP预览制度实际上造成了双轨市场：认识经纪、或者本身就是经纪的人，比普通买家天生就有先机。

目前规定还没有任何改变，但监管机构的关注本身已经是一个信号。如果你计划在即将推出的新楼盘入市，请保持清醒。直接问你的经纪预览流程是怎么运作的，有没有单位被提前保留。一旦规定收紧，公众买家在新楼盘开售时的体验，应该会逐渐公平很多。
