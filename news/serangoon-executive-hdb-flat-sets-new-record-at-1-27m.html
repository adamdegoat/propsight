Serangoon Executive HDB Flat Sets New Record at $1.27M实龙岗特大型HDB组屋以$1.27M成交，创区域新纪录

$1.27M serangoon exec flat record实龙岗特大型组屋纪录

An executive HDB flat in Serangoon has sold for $1.27 million, narrowly pushing past the previous price record for the area. Executive flats are the largest type in the standard HDB range, typically between 1,300 and 1,600 square feet, and they offer a level of space that is genuinely difficult to match anywhere else at a comparable price in Singapore.

Serangoon is a mature estate with strong MRT connections, well-regarded schools nearby, and plenty of food and retail options close at hand. That combination of size, location, and everyday convenience is what drives buyers to pay prices that would have seemed unimaginable for an HDB flat not so many years ago.

If you are looking for a large HDB flat in a well-connected, established town, the Serangoon record is a signal of where the market is heading. Prices are not leaping dramatically, but each new record erodes the old ceiling. Buyers who need the space of an executive flat and have a firm budget should go in with clear numbers in mind, because waiting tends to cost more than acting.
