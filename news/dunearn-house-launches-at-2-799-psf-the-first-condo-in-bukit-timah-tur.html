Dunearn House Launches at $2,799 psf, the First Condo in Bukit Timah Turf CityDunearn House 以每平方英尺$2,799正式开售，成为武吉知马Turf City首个condo项目

$2,799 psf dunearn house launch priceDunearn House 推出价格

Frasers Property has officially launched Dunearn House at the Bukit Timah Turf City site, with prices starting at $2,799 per square foot and entry-level units priced from $1.475 million. It is the first private condo to come to market as part of what is expected to be a large, multi-year redevelopment of the Turf City land.

Being the first project out of the gate means buyers here are getting in early on a corridor that developers are positioning as a long-term growth area. That can work in your favour if the masterplan delivers over time, but it also means living through years of construction activity on the surrounding parcels before the full picture takes shape.

If you are watching the Turf City area, this is the earliest chance to buy in. Whether $2,799 psf makes sense depends on how much you believe in the corridor's story. Before committing, do your homework on what is planned for the land around this first phase and whether the neighbourhood will end up being what the developer is selling you.
