Private home prices edge up 0.5% while HDB resale prices fall again in Q2私宅价格Q2小涨0.5%，HDB转售价连续两个季度下跌

-0.3% hdb resale price drop q2HDB转售价Q2跌幅

Singapore's two biggest housing markets moved in opposite directions in Q2 2026. Flash estimates show private home prices rising 0.5% for the quarter, while HDB resale flat prices slipped 0.3%, the second consecutive quarterly decline for public housing.

The back-to-back dips in HDB resale prices are significant because they suggest a trend, not a one-off correction. Private residential prices, by contrast, continue their slow upward grind, supported by a different buyer pool and a tighter pipeline of new completions.

If you are selling an HDB flat, you need to reset your expectations. The market is not crashing, but the days of easy above-valuation offers are fading. On the private side, modest gains mean owners are broadly holding their value, even if the sharp appreciation of earlier years has cooled considerably.

2026年Q2，新加坡两大主要房产市场走向截然不同。初步估算显示，私宅价格季度上涨0.5%，而HDB转售组屋价格则下滑0.3%，是公共住房市场连续第二个季度出现跌幅。

HDB转售价连续两季下跌，意义不容小觑，因为这表明这是一个趋势，而非偶发性的调整。反观私宅市场，价格继续缓慢攀升，背后支撑来自不同的买家群体，以及新落成项目供应偏紧的格局。

如果你正在出售HDB组屋，需要重新调整心理预期。市场并没有崩盘，但轻松拿到高于估价的开价，那样的日子已经渐渐过去了。私宅这边，小幅上涨意味着业主的资产价值大体上得以保持，尽管早些年那种急剧升值的势头已经明显冷却。
