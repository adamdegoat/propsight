65% drop in new condo sales in june6月新condo销量跌幅

Developer sales of new private homes in Singapore dropped 65% in June, falling to the lowest monthly total in two years. The sharp decline came mainly because very few new projects were launched during the month, which tracks with the usual pattern of developers avoiding June school holidays for major releases.

This is a supply story, not a demand story. The buyers are still out there. Projects that launched earlier in the year sold well, and there is no evidence that buyers have suddenly lost interest. The low June number simply reflects a near-empty pipeline of new units for the month.

A rebound in July and August is the most likely outcome as developers bring more launches to market. If you are tracking new launches before making a buying decision, do not read the June figures as a sign the market is cooling. It is more a calendar gap than a change in underlying conditions.
