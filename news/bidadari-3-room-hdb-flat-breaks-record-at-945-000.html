Bidadari 3-Room HDB Flat Breaks Record at $945,000碧达里3房式HDB组屋创下$945,000成交纪录

$945K record 3-room hdb resale price3房式HDB二手房成交纪录价格

A 3-room flat in Bidadari changed hands for $945,000, setting a new record for that flat type within the estate. What makes this stand out is the pace: the previous record was set just eight months ago, meaning the high-water mark has been raised twice in under a year.

Bidadari is a relatively new BTO estate built with careful design, greenery and good transport links. Buyers have consistently shown they are willing to pay a premium for newer towns that feel different from older HDB estates, and resale demand there has remained strong even as the broader HDB resale market softens.

If you own a flat in a newer, well-designed estate, your property is behaving differently from the overall trend. The macro headline of falling HDB resale prices does not capture the full picture. Estate quality and location continue to command real premiums, and well-placed flats in newer towns are still finding buyers at record levels.

碧达里一套3房式组屋以$945,000易手，创下该市镇该房型的新成交纪录。令人印象深刻的是这一纪录刷新的速度：上一次纪录仅在八个月前才刚刚创下，意味着最高成交价在不到一年内已被刷新两次。

碧达里是一个相对较新的BTO市镇，以精心的规划设计、绿化环境和便捷的交通著称。即便整体HDB二手市场有所降温，买家始终愿意为那些与传统组屋市镇感觉不同的新兴市镇出高价，碧达里的二手需求持续强劲。
