Dunearn House: First Private Condo in Bukit Timah Turf City Launches From $2,799 psfDunearn House：武吉知马Turf City首个私人condo，起价$2,799 psf

$2,799 psf starting price per sq ft每平方英尺起步价

The Bukit Timah Turf City site has been on the radar for years as one of the last sizeable residential land parcels in that part of Singapore. Frasers Property is now the first developer to bring a project to market there, with Dunearn House previewing on July 10. Entry prices start at $1.475 million and the per square foot rate opens at $2,799.

That $2,799 psf starting point puts this firmly in prime territory. Many condos in suburban locations like Tampines or Jurong are transacting well below $2,000 psf. Buyers here are paying for the Bukit Timah address, the mature greenery in the area, and the novelty of being first into a significant new precinct.

The appeal is real for the right buyer. Good schools are nearby, land in this corridor is genuinely scarce, and the Turf City redevelopment has been anticipated long enough that some buyers will find the location compelling on its own merits. Being early in a new precinct has paid off before in Singapore.

That said, go in clear-eyed about what you are committing to. Larger units will run well past $3 million. This is a project for buyers who specifically want this address and are comfortable paying a significant premium for it. Comparing the psf against nearby resale condos before you decide is a sensible step.

武吉知马Turf City地段多年来一直备受关注，因为这是新加坡该地区最后几块较大的住宅用地之一。Frasers Property现在是首家在此推出项目的发展商，Dunearn House将于7月10日开放预览。起步价为$1.475 million，每平方英尺从$2,799起。

$2,799 psf的起步价格，将这个项目牢牢定位在高端价位。淡滨尼或裕廊等郊区地段的许多condo成交价远低于$2,000 psf。在这里，买家所付的溢价来自武吉知马这个地址、该区成熟的绿化环境，以及成为重要新区首批住户的新鲜感。

对合适的买家来说，这个项目的吸引力是实在的。附近有好学校，这一带的土地确实稀缺，Turf City重建计划已经被期待多时，不少买家会单凭地段就觉得值得入手。在新区率先入市，在新加坡历史上确实有过成功的先例。

话虽如此，还是要对自己所承诺的事情保持清醒。较大的单位价格将远超$3 million。这个项目适合那些明确想要这个地址、并愿意为此支付显著溢价的买家。在做决定之前，把psf价格与附近的二手condo作比较，是明智的一步。
