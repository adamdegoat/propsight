Private Home Prices Up 0.5%, HDB Resale Prices Down 0.3% in Q2 20262026年第二季度私人住宅价格涨0.5%，HDB二手房跌0.3%

+0.5% private home price rise Q2 20262026年第二季度私人住宅价格涨幅

Government flash estimates for Q2 2026 show private home prices rising 0.5% while HDB resale prices slipped a further 0.3%. Public housing resale has now fallen for two consecutive quarters, and the split between the two market segments is clearly reflected in the official numbers.

The two markets serve different buyers, but they are connected. Many HDB upgraders sell their flat to fund a private purchase. When HDB resale prices soften and private prices continue to rise at the same time, the real cost of making that upgrade journey increases even if neither buyer nor seller notices the trend on their own.

If your plan is to sell your HDB flat and move into a private condo, redo your numbers using today's figures. The gap between your expected sale proceeds and your target purchase price is wider than it was six months ago, and both trends are still moving in the wrong direction for upgraders. Time your move with care rather than assuming the two segments will converge on their own.

政府发布的2026年第二季度初步估计显示，私人住宅价格上涨0.5%，HDB二手房价格则进一步下滑0.3%。公共住房二手市场已连续两个季度录得价格下跌，两个市场的分化在官方数字中清晰可见。

两个市场服务不同的买家群体，但彼此相互关联。许多HDB升级者会出售组屋来资助私宅购置。当HDB二手价格走软、私宅价格同时持续上涨，即便买卖双方各自察觉不到这一趋势，完成这段升级之旅的实际成本也在悄然上升。

如果你的计划是卖掉HDB组屋、搬入私人condo，请用今天的数字重新核算一遍。你预期的出售所得与目标购入价格之间的差距，已比六个月前更大，而且两个趋势对升级者而言仍在朝不利方向发展。认真做好时机规划，不要指望这两个市场最终会自动靠拢。
