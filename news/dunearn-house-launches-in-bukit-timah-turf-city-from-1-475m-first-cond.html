Dunearn House Launches in Bukit Timah Turf City From $1.475M, First Condo in the TransformationDunearn House 于武吉知马 Turf City 正式开盘，起价 $1.475M，是重建项目首个推出的 condo

$1.475M starting price at dunearn houseDunearn House 起售价

Frasers Property has officially launched Dunearn House, making it the first private condominium to come out of the Bukit Timah Turf City redevelopment. The site has been talked about for years, so this marks the point where plans finally become real flats with real price tags attached. Entry level starts at $1.475 million for the smaller units.

The number that stands out is the gap between unit sizes. Going from a smaller unit up to a 3-bedroom costs roughly $1.1 million more. That is not a small step and it reflects both the Bukit Timah address premium and the scale of what is being built here. Turf City is positioned as a major new neighbourhood, and Dunearn House is the first piece of that puzzle on offer to buyers.

For anyone who has been watching this site and waiting to see what Turf City actually costs, now you have a baseline. But go in with your budget locked in before you view the larger layouts. The price jump between sizes is steep enough that falling for a 3-bedroom when you budgeted for a smaller unit will leave you in a difficult spot. Know your number first, then pick your flat.
