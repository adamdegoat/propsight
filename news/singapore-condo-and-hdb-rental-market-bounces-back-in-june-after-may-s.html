Singapore Condo and HDB Rental Market Bounces Back in June After May Slowdown新加坡condo和HDB租赁市场5月放缓后，6月重拾升势

Singapore's rental market for both private condominiums and HDB flats saw leasing volumes pick up in June 2026 after a quieter May. The recovery appears to have been driven by returning expatriates and students coming back ahead of a new term and work cycle, which tends to push rental demand higher around this time of year.

For landlords, this is a useful signal after a slow patch. If you have a unit sitting empty, conditions in June were more favourable for finding tenants than they were the month before, and that momentum tends to carry into July as more people settle their housing arrangements.

For renters, the softer window that existed in May has likely passed. More competing tenants means landlords have less reason to negotiate hard on price or terms. If you are actively looking for a rental and find something that fits your needs, moving promptly is better than holding back to see if the market softens again.

2026年6月，私人condo和HDB组屋的租赁成交量在经历了较为清淡的5月后回升。这波复苏似乎主要由回流的外籍人士和学生推动，他们赶在新学期和新工作周期开始前回来安顿，每年这个时候租赁需求往往会随之走高。

对业主来说，经过一段淡季之后，这是一个有用的信号。如果你的单位目前空置，6月份的市场状况比前一个月更有利于找到租客，而且随着更多人陆续安顿下来，这股势头往往会延续到7月。

对租客而言，5月份那段相对宽松的窗口期可能已经过去。更多租客争抢，意味着业主在价格或条款上让步的空间减少了。如果你正在积极找房，遇到合适的单位就应果断行动，不要等着市场再次降温。

Landlords should see decent interest from tenants again; renters looking for deals may face more competition now that the slow patch has passed.业主可望再次迎来稳定的租客咨询；正在寻找优惠租金的租客，随着淡季结束，可能会面临更多竞争。
