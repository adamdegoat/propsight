HDB Resale Prices Have Fallen for Two Straight Quarters. What It Means If You Plan to SellHDB转售房价连跌两个季度，如果你打算卖，该怎么办？

HDB resale prices have now fallen for two consecutive quarters, a shift that stands out after several years of strong growth. The decline is not dramatic, but the fact that it has continued for two quarters in a row means it is not a blip.

The earlier price run-up was driven by tight supply, BTO delays and buyers who could not or did not want to wait. Some of those pressures have eased. More completed resale flats are coming to market, buyers have more options to compare, and that tilts bargaining power back toward them at the margin.

If you are planning to sell your HDB flat soon, the main takeaway is simple: pricing at the top of the range and waiting for a buyer to appear is a harder strategy than it was a year ago. Flats that are overpriced are sitting longer. Starting with a realistic asking price based on recent transactions in your block or street, and being willing to negotiate, will get you to a sale faster and with less frustration. Waiting for prices to recover is an option, but there is no clear signal that a quick rebound is coming.

早前的价格上涨，是由供应紧张、BTO延误以及不愿或无法等待的买家共同推动的。如今，这些压力有所缓解。越来越多完成转售的组屋陆续入市，买家有了更多选择可以比较，市场议价力也因此在边际上向买家倾斜。

如果你近期打算出售HDB组屋，核心信息很简单：以挂牌价上限定价、然后等待买家上门，比一年前难多了。定价过高的组屋挂牌时间越来越长。根据同一栋楼或同一条街最近的成交记录来制定切实可行的开价，并愿意协商，才能更快、更省心地完成交易。等待房价回升当然也是一个选项，但目前没有明确信号显示近期会有快速反弹。
