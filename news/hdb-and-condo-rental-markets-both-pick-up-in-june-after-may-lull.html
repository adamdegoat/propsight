HDB and Condo Rental Markets Both Pick Up in June After May LullHDB组屋和condo租赁市场六月双双回暖，从五月淡静中复苏

Rental transactions for both HDB flats and private condos picked up in June, recovering from a quieter May. The volume of leasing deals rose on both sides of the market, pointing to a brief seasonal lull rather than any deeper loss of momentum.

Heading into the second half of the year, tenant demand appears to remain solid. The June rebound is a useful indicator that the Singapore rental market still has legs, even after the sustained run it has had over the past few years.

For landlords, this means the window to find a tenant has reopened after the May quieter patch. More tenants are actively looking, which gives you a better chance of getting your asking rent. For renters, more listings will appear, but competition from other tenants will also increase. If you find a place that works, do not sit on it for too long.

六月份，HDB组屋和私人condo的租赁交易量均有所回升，从较为淡静的五月中复苏过来。市场两边的成交量都在增加，说明五月只是短暂的季节性淡季，并非市场动力真正减弱。

进入下半年，租客需求看来依然稳固。六月的反弹是一个有力信号，说明新加坡租赁市场在过去几年持续走高之后，仍有后劲。

对于房东来说，这意味着在五月的淡静期过后，租出单位的机会之窗已经重新打开。现在有更多租客在积极找房，要到心仪租金的机会也更大。对于租客来说，房源会增多，但其他租客的竞争也会随之加剧。如果找到了合适的地方，别拖太久。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are renting out your flat or condo, or looking to rent, the market is moving again after a brief dip, so expect more listings and stronger competition from both sides.无论你是在出租组屋或condo，还是正在找房子租，市场在短暂回落后已经重新活跃起来，预计房源会增多，租客之间的竞争也会加剧。
