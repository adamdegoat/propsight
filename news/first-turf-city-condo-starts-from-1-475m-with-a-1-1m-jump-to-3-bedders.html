First Turf City Condo Starts From $1.475M, with a $1.1M Jump to 3-Bedders首个Turf City Condo起价$1.475M，三卧室单位再跳升$1.1M

$1.475M Dunearn House starting priceDunearn House起价

Dunearn House is the first private condominium to launch from the Turf City redevelopment in Bukit Timah, and a reporter visited the showflat ahead of its official opening. Two-bedroom units start at $1.475 million, but the price climbs by $1.1 million to reach the three-bedroom tier, a gap that is hard to ignore.

Turf City is a long-awaited project on the former Singapore Turf Club grounds along Dunearn Road. The Bukit Timah address carries a premium for good reason: strong schools, green surroundings and reasonable proximity to the city. But a jump of more than a million dollars between unit types is steep, even for this location, and it will likely push many families straight to the top of their budget range before they have finished the site visit.

The practical advice here is simple: work out your number before you walk into the showflat. These launches are designed to impress, and a $1.475 million entry price combined with that sharp step-up makes it easy to stretch further than intended. Compare the total cost and financing against resale options in the area before you commit to anything.
