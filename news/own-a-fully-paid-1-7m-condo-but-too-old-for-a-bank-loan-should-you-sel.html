Own a Fully Paid $1.7M Condo But Too Old for a Bank Loan. Should You Sell and Buy a Resale HDB?坐拥全额还清的$1.7M condo却因年龄申请不到银行贷款，卖掉转买转售HDB，划算吗？

$1.7M condo value in right-sizing scenario缩房方案中的condo估值

This is a situation more older Singaporeans are running into: you own a valuable private property outright, but banks will not extend you a new loan because of your age. The homeowner here has a $1.7 million condo fully paid off, no outstanding mortgage, but cannot borrow against it. The question being asked is whether selling and moving to a cheaper resale HDB flat is the smarter move.

On the surface, selling at $1.7 million and buying a resale flat for perhaps $500,000 to $700,000 looks like it frees up a large chunk of cash. But the actual numbers are more complicated once you factor in stamp duties, the five-year minimum occupation period before you can sell the HDB flat, and what your CPF can and cannot do for you at this stage. The lifestyle shift is also real, and for some people that matters as much as the money.

The honest answer depends on what you plan to do with the cash you unlock and whether your current condo is costing you in maintenance or sitting underutilised. For some owners, right-sizing to an HDB flat genuinely improves their financial position in retirement. For others, the numbers are tighter than they look on paper. Either way, this decision needs proper calculations run through it, not just a rough comparison of sale price versus purchase price.

越来越多年长新加坡人正面临这样的处境：手上持有一套价值不菲的私人住宅，但银行因年龄原因不肯批出新贷款。这位屋主的$1.7M condo已全额还清、没有未了结的房贷，却无法以此向银行借款。他考虑的问题是：卖掉condo，转买一套价格较低的转售HDB组屋，是否更划算？

表面上看，以$1.7M卖出，再花$500,000到$700,000买一套转售组屋，似乎能释放出一大笔现金。但实际数字要复杂得多，你还得考虑印花税、HDB组屋转售前必须住满五年的最低居住期，以及在这个人生阶段CPF能帮你做什么、又做不到什么。生活方式上的转变也是真实存在的，对某些人来说，这和钱一样重要。

诚实的答案取决于你打算如何运用释放出来的现金，以及你现在的condo是否正在产生维护费用，或是一直闲置未被充分利用。对某些屋主而言，缩房到HDB组屋确实能改善退休后的财务状况；但对另一些人来说，账面上的数字比看起来要紧绷得多。无论如何，这个决定需要认真地把数字算清楚，而不是单凭售价和买价的粗略对比就下结论。
