Queenstown 2-Room Flat Sells for $696,000, Breaking National Record女皇镇两房式组屋以$696,000成交，创全国最高纪录

$696,000 national record 2-room flat price两房式组屋全国最高成交价

A 2-room HDB resale flat in Queenstown has changed hands for $696,000, a new national record for the smallest flat type on the public housing market. A 2-room flat is the smallest category HDB offers, which makes this price remarkable even against today's already elevated resale standards.

The buyer paid that amount for the location, not the size. Queenstown is a mature estate with excellent MRT access, a full range of amenities, and an established community that some buyers will pay a very high premium to be part of. The flat itself is almost secondary to where it sits.

This transaction is a useful reminder of how HDB resale pricing actually works. Location still dominates everything else. When people speak of public housing as affordable, a near-$700,000 price for the smallest flat type in a prime estate shows that affordability is very relative once you factor in the address.

女皇镇一间两房式HDB转售组屋以$696,000易手，创下公共房屋市场上最小户型的全国新纪录。两房式是HDB提供的最小单位类型，即便放在如今已经相当高企的转售市场标准来看，这个价格也相当惊人。

买家愿意出这个价钱，买的是地点，不是面积。女皇镇是成熟组屋区，MRT交通便利，配套设施齐全，社区氛围成熟，有些买家为了住进这里，愿意付出相当高的溢价。单位本身反而是次要的，关键在于它坐落在哪里。

这宗交易提醒我们，HDB转售定价的逻辑到底是怎么一回事。地段始终凌驾于其他一切因素之上。当人们说公共组屋价格亲民时，黄金地段最小户型近$700,000的成交价说明，一旦把地址这个因素算进去，所谓的「负担得起」其实是非常相对的。
