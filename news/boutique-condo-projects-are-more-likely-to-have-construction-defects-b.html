Boutique Condo Projects Are More Likely to Have Construction Defects, BCA FindsBCA发现：精品小型condo项目出现建筑缺陷的概率更高

The Building and Construction Authority has found that smaller boutique condo developments have higher rates of construction defects compared to larger projects. This is worth taking seriously if you are buying into a small development, because it reflects a real and measurable difference in build quality outcomes, not just anecdote.

Boutique condos often carry a premium appeal based on exclusivity and a quieter residential feel. But the BCA data suggests the construction process at smaller sites is more prone to quality problems, possibly because large projects have more resources and tighter quality control built into their workflows by necessity.

When you collect your keys from a boutique condo, do not skip the defect inspection or rush through it. Engage a professional inspector if your budget allows. The odds of finding something that needs fixing are meaningfully higher than at a large development, and the developer's defect liability period is your best window to get issues sorted at no cost to you.

建设局（BCA）发现，小型精品condo发展项目的建筑缺陷率，比大型项目更高。如果你正在购买小型发展项目的单位，这一点值得认真对待，因为它反映的是有据可查的施工质量差异，而不仅仅是坊间说法。

精品condo往往以独特性和安静的居住氛围作为卖点，定价也经常偏高。但BCA的数据显示，小型工地的施工过程更容易出现质量问题，原因可能在于大型项目在资源和质量管控方面有更严格的流程要求。

收到精品condo的钥匙时，不要跳过验房环节，也不要草草了事。如果预算允许，建议聘请专业验房师。在这类项目发现需要修缮问题的概率，明显高于大型发展项目，而发展商的缺陷责任期是你免费解决问题的最佳窗口，错过了就要自掏腰包。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
