HDB Resale Prices Have Now Fallen For Two Straight QuartersHDB转售价格已连续两个季度下滑

2 quarters hdb resale price fall streakHDB转售价格连跌纪录

HDB resale flat prices have now fallen for two quarters running. It is not a crash, but it is a clear and consistent trend heading downward, and the main force behind it is the same one drawing younger buyers toward BTO flats instead of resale.

When more first-time buyers leave the resale pool, sellers face less competition among buyers and more pressure on price. The data backs this up across flat types and locations, not just in one corner of the market.

If you are shopping for a resale flat right now, the market is shifting in your favour. You have more options, less urgency, and more room to negotiate than you would have had twelve months ago. That does not mean lowballing every seller, but a fair and firm negotiation is more realistic than it was at the peak.

HDB转售组屋价格已连续两个季度下滑。这不是崩盘，但却是一个清晰而持续的向下趋势，而背后的主要推力，正是那股将年轻买家吸引向BTO组屋、而非转售市场的同一股力量。

当越来越多的首次购房者离开转售市场，卖家就面临更少的买家竞争和更大的价格压力。数据印证了这一点，涉及范围横跨各种房型和地段，并非局限于市场某个角落。

如果你正在寻找转售组屋，市场正在向你倾斜。你有更多选择、更少紧迫感，议价空间也比十二个月前更大。这并不意味着要对每一位卖家大幅杀价，但进行合理而坚定的谈判，比市场高峰时期现实得多了。
