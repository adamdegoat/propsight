HDB Resale Prices Have Now Fallen for Two Straight QuartersHDB转售房价已连续两个季度下跌

2 quarters consecutive hdb resale price fallsHDB转售价格连续下跌季度数

HDB resale flat prices have now fallen for two quarters in a row. One quarter of softness can be noise, but two consecutive quarters of decline points to a real shift in what buyers are willing to pay and how much leverage sellers actually have right now.

Part of what is driving this is that buyers have more options than before. More BTO flats are completing and entering the market, and resale listings have grown. When buyers have alternatives, they stop bidding aggressively and start negotiating. That change in behaviour is showing up in transaction prices.

If you are selling an HDB flat, you need to price it with the current market in mind rather than peak prices from a year or two ago. Listings that sit for weeks are usually overpriced. Sellers who adjust their asking price early tend to get better outcomes than those who wait, hoping the market will turn back in their favour.

HDB转售组屋价格已连续两个季度下跌。单个季度的疲软或许只是正常波动，但连续两个季度的下滑，说明买家愿意出的价格和卖家实际掌握的议价空间，都确实发生了变化。

背后的原因之一，是买家现在的选择比以前多了。越来越多的BTO组屋陆续竣工进入市场，转售房源也在增加。当买家有了其他选择，他们就不会再积极竞价，而是开始讨价还价。这种行为上的转变，已经反映在成交价格上了。

如果你正在卖HDB组屋，定价要参考当前的市场行情，而不是一两年前的高峰价格。挂牌好几个星期都没动静的，通常是开价太高了。尽早调整开价的卖家，往往比那些苦等市场好转的人，结果要好得多。
