New Gillman Barracks Housing Estate Will Keep 25 Heritage Buildings吉门军营新住宅区将保留25座历史建筑

25 heritage buildings to be kept将保留的历史建筑数目

The new residential estate planned for Gillman Barracks, tucked in the south near Alexandra, will preserve 25 colonial-era heritage buildings as part of its design. Green spaces will also be woven throughout the estate alongside the new homes.

Gillman Barracks already carries a reputation as a distinctive, low-rise enclave. Keeping 25 heritage structures means the new estate will not resemble a typical blank-slate HDB or condo development, which could be a genuine draw for buyers who want character and history in their surroundings.

No launch dates have been set yet, so this is one to monitor rather than act on immediately. But if you are tracking properties in the south, this estate is worth putting on your watchlist. A combination of conserved heritage buildings, greenery and a fresh address could make it one of the more sought-after launches when it eventually comes to market.

计划在亚历山大附近南部吉门军营打造的新住宅区，将在其设计规划中保留25座殖民时代的历史建筑。绿化空间也将贯穿整个住宅区，与新住宅融为一体。

吉门军营本就以独特的低矮建筑风格著称。保留25座历史建筑，意味着新住宅区不会像典型的全新HDB或condo发展项目那样，是一片白板式的开发，这对于重视生活底蕴与历史感的买家来说，可能是真正的吸引力所在。

目前尚未公布任何推出日期，所以现阶段宜静观其变，无需急于行动。但如果你在追踪南部一带的房产，这个住宅区值得列入观察名单。保育历史建筑、绿化空间与全新地址三者兼具，一旦推出市场，很可能成为备受追捧的项目之一。
