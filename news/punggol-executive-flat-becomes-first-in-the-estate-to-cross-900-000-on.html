Punggol executive flat becomes first in the estate to cross $900,000, on a lower floor榜鹅执行式单位首次突破$900,000，成交楼层并不高

$900K+ punggol exec flat record price榜鹅执行式单位纪录成交价

A Punggol executive flat has sold for more than $900,000, crossing that threshold for the first time in the estate. What makes this more striking is that the unit was on a lower floor. This was not a penthouse view pushing the price up.

Executive flats are among the largest HDB flat types available. Punggol has grown into a popular town with good amenities and transport links, and demand for bigger units there has been building steadily. Buyers with families who want space and are priced out of private property have been willing to pay a premium for it.

The takeaway for owners of large HDB flats in towns like Punggol is that floor level matters less than you might expect. The demand is for the space itself. If you have been underpricing because your unit is not on a high floor, this transaction suggests you may have more negotiating room than you assumed.

榜鹅一个执行式HDB单位成交价超过$900,000，在该区首次突破这个门槛。更引人注意的是，该单位并非高楼层，推高价格的不是什么顶层风景。

执行式单位是HDB房型中面积最大的之一。榜鹅已经发展成一个设施完善、交通便利的成熟市镇，对大面积单位的需求一直在稳步增加。有家庭需要空间、又被私宅价格拒之门外的买家，愿意为此付出溢价。

对拥有像榜鹅这样的市镇大型HDB单位的业主来说，这宗成交说明楼层的影响比你想的要小。买家要的是空间本身。如果你因为单位不在高楼层而把价格定低了，这宗交易告诉你，你或许还有更多的议价空间。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Large HDB flats in towns like Punggol are hitting new highs even for mid-floor units, so do not assume only top-floor or prime-facing units command big prices.像榜鹅这样的市镇，大型HDB单位即使在中低楼层也能创出新高，别以为只有顶层或朝向好的单位才能卖出高价。
