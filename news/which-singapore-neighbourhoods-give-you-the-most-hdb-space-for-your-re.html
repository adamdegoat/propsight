Which Singapore Neighbourhoods Give You the Most HDB Space for Your Rental Budget同样的租金预算，在新加坡哪个地区能租到最大的HDB单位？

A closer look at HDB rental prices across Singapore reveals a wide gap in what tenants can actually get for the same monthly budget. Central and mature estates command a significant premium per room compared with towns further from the city, and the difference is not marginal.

Depending on which estates you compare, the same monthly rent can get you a three-room flat in one area or only a two-room flat a few MRT stops closer to town. For tenants who have some flexibility on exact location, the numbers make a compelling case for widening the search.

If you are renting now or about to start looking, do not lock yourself to one neighbourhood before checking what the next town along actually costs. Shifting your search by one or two stations can translate into meaningfully more space for the same monthly outlay, and that difference compounds fast over a twelve-month lease.

仔细研究新加坡各地HDB租金会发现，租客用同样的月预算实际能租到的单位差异相当大。相比距市区较远的市镇，中部和成熟市镇的每间房租金明显更贵，而且差距绝非可以忽略不计。

根据所比较的市镇不同，同样的月租金，在某个地区可以租到三房式组屋，而往市区方向走几个MRT站，可能只能租到两房式。对于地点有一定灵活度的租客来说，这些数字很有说服力，值得扩大搜索范围。

如果你目前在租房或即将开始找房，在锁定某个地区之前，先了解一下邻近市镇的租金行情。把搜索范围扩大一两个站，可能意味着花同样的月租金住进明显更宽敞的家，而这个差距在十二个月的租约里会越来越有感觉。
