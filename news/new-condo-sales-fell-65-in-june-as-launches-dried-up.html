New condo sales fell 65% in June as launches dried up六月新condo销售量骤跌65%，新项目推出大幅减少

65% drop in new condo sales june六月新condo销售量跌幅

Developer sales of new private homes in Singapore dropped 65% in June compared to May, a steep fall that looks alarming at first glance. The actual explanation is straightforward: very few major residential projects launched during the school holiday period, leaving buyers with little to choose from and monthly volumes naturally thin.

New launch sales are driven almost entirely by what is put on the market each month. When developers hold back, transactions fall regardless of whether buyer appetite is healthy. Analysts are already pointing to a stronger pipeline of launches expected in July, which should push numbers back up.

Do not mistake a slow launch month for a weakening market. The underlying demand from buyers has not disappeared. If you are considering a new launch purchase, focus on whether a specific project suits your needs and is priced fairly. One month of headline numbers driven by a thin pipeline tells you very little about where the market is actually heading.

新加坡发展商六月份的新私宅销售量，比五月跌了65%，乍看之下挺吓人的。不过实际原因很简单：学校假期期间，几乎没有大型住宅项目推出，买家本来就没什么可选的，当月成交量自然偏低。

新项目的销售量，几乎完全取决于当月有多少项目推出市场。发展商若选择暂缓推盘，成交量就会下滑，跟买家的购买意愿强不强并没有直接关系。分析师已经指出，七月份预计有更充裕的新项目推出，数字应该会回升。

别把推出量少的月份误以为是市场转弱的信号。买家的实际需求并没有消失。如果你正在考虑购买新推出的项目，还是把重点放在那个项目本身是否符合你的需求、定价是否合理。单靠一个月因推盘量少而产生的数字，很难真正看出市场接下来的走向。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
