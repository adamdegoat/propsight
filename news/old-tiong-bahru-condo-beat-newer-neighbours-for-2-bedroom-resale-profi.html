Old Tiong Bahru condo beat newer neighbours for 2-bedroom resale profits中峇鲁旧condo两房转售利润跑赢周边新盘

$306,000 avg profit per 2-bedder resale两房单位转售平均获利

An older condominium in Tiong Bahru averaged $306,000 in profit per 2-bedroom resale unit, outperforming newer developments in nearby Bukit Merah and Queenstown on the same measure. The result is a clean reminder that a building's age tells you far less about its investment potential than its address does.

Tiong Bahru has a loyal following. Its heritage character, central location and strong amenities draw a consistent pool of buyers and tenants who are willing to pay up. That sustained demand is what keeps older buildings in the area competitive even when newer projects with fresher fittings are available nearby.

For buyers and investors, the lesson is to resist the instinct to equate newer with better value. A well-located older condo with solid demand can deliver stronger gains than a newer one in a less desirable spot. When sizing up resale potential, weight the neighbourhood at least as heavily as the condition and age of the building itself.

中峇鲁一个较旧的condo，两房单位转售平均获利$306,000，在同一指标上跑赢了附近武吉美拉和女皇镇的新盘。这清楚说明了一件事：一栋楼的楼龄，对投资潜力的参考价值，远不及它的地段来得大。

中峇鲁有一批忠实的拥趸。这里浓厚的历史风情、优越的地理位置，加上完善的生活配套，长期吸引着一批愿意出价的买家和租客，需求持续稳定。正是这股持续的需求，让这一带的旧楼依然具有竞争力，即使附近有装修更新的新盘可供选择，也不例外。

对买家和投资者来说，这里有一个值得记住的道理：不要理所当然地认为越新就越值钱。一个地段好、需求稳定的旧condo，往往比位置欠佳的新盘带来更高的升值空间。评估转售潜力时，地段的分量，至少要和楼况与楼龄并重，甚至更重。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
Older condos in popular neighbourhoods can still deliver strong gains, so do not automatically dismiss them in favour of newer projects.热门地区的旧condo同样可以带来可观的回报，不要因为楼龄较旧就直接把它们排除在外，转而只看新盘。
