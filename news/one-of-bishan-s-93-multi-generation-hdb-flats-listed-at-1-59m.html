One of Bishan's 93 Multi-Generation HDB Flats Listed at $1.59M碧山仅有93个多代同堂HDB单位之一挂牌$1.59M

$1.59M asking price dual-key flat双钥匙单位开价

Multi-Generation flats are a rare HDB flat type built specifically so two families, typically parents and an adult child's household, can share an address while still having separate entrances and self-contained living spaces. Across all of Bishan, just 93 of these units exist. One of them has now been listed for sale at $1.59 million.

The scarcity here is not a selling point being exaggerated by an agent. These flats were designed for a specific purpose and were built in limited numbers in selected towns. When one comes to market, you are not choosing from a list of comparable options the way you would with a standard four-room flat.

For families who want two generations under one roof without actually living on top of each other, this setup solves a genuine problem. Two separate living areas, two sets of keys, one address. Buying two separate homes to achieve the same result would cost considerably more and would not give you the proximity that many families are actually after.

$1.59 million is high by HDB standards but not hard to understand given how few ever come up for sale. If this arrangement is what your family needs, expect to pay the premium and act quickly when you find one. Listings like this do not sit around.

这里的稀缺性并非经纪人夸大的卖点。这类单位是为特定需求而设计的，仅在部分市镇有限数量建造。当市场上出现一个，你不会像挑选普通四房式单位那样，有一长串可供比较的选择。

对于希望两代同堂、又不想彼此过于紧密的家庭来说，这种安排解决了一个真实的问题。两套独立的生活空间，两把钥匙，一个地址。若要另外购买两个独立单位达到同样效果，费用会高得多，而且也得不到许多家庭真正想要的那种亲近感。

$1.59 million以HDB标准来说价格不低，但考虑到这类单位极少出现在市场上，这个价格不难理解。如果这种安排正是你家庭所需要的，做好支付溢价的心理准备，并在找到合适单位时迅速行动。这类挂牌不会在市场上久等。
