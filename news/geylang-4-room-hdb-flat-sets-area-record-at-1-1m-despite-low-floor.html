Geylang 4-Room HDB Flat Sets Area Record at $1.1M Despite Low Floor芽笼4房式HDB组屋以$1.1M创区域成交纪录，楼层并不算高

$1.1M Geylang 4-room HDB record芽笼4房式HDB成交纪录

A 4-room HDB resale flat in Geylang has changed hands for $1.1 million, setting a new price record for the estate. What makes this sale particularly notable is that the unit was not on a high floor, where buyers typically expect to pay extra for views and better airflow.

Geylang has historically traded at a discount compared to similarly sized flats in nearby estates, partly because of its mixed-use character and long-standing reputation. That discount has clearly eroded. Buyers are now willing to pay for the area's central location, transport links, and amenities regardless of how the neighbourhood has traditionally been perceived.

Sellers in Geylang should stop assuming their flat is worth less than equivalent units elsewhere. And buyers who have been targeting Geylang specifically for affordability need to revise their budgets upward. The million-dollar mark is no longer a ceiling for the area; for well-located units it is starting to look more like a floor.

芽笼一个4房式HDB转售组屋以$1.1 million成交，创下该区的新纪录。这次交易特别值得关注的地方，在于该单位并非高楼层，通常买家才会愿意为高楼层的视野和通风支付额外溢价。

芽笼历来比附近屋苑同等面积的组屋便宜，部分原因是该区混合用途的特性和长久以来的口碑。但这个折扣显然已经消退。买家如今愿意为这里的中心位置、交通便利和周边配套买单，不再受传统印象左右。

芽笼的卖家不应再以为自己的组屋比其他地方的同类单位逊色。而那些冲着芽笼价格相对亲民而来的买家，也得调高预算了。百万元已不再是这个区的天花板，对于位置优越的单位来说，这个价位越来越像是地板。
