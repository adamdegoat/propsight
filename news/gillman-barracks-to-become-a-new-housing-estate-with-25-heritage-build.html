Gillman Barracks to Become a New Housing Estate, With 25 Heritage Buildings PreservedGillman Barracks将建成新住宅区，25座历史建筑获保留

8ha greenery preserved at gillmanGillman保留绿地面积

Gillman Barracks, the former military camp in southern Singapore, is being converted into a full residential estate. Plans confirm that 25 heritage buildings on the site will be kept, and 8 hectares of greenery will be retained as part of the new precinct.

The site sits near Alexandra, in a stretch of Singapore that has seen almost no new housing come to market in years. That scarcity, combined with the area's proximity to the city fringe and good connectivity, makes this a meaningful addition to residential supply in the south.

For buyers, this is a rare chance to enter a new estate that comes built-in with heritage character and green space, rather than a blank-slate development. That combination tends to attract buyers who want something different, and historically those factors hold value well over time.

Owners in the surrounding area, including Alexandra Road and Labrador Park, should also take note. A new estate of this scale tends to lift the profile of nearby properties as amenities, foot traffic and attention follow. Keep an eye on future launch details and pricing benchmarks when units are eventually released.

该地段毗邻亚历山大，是新加坡多年来几乎没有新房推出市场的地区之一。供应稀缺、靠近市区边缘、交通便利，多重因素叠加，使这个项目成为南部住宅供应的一次重要补充。

对买家来说，这是难得的机会，入驻一个自带历史韵味与绿色空间的全新社区，而非千篇一律的白板发展项目。这样的特质向来能吸引追求与众不同的买家，历史经验也表明，这些因素有助于长期保值。

亚历山大路及Labrador Park一带的现有业主同样值得留意。这种规模的新社区落成，往往会带动周边房产的整体形象提升，随之而来的是配套增加、人流上涨以及市场关注度提高。项目最终推出时，请密切关注详情及定价基准。
