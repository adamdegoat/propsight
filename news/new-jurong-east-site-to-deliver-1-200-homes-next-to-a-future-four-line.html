New Jurong East Site to Deliver 1,200 Homes Next to a Future Four-Line MRT Interchange裕廊东新地段可建约1,200套住宅，毗邻未来四线MRT交汇站

1,200 homes in new jurong east site裕廊东新地段住宅数量

The government has released a land parcel in Jurong East for sale, with the site expected to produce around 1,200 homes. The plot sits right beside a planned MRT interchange where four lines will eventually converge, making it one of the most connected locations anywhere in the west.

This release is part of Singapore's long-running push to build Jurong into a genuine second hub for commerce and living. A four-line interchange at a single node is rare, and developments nearest to such a node tend to set the pricing tone for the surrounding area for years after they launch.

If you are considering buying in the west, watch how this site is tendered and what the winning developer pays for the land. That land cost feeds directly into the eventual launch price of the project, and it will also put a floor under what resale homes in the wider Jurong corridor can ask for. This single site will shape the west's property market for a long time.

政府已在裕廊东推出一幅土地供出售，该地段预计可建约1,200套住宅。地块紧邻一个规划中的MRT交汇站，届时将有四条线路在此汇合，是西部地区连接性最强的地段之一。

此次推出是新加坡长期打造裕廊成为真正商业与生活第二中心计划的一部分。单一节点拥有四线MRT交汇实属罕见，最靠近这类枢纽的发展项目，往往在开盘后多年内左右周边地区的定价走势。

如果你正考虑在西部置业，留意这幅地段的招标进展以及得标发展商支付的地价。地价直接影响项目最终的开售价格，同时也会为裕廊走廊一带二手住宅的开价设立底线。这幅地段将在相当长的时间内影响西部的房产市场格局。
