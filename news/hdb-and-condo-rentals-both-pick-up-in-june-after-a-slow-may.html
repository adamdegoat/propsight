HDB and condo rentals both pick up in June after a slow May6月份HDB和condo租赁活动双双回升，走出5月淡季

Rental activity for both HDB flats and private condos picked up in June after a quieter May. The dip in May is a pattern that repeats most years, as school holidays temporarily reduce the number of tenants actively looking to move.

With school back in session and companies bringing in new hires ahead of the second half of the year, the market typically sees a lift in June and July. This year is following that same rhythm, with more listings appearing and more inquiries coming through compared to a month ago.

For landlords who held off listing during the quiet period, now is a reasonable time to put your unit on the market. For tenants currently hunting for a home, there are more options to choose from than there were in May, though that also means more competition from other renters looking at the same units.

HDB单位和私人condo的租赁活动在6月份重新活跃，走出了5月的淡静期。5月较为清淡是每年都会出现的规律，因为学校假期期间，主动寻找住所的租客会暂时减少。

随着学校复课，加上各公司为下半年引进新员工，市场在6月和7月通常会迎来一波活跃期。今年也不例外，与上个月相比，盘源数量和询问量都有所增加。

对于在淡静期按兵不动的房东来说，现在是把单位推出市场的合适时机。对于目前正在找房的租客，可选的房源比5月份多了，不过这也意味着，同时在看同一个单位的竞争者也多了。

What it means for you对你意味着什么
If you are a landlord or a tenant looking for a place, the market is moving again with more listings and inquiries than a month ago.如果你是房东或正在找房的租客，市场已重新活跃，盘源和询问量都比上个月多。
