Punggol executive flat is first in the estate to sell for over $900,000, even on a lower floorPunggol一个executive flat以逾$900,000成交，成为该区首个突破此价位的单位，楼层并不高

$900K+ punggol exec flat record pricePunggol executive flat成交纪录

A resale executive flat in Punggol has crossed the $900,000 mark, making it the first flat of its type in the estate to reach that price level. What makes the transaction particularly striking is that the unit was not even on a high floor, which would normally command a premium.

Executive flats are among the largest HDB flat types available, offering more space than a standard five-room flat. But they are still public housing, and Punggol was not long ago considered a far-flung new town. A transaction above $900,000 there signals just how much buyer sentiment has shifted as the estate has matured and transport links have improved.

For existing Punggol flat owners, this is a useful reference point when thinking about what your home might fetch. For buyers, it is a clear signal that large HDB flats in well-connected and maturing towns are now trading at prices that overlap with entry-level private condos in some other parts of the island.

Punggol一个转售executive flat的成交价突破$900,000，成为该区同类型单位首个达到这一价格水平的交易。令这宗交易格外引人注目的，是这个单位所在楼层并不高，高楼层通常才能卖出溢价。

Executive flat是现有HDB组屋中面积最大的类型之一，比标准五房式单位提供更多居住空间。但它终究是公共住房，而Punggol不久前还被视为偏远的新市镇。这宗逾$900,000的交易，清楚说明随着该区日趋成熟、交通联系持续改善，买家的情绪已经发生了多大的转变。

对于Punggol现有组屋业主而言，这是评估自己房子可能卖出多少的一个有用参考点。对于买家来说，这是一个明确的信号：联系方便、日渐成熟的市镇中，大型HDB组屋的成交价，已经与新加坡某些其他地区入门级私宅condo的价格范围重叠。
