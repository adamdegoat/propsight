Built on official records

The numbers the pros pay for. Yours, free.

Every figure on PropSight traces back to source. We pull the official transaction records, what homes actually sold for, not what a listing is asking, and rebuild the picture from the ground up each week.

  • 137,000+ private caveats from URA, plus every HDB resale and OneMap's school & amenity data.
  • A valuation engine measured against reality, within about 4% of the eventual price on a typical home, and honest when it's unsure.
  • Refreshed weekly, so what you see is the market as it is now, not last year.
