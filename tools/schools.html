          How the 1km / 2km rule works. When a school has more applicants than places, children are ranked by how close home is: those within 1km come first, then 1-2km, then beyond 2km, applied within each group (Singapore Citizens first, then PRs). Distance is the straight-line (“as the crow flies”) distance from your home to the school’s main gate, using SLA’s OneMap.

          Living in the zone is a priority, not a guarantee, popular schools still ballot. And to keep the priority you must live at that address for at least 30 months from the start of the registration exercise.
