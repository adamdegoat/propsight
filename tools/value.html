This is an estimate from recent comparable sales (URA caveats for private homes, HDB resale records), not a formal bank or licensed valuation. A bank's valuation, your unit's condition, view, renovation and exact stack can move the real number. Important: your bank loan and CPF only stretch up to the bank's valuation, if you agree to pay above it, that gap comes entirely out of your own cash. Newer or thinly-traded projects may have few comparables. Treat the range as a guide, and message us for a proper, address-specific valuation. Data refreshed regularly from official sources.
