    An estimate of the main grants (EHG, CPF Housing/Family Grant, Proximity, EC), not an official confirmation. Grants are credited to your CPF and used to pay for the flat (not cash). EHG also needs 12 months' continuous work; "first-timer" means no prior housing subsidy. Other rules apply (age, occupier, ownership, citizenship), and second-timers may get the $15k Step-Up Grant in some cases. Always confirm your exact grants with HDB. Figures as of 2026.
