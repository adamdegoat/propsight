Private resale has flattened. The median is $1,753 psf, up 0.6% on the month and 3.8% over the year, with a typical deal near $1.65m. Across 22,196 transactions over the past 18 months, prices are still rising but the pace has slowed. The latest month is preliminary, so treat it lightly, but the direction is clear.

The three regions have split. Suburbs (OCR) are at $1,523 psf, up 1.4% over three months. City-fringe (RCR) is at $1,962 psf, up 0.6%. Prime (CCR) is at $2,203 psf, down 2.4%, the only segment falling. The mass market has the momentum, and prime is where to negotiate.

New-launch premium over resale新盘对转售的溢价

New launches cost far more than resale. Suburban resale is $1,523 psf, while the OCR best sellers are well above it: Tengah Garden Residences $2,113 psf, Springleaf Residence $2,169 psf, Parktown Residence $2,361 psf and Chuan Park $2,598 psf. In the city-fringe they are higher, The Orie at $2,723 psf and One Marina Gardens at $2,957 psf. You pay a premium for a new unit in the same districts where resale is cheaper.

That premium is not automatically value. A new unit buys a fresh 99-year lease, modern layouts and no renovation, but at these prices future growth is priced in, and the buyer carries the risk if it stalls. For many, the better move is to weigh a launch against a nearby resale unit and ask what the difference of hundreds of dollars per square foot buys. When new stock can sell for up to nearly double the resale benchmark, comparison pays.

新盘价格远高于转售。郊区转售为每平方英尺$1,523，而OCR的畅销盘远高于此：丹戈花园居（Tengah Garden Residences）每平方英尺$2,113、实里达居（Springleaf Residence）每平方英尺$2,169、白屋居（Parktown Residence）每平方英尺$2,361、川安园（Chuan Park）每平方英尺$2,598。城市边缘更高，傲丽（The Orie）每平方英尺$2,723，滨海一号花园（One Marina Gardens）每平方英尺$2,957。在同样的地段，转售更便宜，你却要为一套新单位多付溢价。

这份溢价并不自动等于价值。一套新单位能买到全新的99年地契、现代化的格局和免装修，但在这样的价位上，未来的增值已被计入价格，一旦增长停滞，买家就要承担风险。对许多人来说，更好的做法是把新盘和附近的转售单位放在一起权衡，问问每平方英尺相差几百元究竟买到了什么。当新盘的售价最高可达转售基准的近两倍时，多做比较是值得的。

The headline HDB number is quiet. The resale median is $605 psf, up 0.3% over the year, and the flat medians are $446k for a 3-room, $630k for a 4-room, $740k for a 5-room and $910k for an executive. That average blends two different stories.

HDB的表面数字很平静。转售中位价为每平方英尺$605，同比上涨0.3%，各房型中位价为：3房$446k、4房$630k、5房$740k、公寓式组屋$910k。这个平均数混合了两个截然不同的故事。

Private yields are low. Private homes rent at a median $4,300 a month, a gross yield of 3.6%. At today's prices, that is thin once you count interest, maintenance and vacant months between tenants.

HDB yields more. A flat rents at a median $3,200 a month, and because the entry price is lower, the gross yield is 6.1%, close to double the private figure. For cash return, the numbers favour public housing over a condo.

私宅回报率偏低。私宅月租中位数为$4,300，毛回报率3.6%。在如今的价位下，一旦算上利息、维护以及换租客之间的空置期，这个回报就很单薄了。

HDB回报更高。组屋月租中位数为$3,200，由于入手价更低，毛回报率达6.1%，接近私宅的两倍。论现金回报，数字更青睐组屋而非公寓。

Supply & en bloc供应与集体出售

The pipeline behind 2027 and 2028 prices支撑2027与2028年价格的供应管道

The collective sale market is active. Chuan Park sold for $890m, Loyang Valley for $880m and Thiam Siew Avenue for $815m. Each site pays out its owners now and becomes new homes in a few years, so the spend is a bet on 2027 and 2028 buyers.

On the public side, a wave of HDB flats reaches their five-year MOP over the next year, freeing more owners to sell. More resale supply tends to cap price growth, so this is the reason to watch how fast values climb into 2027 and 2028, rather than the next few months.

集体出售（en bloc）市场很活跃。川安园（Chuan Park）以$890m售出，罗央谷（Loyang Valley）以$880m售出，添寿园（Thiam Siew Avenue）以$815m售出。每一幅地皮现在都让业主套现，几年后变成新住宅，所以这笔投入是对2027年和2028年买家的一场押注。

在公共住房这一端，未来一年一批HDB组屋陆续满5年MOP，让更多业主可以出售。转售供应增多往往会抑制价格增长，所以值得关注的是进入2027年和2028年时房价上涨的速度，而不是接下来几个月的变化。

Rates & policy利率与政策

Cooling measures intact, rates in charge降温措施不变，利率主导全局

The cooling measures are unchanged, and Budget 2026 left housing untouched. A citizen buying a second home pays 20% ABSD, a foreigner 60%. Sell within the first year and Seller's Stamp Duty takes 16%, under the four-year holding window set in July 2025.

The borrowing rules are unchanged too. TDSR caps total debt repayments at 55% of income, and a first bank loan is capped at 75% of price. With policy frozen, interest rates move the market. Cheaper money lets buyers stretch, dearer money pulls them back.

降温措施维持不变，Budget 2026也未触及房地产。公民购买第二套房支付20%的ABSD，外国人支付60%。第一年内卖出，Seller's Stamp Duty收取16%，属于2025年7月设定的四年持有期范围内。

借贷规则同样不变。TDSR将总债务偿还上限设为收入的55%，首套银行贷款上限为房价的75%。政策冻结之下，是利率在牵动市场。资金便宜，买家就能拉高预算；资金变贵，买家就往回缩。

The month ahead展望未来一月

Outlook展望

Base case for the month ahead is a continued slow rise. Private resale is at a median $1,753 psf, up 0.6% on the month and 3.8% on the year, and nothing in the data points to that changing. Suburbs lead, OCR at $1,523 psf, up 1.4% over three months, while prime CCR at $2,203 psf is down 2.4% and stays the weak spot.

Risks cut both ways. If rates fall, buyers stretch and the mass market runs hotter. If rates stay high, or the MOP supply wave lands faster than expected, price growth stalls and prime could fall further. HDB resale, up 0.3% on the year at a median $605 psf, looks steady either way.

未来一个月的基准情景是继续缓慢上涨。私宅转售中位价为每平方英尺$1,753，环比上涨0.6%，同比上涨3.8%，数据中没有任何迹象显示这会改变。郊区领跑，OCR为每平方英尺$1,523，三个月内上涨1.4%，而高档区CCR为每平方英尺$2,203，下跌2.4%，仍是弱点所在。

风险两头都有。若利率下降，买家拉高预算，大众市场会更火热。若利率维持高位，或MOP供应潮来得比预期更快，价格增长就会停滞，高档区可能进一步下跌。HDB转售同比上涨0.3%，中位价为每平方英尺$605，无论哪种情况都显得稳健。

Our call我们的判断

Our call: with policy frozen, rates drive the market. Favour the firm suburban and HDB markets over prime, and let borrowing costs, not headlines, set your timing.我们的判断：政策冻结之下，利率主导市场。看好稳健的郊区和HDB市场，胜过高档区，并让借贷成本而非新闻头条来决定你的时机。
