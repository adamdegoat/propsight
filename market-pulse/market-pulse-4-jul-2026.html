Loyang Valley, a large older estate in the east, was sold collectively for around $880 million earlier this year. The deal is done, but its effect is still spreading. When a site this size changes hands, developers are effectively putting a price on the land beneath it, and that number becomes a reference point for every older block in the surrounding area. Owners who have quietly wondered what their home would fetch in a collective sale now have a real, recent data point to work from.

If you are buying to rent out, the numbers from this week are worth sitting with. A resale flat in the heartland brings in around 6.1% a year in rent, at roughly $3,200 a month. A condo brings in around 3.6%, at roughly $4,300 a month. The condo earns more rent in cash terms, but you are paying significantly more to own it. For every dollar you put in, the flat works nearly twice as hard. That is not a reason to rule out a condo, but it is a reason to be clear-eyed about what the actual return looks like before you commit.

The flat earns nearly twice the yield. If rental income is the goal, that gap is hard to ignore.

For sellers, especially those in ageing developments that have been circling the idea of a collective sale, the Loyang Valley deal is a useful anchor. It confirms that appetite for large land parcels is still real, and that developers are prepared to pay seriously for the right site. Even if your block never goes en bloc, a nearby deal like this tends to lift what individual buyers are willing to offer, because it raises the floor on what the land underneath is worth. The simple takeaway: a completed en bloc near your home is one of the cleaner signals that land values in your area have reset upward.

Loyang Valley 是东部一个规模庞大的老旧屋苑，今年早些时候以约$880 million的价格完成集体出售。交易已经落实，但它的影响仍在持续扩散。当一块这么大的地皮易手，发展商实际上是在为地底的土地定价，而这个数字，就成了周边每一栋老旧楼宇的参考基准。一直在心里默默盘算自己的家在集体出售中能卖多少钱的屋主，如今终于有了一个真实、新鲜的参考数据可以依循。

如果你买房是为了出租，这个星期的数据值得好好思量一番。组屋区里的转售组屋每年可带来约6.1%的租金回报，每月租金大约$3,200。condo 的回报率则约为3.6%，每月租金大约$4,300。从现金收入来看，condo 的租金收入确实更多，但你买入时所付出的代价也高出许多。每投入一块钱，组屋所产生的回报几乎是condo 的两倍。这不是说condo 不值得考虑，但在拍板之前，你需要睁大眼睛看清楚实际回报到底有多少。

组屋带来的租金回报率近乎是condo 的两倍。如果目标是租金收入，这个差距实在难以忽视。

对于卖家来说，尤其是住在老旧楼盘、一直在考虑集体出售可能性的屋主，Loyang Valley 的这笔交易是一个很好的参照点。它证明了市场对大型地皮的需求依然真实存在，发展商也愿意为合适的地段认真出价。即使你所在的楼宇最终没有走en bloc 这条路，附近有这样的交易成功落实，往往也会带动个别买家愿意出更高的价钱，因为这拉高了地底土地价值的底线。简单来说：家附近有en bloc 成功落实，是你所在地区地价已经向上重新定位的其中一个最清晰的信号。
