Here is the number that stopped me mid-conversation with a client this week. A resale flat is earning around 6.1% a year in rent, at roughly $3,200 a month. A condo is pulling about 3.6%, at around $4,250 a month. The condo collects more rent in dollar terms, but you paid far more for it. Divide the rent by what you actually spent, and the flat wins clearly. For landlords right now, the heartland is quietly the stronger earner.

The flat works harder for every dollar you put in.

If you are buying to rent out, this gap matters more than it looks. Many buyers gravitate toward condos because the monthly rent number is higher and the project feels more aspirational. But yield is what services your mortgage and puts money in your pocket. A 6.1% return versus 3.6% is not a rounding difference. If steady rental income is your main goal, the case for a heartland resale flat is real this week, not just on paper.

For sellers, where you are makes a big difference. The most expensive central areas are cooling. But places like Toa Payoh are up 5.6% and Bukit Merah is up 4.2%, so if you are in those parts of town, conditions are still working in your favour. One quiet piece of news worth knowing if you own an older development: Loyang Valley recently sold en bloc for around $880 million. When a deal that size lands, it tends to reset what buyers and developers will pay for nearby land, and can quietly lift what your own block could fetch even if nothing about your unit has changed. If your development is getting on in years and this kind of news is landing close to home, it pays to get a proper read on your value before conditions shift. The simple takeaway this week: own in the heartland, and the market is doing more for you than the headlines suggest.

这一周我和客户聊天时，有个数字让我不得不停下来细想。组屋转售单位的年租金回报率大约是6.1%，每个月租金大概$3,200。condo的回报率约3.6%，每月租金约$4,250。论月租金额，condo是高一些，但你当初买的时候付出的钱也多得多。把租金除以实际投入的成本，组屋明显占优。对现在的房东来说，邻里组屋区悄悄地才是更能赚到钱的选择。

每一块钱投进去，组屋替你赚得更勤。

如果你买房是为了出租，这个差距比表面上看起来重要得多。很多买家会倾向选condo，因为月租的数字更高，而且这类项目感觉更有档次。但真正帮你还房贷、让口袋有进账的，是回报率。6.1%对比3.6%，可不是四舍五入可以忽略的小差别。如果稳定的租金收入是你的主要目标，那这一周来看，选心脏地带的转售组屋是真正说得通的，不只是纸上谈兵。

对于卖家来说，地点非常关键。最贵的中央区目前正在降温。但Toa Payoh一带涨了5.6%，Bukit Merah涨了4.2%，如果你的房子就在这些地区，目前的市场条件仍然对你有利。如果你持有的是较老的发展项目，有一则消息值得留意：Loyang Valley最近以约$880 million完成了en bloc。这种规模的交易一旦达成，往往会重新定标附近地段的价格，买家和发展商愿意出手的金额预期都会跟着调整，也可能悄悄抬升你那栋楼的潜在价值，就算你的单位本身什么都没变。如果你的发展项目已经有些年头，类似的消息又恰好就在附近，那在市场条件改变之前，认真了解一下自己的房产价值是值得的。这一周的简单结论是：在组屋心脏地带持有房产，市场为你做的事，比新闻标题说的还要多。
