What living here is like

Sengkang is a newer north-eastern town built around the LRT and Compass One, hugely popular with young families. Long leases and fresher stock are the appeal, with distance from town the trade.

Honest pros and cons

What works
  • Strong HDB yield. A 4-room rents at roughly $3,200 a month, about 5.9% gross, among the higher returns going.
Worth weighing
  • Out-of-town address. The north-east location means a longer commute to the central districts than estates closer to town.
  • Condo is a big step up. Median around $1.56m at modest yields near 3.3%, well above the flats.
  • Competitive resale. Plenty of flats change hands here, so you are one of many buyers.
  • Prices easing. The 4-room is down about 1.2% recently, more room to negotiate but a softer market.
