What living here is like

Serangoon enjoys an MRT interchange, the large NEX mall and a clutch of good schools, sitting closer to the centre than most north-eastern towns. That connectivity keeps demand and prices firm.

Honest pros and cons

What works
  • Strong HDB yield. A 4-room rents at roughly $3,400 a month, about 6.2% gross, among the higher returns going.
  • Genuinely mature. Established schools, hawker centres and a settled town centre.
Worth weighing
  • Lease decay. Many flats now have around 60 years left, which matters for a long hold and future financing.
  • Out-of-town address. The north-east location means a longer commute to the central districts than estates closer to town.
  • Competitive resale. Plenty of flats change hands here, so you are one of many buyers.
  • Prices easing. The 4-room is down about 2.8% recently, more room to negotiate but a softer market.

What living here is like

Serangoon 设有MRT转换站和大型购物中心NEX，附近也有几所口碑不错的学校，位置比大多数东北区市镇更靠近市中心。便捷的交通带动需求持续稳定，房价也因此保持坚挺。

Honest pros and cons

What works
  • HDB租金回报强劲。四房式单位月租约$3,400，毛回报率约6.2%，在市场上属于较高水平。
  • 名副其实的成熟市镇。学校、熟食中心和市镇中心配套完善，生活稳定。
Worth weighing
  • 租约递减。许多单位目前约剩 60 年租约，若打算长期持有或日后申请贷款，这一点值得留意。
  • 地处市郊。位于东北部，前往市中心一带的通勤时间，比靠近市区的市镇稍长。
  • 转售竞争激烈。这里组屋交易活跃，市场上买家众多，你只是其中之一。
  • 价格略有松动。四房式单位近期跌幅约2.8%，议价空间有所增加，不过整体市场也趋于平稳。
