What living here is like

Toa Payoh is a mature, very central estate with a lively town centre, an MRT interchange and good schools. Being this close to town keeps flats well-bid and renting strongly.

Honest pros and cons

What works
  • Steady rental demand. A 4-room rents at roughly $3,650 a month, about 4.4% gross.
  • Affordable entry. The 4-room at $1,022 per sqft sits below the national average, good value for the space.
  • Genuinely mature. Established schools, hawker centres and a settled town centre.
  • Prices firm. The 4-room is up about 2.0% in the recent window.
Worth weighing
  • Mostly an HDB town. Little private condo stock is tracked here, so options are largely flats.
  • Competitive resale. Plenty of flats change hands here, so you are one of many buyers.

What living here is like

Toa Payoh是一个位置非常中心的成熟市镇，市区生活气息热闹有活力，设有MRT转换站，周边好学校也不少。地段这么靠近市区，组屋叫价向来扎实，出租也相当旺。

Honest pros and cons

What works
  • 租赁需求稳定。四房式单位月租约$3,650，毛回报率约4.4%。
  • 入场价亲民。四房式单位尺价$1,022，低于全国平均水平，空间划算，物超所值。
  • 名副其实的成熟市镇。学校、熟食中心和市镇中心配套完善，生活稳定。
  • 价格坚挺。四房式单位近期涨幅约2.0%。
Worth weighing
  • 以 HDB 为主的市镇。区内私人 condo 供应量不多，置业选择以组屋为主。
  • 转售竞争激烈。这里组屋交易活跃，市场上买家众多，你只是其中之一。
