What living here is like

Bedok is one of the largest HDB towns, packed with hawker centres, markets and schools, and close to East Coast Park and Changi. The trade is size and density: it is busy, and the far-east address means a longer ride to the central districts.

Honest pros and cons

What works
  • Strong HDB yield. A 4-room rents at roughly $3,300 a month, about 6.7% gross, among the higher returns going.
  • Well connected. 5 train stations serving the town, so getting around is easy.
  • Genuinely mature. Established schools, hawker centres and a settled town centre.
  • Prices firm. The 4-room is up about 0.8% in the recent window.
Worth weighing
  • Lease decay. Many flats now have around 59 years left, which matters for a long hold and future financing.
  • Out-of-town address. The east location means a longer commute to the central districts than estates closer to town.
  • Condo is a big step up. Median around $2.15m at modest yields near 3.1%, well above the flats.
  • Competitive resale. Plenty of flats change hands here, so you are one of many buyers.

What living here is like

Bedok 是新加坡规模最大的 HDB 市镇之一，小贩中心、巴刹和学校样样齐全，紧邻 East Coast Park 和 Changi。美中不足是人口密集、生活节奏忙碌，加上地处偏东，前往市中心地区需要多花一些时间。

Honest pros and cons

What works
  • HDB 租金回报亮眼。四房式单位月租约 $3,300，毛回报率约 6.7%，在市面上属于较高水平。
  • 交通四通八达。市镇内设有 5 个地铁站，日常出行十分方便。
  • 名副其实的成熟市镇。学校、熟食中心和市镇中心配套完善，生活稳定。
  • 房价稳健。四房式单位近期涨幅约 0.8%。
Worth weighing
  • 剩余租约递减。不少组屋目前剩余约 59 年，长期持有或日后申请贷款时需纳入考量。
  • 位置偏远。地处东部，前往中央区的通勤时间比靠近市区的市镇要长。
  • Condo 入场价高出一截。中位数约 $2.15m，租金回报率约 3.1%，入手门槛远高于组屋。
  • 转售竞争激烈。这里组屋交易活跃，市场上买家众多，你只是其中之一。
