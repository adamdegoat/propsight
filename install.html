Keep all your property tools one tap away.

Add PropSight to your home screen, it opens like an app, works even when you're offline, and there's no app store or sign-up.

One tap to every tool Works offlineNo app store

iPhone & iPad

  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (bottom of the screen).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add, the icon appears on your home screen.

Android

  1. Open this page in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right).
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  4. Tap Add / Install, done.

Or just tap Install now above if it appears.

It's not a "real" app from the store, it's this website, saved to your home screen so it opens full-screen in one tap. Nothing to download, nothing to update.
