For everyone

The words you'll keep hearing.

Every term the tools and agents throw at you, translated once, with what it actually means for you.

OTP · Option to Purchase

The little contract that reserves the home for you for about two weeks while you sort your loan. You pay a small fee for it.Once you "exercise" it, the deal is firm.

TDSR · the loan cap

Banks won't let all your monthly repayments add up to more than 55% of your income.More income or less debt → bigger budget.

MSR · HDB & EC only

A tighter cap: your home loan alone can't exceed 30% of your income.Why HDB budgets feel smaller than condo ones.

LTV · how much they lend

The most a bank will lend, usually 75% of the price on your first loan. You find the other 25%.Drops to 55% if the loan runs past age 65 or 30 years, lower still on a second home loan.

Stress test

Banks check you could still pay if rates jumped to about 4%, even though you'll likely pay less.It's why the "safe" loan feels conservative, that's the point.

BSD · Buyer's Stamp Duty

A tax everyone pays on the price, roughly 1-6% depending on how much the home costs.Budget for it in cash, on top of the down payment.

ABSD · Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty

Extra duty if it's not your first home, or you're a PR/foreigner. Can be very large.The single biggest cost surprise when upgrading.

SSD · Seller's Stamp Duty

A penalty for selling a private home within a few years of buying it.Hold past the window and it's zero.

CPF + accrued interest

You can use CPF for a home, but on selling you must put back what you used plus the interest it would have earned.It quietly eats into your cash proceeds.

Valuation gap

A bank only lends, and CPF only applies, up to its own valuation of the home.Pay above valuation and the difference is cash on top.

Minimum cash

At least 5% of the price must be paid in actual cash, CPF can't cover this part.The first real cash hurdle to clear.

MOP · Minimum Occupation Period

Usually 5 years you must live in an HDB before you can sell it or buy private.Sets the earliest you can upgrade.

Lease decay

A 99-year home slowly loses value, and loan/CPF eligibility, as the lease counts down.Matters most on older flats and for resale.

HFE · HDB Flat Eligibility

An online letter from HDB that confirms what you can buy, borrow, and claim in grants before you start.Your first step for any HDB purchase.
