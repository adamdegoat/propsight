What PropSight is

PropSight is an information service. We take public property records and turn them into estimates, valuations, affordability sums, market reads and answers from Aillie, our property assistant. The goal is to help you understand the market and ask better questions.

By using the site, you agree to these terms. If you don't agree with them, please don't use it.

This is not advice

Everything on PropSight is for general information only. It is not financial, investment, legal, tax or property advice, and nothing here is a recommendation to buy, sell, rent or finance any particular property.

Our valuations, affordability figures, market commentary and Aillie's answers are estimates. They can be incomplete, out of date, or simply wrong. Real prices depend on things we can't see, a unit's condition, renovation, exact floor and facing, the mood on the day. Treat every number as a starting point, not a final answer.

Always do your own checks

Before you make any property or money decision, do your own due diligence and speak to qualified professionals:

  • A licensed property agent or valuer for pricing and the actual unit.
  • A bank or mortgage broker for what you can really borrow.
  • A lawyer for contracts, eligibility and the legal side.

You are responsible for any decision you make. PropSight is one input among many, never your only one.

We work hard to keep PropSight accurate and up to date, but we provide it "as is", with no guarantees that it will be correct, complete, available, or free of errors or interruptions. We may change, pause or remove any part of the site at any time.

Where our data comes from

Our figures are built from public records, including the Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA), the Housing & Development Board (HDB) and OneMap, used under their respective open-data terms. These sources are reliable but not perfect, and there can be gaps or delays between a transaction happening and it appearing in the records. We refresh our data regularly, but it won't always reflect the very latest sale.
