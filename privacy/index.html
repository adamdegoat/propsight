你的隐私

我们收集什么，以及绝不会做什么。

PropSight 是免费的，我们力求简单。我们只问最必要的信息，绝不出售你的资料，你提供的一切都可以随时查看、修改或删除。下面就是具体做法。

最后更新于 2026年6月28日 · 本政策遵循新加坡《个人数据保护法》(PDPA)。

What we collect

When you join PropSight, we collect just two things:

  • Your first name, so we can say hello and address our emails to you.
  • Your email address, to confirm your membership, sign you back in (we use no passwords), and send you what you asked for.

That's it. You can browse and value any home, ask Aillie for free, those tools don't ask for any personal details.

Why we collect it

We use your name and email only to run your free membership:

  • To create and manage your account, and to sign you in.
  • To send you our weekly newsletter and the occasional update.
  • If you choose to join our Telegram channel (@propsightsg), to share occasional property updates there. Joining Telegram is optional and separate.

We don't use your data for anything you didn't sign up for, and we won't start without asking you first.

Where it's stored

Your name and email are held securely in our membership database, run on Supabase, a trusted hosting provider. Access is limited to PropSight, and we keep only what's needed to run your membership, nothing more.

We never sell your data

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal details to anyone, no advertisers, no agents, no third parties. We don't pass your email to property agents or brokers. Full stop.

How long we keep it

We keep your details for as long as you're a member. If you unsubscribe or ask us to delete your account, we remove your personal data from our active records promptly, and keep nothing beyond what the law requires us to.

Your rights under the PDPA

Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act gives you clear rights over your data. With PropSight you can, at any time:

  • Access it, ask us what we hold about you.
  • Correct it, fix your name or email if it's wrong.
  • Withdraw consent, tell us to stop emailing or contacting you.
  • Delete it, ask us to remove your account and details entirely.

To do any of these, just email us (below) and we'll sort it out, usually within a few days.

Unsubscribing

Every newsletter we send has an unsubscribe link at the bottom, one click and you're off the list. You can also email us to be removed, or leave the Telegram channel at any time straight from Telegram. Unsubscribing doesn't delete your account; if you'd like that too, just say so.

Contact us

Questions, requests, or want your data changed or removed? Reach our data-protection contact:

PropSight, propsightsg@gmail.com

See also our Terms of Use. PropSight is an information service only, not financial or property advice.

我们收集什么

加入 PropSight 时，我们只收集两样东西：

  • 你的名字，方便我们打招呼，并在邮件中称呼你。
  • 你的电邮地址，用于确认你的会员资格、让你重新登录（我们不用密码），以及发送你所要求的内容。

就这些。你可以免费浏览和估算任何房子、向 Aillie 提问，这些工具不会要求任何个人资料。

我们为什么收集

我们仅用你的名字和电邮来运行你的免费会员：

  • 创建和管理你的账户，并让你登录。
  • 给你发送每周通讯和偶尔的更新。
  • 如果你选择加入我们的 Telegram 频道（@propsightsg），在那里分享偶尔的房产更新。加入 Telegram 是可选的，且是独立的。

我们不会把你的数据用于你没有同意的用途，未经你同意也不会擅自开始。

存储在哪里

你的名字和电邮安全地保存在我们的会员数据库中，运行于 Supabase（一家可信的托管服务商）。访问权限仅限 PropSight，我们只保留运行会员所需的信息，绝不多留。

我们绝不出售你的数据

我们不会向任何人出售、出租或交易你的个人资料，没有广告商、没有中介、没有第三方。我们不会把你的电邮交给房产中介或经纪。绝对不会。

我们保留多久

只要你是会员，我们就保留你的资料。如果你取消订阅或要求删除账户，我们会及时从活跃记录中移除你的个人数据，除法律要求外不保留任何内容。

你在 PDPA 下的权利

新加坡《个人数据保护法》赋予你对自己数据的明确权利。在 PropSight，你随时可以：

  • 查看：询问我们持有你的哪些信息。
  • 更正：修正错误的名字或电邮。
  • 撤回同意：让我们停止给你发邮件或联系你。
  • 删除：要求我们彻底移除你的账户和资料。

要办理以上任何一项，只需发邮件给我们（见下方），我们通常会在几天内处理好。

取消订阅

我们发送的每封通讯底部都有取消订阅链接，一键即可退出名单。你也可以发邮件要求移除，或随时直接在 Telegram 退出频道。取消订阅不会删除你的账户；如果你也想删除账户，告诉我们一声即可。
