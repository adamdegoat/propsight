你的隐私

我们收集什么，以及绝不会做什么。

PropSight 是免费的，我们力求简单。本站没有账户、无需注册，因此我们完全不收集你的个人数据，也不使用任何追踪器。下面就是具体做法。

最后更新于 2026年7月16日 · 本政策遵循新加坡《个人数据保护法》(PDPA)。

What we collect

Nothing about you, personally. PropSight has no accounts and no sign-up, so we never ask for your name, your email, or your phone number, and we have no way to identify you.

  • No account, no sign-up. Every tool on this site, valuations, affordability, stamp duty, grants, the guides and Aillie, works without you telling us who you are.
  • No tracking or advertising cookies. We run no analytics, no ad pixels and no third-party trackers of any kind.
  • Your browser remembers two small settings, your language choice and your Aillie voice preference. These stay on your device, and we never see them.

When you ask Aillie something

When you send Aillie a question, the text of that message is sent to our server so an answer can be generated and sent back to you. It is not linked to any account, because there are no accounts. Please don't type personal details into the chat, you don't need to, and we'd rather not receive them.

If you join our Telegram channel

Our Telegram channel (@propsightsg) is completely optional and separate from this site. If you join it, Telegram (not PropSight) handles your account and whatever your Telegram privacy settings expose. You can leave at any time from inside Telegram.

We never sell your data

We do not sell, rent or trade personal details to anyone, no advertisers, no agents, no third parties. We don't pass anything to property agents or brokers. Full stop. Since we don't collect personal data in the first place, there is nothing to sell.

Your rights under the PDPA

Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act gives you clear rights over your personal data. Because PropSight holds no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to show, correct or delete. If you believe we hold something about you, or you have any question about this policy, email us and we'll answer properly.

我们收集什么

关于你个人的信息，我们一概不收集。PropSight 没有账户、无需注册，因此我们不会索取你的姓名、电邮或电话号码，也无从识别你的身份。

  • 没有账户，无需注册。本站的每一个工具，包括估价、负担能力、印花税、津贴、指南以及 Aillie，都无需你告知身份即可使用。
  • 没有追踪或广告 Cookie。我们不使用任何分析工具、广告像素或第三方追踪器。
  • 你的浏览器会记住两项小设置：语言选择和 Aillie 语音偏好。它们只保存在你的设备上，我们看不到。

当你向 Aillie 提问时

当你向 Aillie 发送问题时，该消息的文字会发送到我们的服务器以生成回复并返回给你。它不会关联到任何账户，因为本站没有账户。请不要在对话中输入个人资料，你无需这样做，我们也不希望收到。

如果你加入我们的 Telegram 频道

我们的 Telegram 频道（@propsightsg）完全可选，且独立于本站。如果你加入，你的账户及隐私设置由 Telegram（而非 PropSight）处理。你随时可以在 Telegram 内退出频道。

我们绝不出售你的数据

我们不会向任何人出售、出租或交易个人资料，没有广告商、没有中介、没有第三方。我们不会把任何信息交给房产中介或经纪。绝对不会。由于我们本来就不收集个人数据，也就没有什么可出售的。

你在 PDPA 下的权利
