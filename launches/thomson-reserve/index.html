Nature

Where nature is your first neighbour.

The development sits within a rare green setting. Windsor Nature Park and MacRitchie Reservoir lie to the southwest, with the Singapore Island Country Club to the northwest, and long views towards the Lower and Upper Peirce Reservoirs beyond.

The six towers are carefully positioned to open onto these panoramas while avoiding direct overlooking between blocks, so homes enjoy both the outer green vistas and the extensive gardens held within.

  • MacRitchie & Peirce ReservoirsWater, forest and the island's central catchment on your doorstep
  • Windsor Nature ParkBoardwalks and rainforest trails minutes from home
  • Singapore Island Country ClubEstablished greenery and open space to the northwest

The most endearing thing about this sanctuary is what it chooses to protect. Your calm, your peace, and the quiet in between.

Connectivity

Moments from the city, without the rush.

A sheltered two-minute walk brings you to Upper Thomson MRT on the Thomson-East Coast Line, with a direct ride to Orchard Road and seamless transfers to the Circle, Downtown, North-South and North-East lines.

Arrival is unhurried. A grand entrance sequence runs off Bright Hill Drive, with a secondary entrance along Sin Ming Avenue and a pedestrian gateway that links straight into the sheltered walkway network. Here, centrality becomes freedom. Less time in transit, more time for living.

  • Upper Thomson MRT (TE8)Two-minute sheltered walk, Thomson-East Coast Line
  • Direct to Orchard RoadPlus links to the Circle, Downtown, North-South and North-East lines
