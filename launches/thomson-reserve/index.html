The Reserve

A sanctuary shaped by what Thomson has always done best. Lived-in warmth, deep greenery, and a calm that arrives quietly.

Embraced by nature, the residence feels like a reserve in the truest sense. A place protected from the rush, where shade, trees and soft paths set a slower, steadier pace for everyday life. Here, nature is not a distant view. It is your closest neighbour, and the best of life is kept intact.

Nature

Where nature is your first neighbour.

Designed around a single idea the developer calls Calmness Grounded in Nature, the development sits within a rare green setting. Windsor Nature Park and MacRitchie Reservoir lie to the southwest, with the Singapore Island Country Club to the northwest, and long views towards the Lower and Upper Pierce Reservoirs beyond.

To the south, the site faces an established low-rise landed area, keeping an unblocked visual corridor. Nature here guides a way of living, a refined sanctuary between the worlds that matter most.

  • MacRitchie & Pierce ReservoirsWater, forest and the island's central catchment nearby
  • Windsor Nature ParkBoardwalks and rainforest trails to the southwest
  • Singapore Island Country ClubEstablished greenery and open space to the northwest

The most endearing thing about this sanctuary is what it chooses to protect. Your calm, your peace, and the quiet in between.

Connectivity

Moments from the city, without the rush.

A sheltered two-minute walk brings you to Upper Thomson MRT on the Thomson-East Coast Line, with a direct ride to Orchard Road and connections to the Circle, Downtown, North-South and North-East lines.

Arrival is unhurried. A grand entrance sequence runs off Bright Hill Drive, with a secondary entrance along Sin Ming Avenue and a pedestrian gateway that links straight into the sheltered walkway network. Residents also benefit from an extensive network of bicycle-friendly routes and sheltered pedestrian linkways. Here, centrality becomes freedom, less time in transit, more time for living.

  • Upper Thomson MRT (TE8)Two-minute sheltered walk, Thomson-East Coast Line
  • Direct to Orchard RoadPlus links to the Circle, Downtown, North-South and North-East lines

The Residences

Calmness is the new luxury.
